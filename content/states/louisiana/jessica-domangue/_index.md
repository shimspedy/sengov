Jessica Domangue is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives from the 53rd district. She is a member of the Republican Party and has been in office since January 8, 2024.

Before her current role, Jessica Domangue was the Chairwoman of the Terrebone Parish Council. She first ran to represent District 53 in 2023 and advanced to the runoff after the October 14, 2023 Jungle primary. In the November 18, 2023 runoff election, she won with a significant majority against Dirk Guidry.

Jessica Domangue lives in Louisiana. She earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Nicholls State University and a master's degree in social work from Louisiana State University. Her career experience includes working as a licensed clinical social worker with Start Community Health Center. She is also a member of the National Rifle Association (NRA).

In terms of her political stance, Domangue has announced her support of term limits. This shows her commitment to ensuring fresh ideas and perspectives in the political arena.

Her tenure as a representative is marked by her dedication to her constituents and her commitment to upholding the values of her party. As she continues her political journey, she strives to make a positive impact in her district and the state of Louisiana.
