Troy D. Romero is an American politician and businessman who is currently serving as a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives from the 37th district. He was elected in November 2019 and assumed office on January 13, 2020.

Early Life and Education:
Romero is a native of Iowa, Louisiana. He completed his high school education at Welsh High School and later attended Louisiana State University.

Career:
Before entering politics, Romero was a businessman. He is the owner of Sports Turf Specialists, a company that installs turf on athletic fields. His political career began when he was elected to the Louisiana House of Representatives in November 2019. He assumed office on January 13, 2020.

Personal Details:
Romero is a member of the Republican party. He is a father to four children.
