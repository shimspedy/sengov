Adrian Fisher is a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives, representing District 16. He is a member of the Democratic Party. He assumed office on December 6, 2021, and his current term ends on January 10, 2028.

Adrian Fisher was re-elected to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 16 outright in the primary on October 14, 2023, after the primary and general election were canceled.

Education:
Adrian Fisher earned a Bachelor of Science degree in accounting from Southern University and a Master of Science in community counseling from the University of Louisiana at Monroe.

Career:
Outside of politics, Adrian Fisher is a licensed counselor and operates a behavioral health business. He was elected to the Louisiana House of Representatives in November 2021 and assumed office on December 6, 2021.

In addition to his political career, Fisher's experience includes working as a pastor and owning a business. His tenure in the Louisiana House of Representatives began in 2021 and is set to end in 2028.

Committee Assignments:
In the year 2023-2024, Fisher was assigned to the following committees:
- Agriculture, Forestry, Aquaculture, and Rural Development Committee
- Commerce Committee
- Natural Resources and Environment Committee

Elections:
In the elections of 2023, the primary election was canceled. Adrian Fisher (D) won the election without appearing on the ballot. In the special primary for Louisiana House of Representatives District 16, Adrian Fisher won the election outright against Alicia Calvin and Charles Bradford on November 13, 2021.

Personal Life:
Adrian Fisher is married to LaTondra. He is a business owner and a pastor.
