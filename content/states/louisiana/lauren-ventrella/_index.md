Lauren Ventrella is a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives, representing District 65. She is a member of the Republican Party. She assumed office on January 8, 2024, and her current term ends on January 10, 2028.

Ventrella ran for election to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 65. She won in the general election on November 18, 2023.

Lauren Ventrella earned a bachelor's degree from Louisiana State University and a J.D. and M.P.A. from Southern University. Her career experience includes working as an attorney at her own law firm.

In the general election for Louisiana House of Representatives District 65, Lauren Ventrella defeated Brandon Ivey. The election took place on November 18, 2023. Lauren Ventrella received 56.7% of the votes, which equated to 4,202 votes. Brandon Ivey received 43.3% of the votes, which equated to 3,207 votes.

In the nonpartisan primary for Louisiana House of Representatives District 65, Brandon Ivey and Lauren Ventrella defeated Aaron Moak, Stephen Whitlow, and Jamie Pope. The primary took place on October 14, 2023. Brandon Ivey received 32.1% of the votes, which equated to 3,449 votes. Lauren Ventrella received 27.7% of the votes, which equated to 2,976 votes.

Lauren Ventrella represents parts of East Baton Rouge Parish and has been in office since January 8, 2024. She is an American politician serving as a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives from the 65th district.
