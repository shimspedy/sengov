Ed Larvadain III

Ed Larvadain III is an American attorney and politician serving as a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives from the 26th district. He assumed office in 2019.

Personal Background:
Ed Larvadain III's father, Ed Larvadain Jr., was a prominent civil rights attorney in Alexandria, Louisiana. Ed Larvadain III, as of March 2020, lived in Alexandria, Louisiana.

Education:
He earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in political science from Southern University and a Juris Doctor from the Southern University Law Center.

Career:
Since graduating from law school in 1992, Larvadain has worked as an attorney. He was elected to the Louisiana House of Representatives in 2018 and assumed office in 2019. He is a member of the Civil Law and Procedure Committee.

Political Party:
He is a member of the Democratic Party.

Tenure:
His current term ends on January 10, 2028. He won re-election to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 26 outright in the primary on October 14, 2023, after the general election was canceled.

Committee Assignments:
Larvadain was assigned to the following committees:
- Health and Welfare Committee
- Labor and Industrial Relations Committee
- Transportation, Highways and Public Works Committee
