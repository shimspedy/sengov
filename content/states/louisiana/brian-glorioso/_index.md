Brian Glorioso is a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives, representing District 90. He is a member of the Republican Party. He assumed office on January 8, 2024, and his current term ends on January 10, 2028.

Glorioso ran for election to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 90 and won in the general election on November 18, 2023. He defeated incumbent Mary DuBuisson in the general election for Louisiana House of Representatives District 90.

Brian Glorioso earned a bachelor's degree in social science from the University of New Orleans in 1997 and a J.D. from Loyola University New Orleans College of Law in 2000. His career experience includes working as an attorney, including as managing partner with Tonry, Brinson & Glorioso.

In the nonpartisan primary for Louisiana House of Representatives District 90, incumbent Mary DuBuisson and Brian Glorioso defeated Heide Alejandro-Smith on October 14, 2023. In the general election, Brian Glorioso defeated incumbent Mary DuBuisson on November 18, 2023.

Glorioso represents parts of St. Tammany Parish and has been in office since January 8, 2024. He is an American politician serving as a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives from the 90th district.
