Michael Gabriel "Gabe" Firment is an American politician and businessman who is currently serving as a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives from the 22nd district. He assumed office on January 13, 2020.

Born as Michael Gabriel Firment, he is known by his nickname "Gabe". He is a member of the Republican party. He resides in Pollock, Louisiana, U.S. with his spouse Erica Miller and their two children.

Education:
After graduating from Tioga High School in 1989, Firment pursued higher education and earned a Bachelor of Science degree in public administration from Louisiana College in 1994. He further expanded his academic credentials by obtaining a Master of Business Administration from Louisiana Tech University.

Career:
Outside of politics, Firment has worked in the construction and insurance industries. His career in politics began when he was elected to the Louisiana House of Representatives in November 2019. He assumed office on January 13, 2020.

In addition to his political and business pursuits, Gabe Firment is very active in serving his community and church in a variety of roles. He and his wife, Erica Miller Firment, have 3 children and are the proud grandparents of 2 young grandchildren.
