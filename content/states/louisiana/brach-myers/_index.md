Brach Myers is a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives, representing District 45. He assumed office on January 8, 2024, and his current term ends on January 10, 2028. Myers is affiliated with the Republican Party.

Myers won the election to represent District 45 outright in the primary on October 14, 2023, after the general election was canceled. He won the election with 70% of the vote against Paul "Scott" LeBleu's 25%.

Before his political career, Myers had a professional experience working as the Senior Vice President of Corporate Development for LHC Group. His roots run deep in Acadiana. He was born and raised in Palmetto and spent a few formative years in New Iberia before settling down in Lafayette with his wife, Carly.
