Peter Egan is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 74 in the Louisiana House of Representatives. He assumed office on January 8, 2024, and his current term ends on January 10, 2028. He ran for election to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 74 and won in the general election on November 18, 2023.

His career experience includes working as a small business owner. He has been affiliated with the Republican Parish Executive Committee and the Homecare Association of Louisiana. He is also a member of the James Samaritan.

In the general election for Louisiana House of Representatives District 74, Peter Egan defeated Buffie Singletary on November 18, 2023. He received 54.0% of the votes, which amounted to 4,480 votes. In the primary for the same district, Peter Egan received 46.8% of the votes, which amounted to 5,872 votes.

Peter Egan is a highly accomplished individual with a successful career in the healthcare industry and active involvement in his community. Having lived in the USA since 1971, Peter has lovingly embraced America as his own country. He is married to Dr. Pamela Egan and they have been together for 43 years. He is the proud father of four and grandfather of six.

As a State Representative, Peter Egan serves on the Civil Law and Procedure, Health and Welfare, Retirement House Select Committee on Homeland Security. He is also a part of the Louisiana Republican Legislative Delegation and the Northshore Delegation. His district office is located at 78461 Hwy 437, Covington, LA 70435.
