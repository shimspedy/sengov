Biography

Jay Gallé was born in New Orleans, Louisiana. His professional experience includes working as an actor, writer, producer, and military technical advisor. He served in the U.S. Marine Corps. Gallé earned a degree from Loyola University.

Political Career

Jay Gallé (Republican Party) is a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives, representing District 104. He assumed office on January 8, 2024. His current term ends on January 10, 2028. Gallé (Republican Party) won election to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 104 outright in the primary on October 14, 2023, after the general election was canceled.

Elections 2023

In the 2023 elections, all candidates competed in the same primary, and a candidate could win the election outright by receiving more than 50 percent of the vote. If no candidate did, the top two vote recipients from the primary advanced to the general election, regardless of their partisan affiliation. Jay Gallé won election outright against John Raymond in the primary for Louisiana House of Representatives District 104 on October 14, 2023.
