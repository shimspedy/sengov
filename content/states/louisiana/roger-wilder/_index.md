Roger Wilder III is a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives, representing District 71. He is a member of the Republican Party. He assumed office on January 8, 2024, and his current term ends on January 10, 2028.

Wilder won the election to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 71 outright in the primary on October 14, 2023, after the general election was canceled. This victory marked the beginning of his political career in the Louisiana House of Representatives.

Before his political career, Roger Wilder's professional experience includes working as a small business owner. This experience has provided him with a unique perspective on the needs and challenges of small businesses in Louisiana.

In the primary for Louisiana House of Representatives District 71, Roger Wilder III won the election outright against Jim Norred and Walley Avara on October 14, 2023. He received 56.9% of the votes, amounting to 5,252 votes in total.

As a representative, Wilder has been an active participant in the legislative process, contributing to the development and passage of laws that benefit the residents of District 71 and the state of Louisiana.

In addition to his legislative duties, Wilder maintains a strong connection with his constituents. He is known for his accessibility and responsiveness to the concerns and needs of the people he represents.
