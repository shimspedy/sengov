Michael Echols is an American politician from the state of Louisiana. He is a member of the Republican party and represents the 14th district in the Louisiana House of Representatives.

Echols hails from Bastrop, Louisiana, and currently resides in Monroe. He holds a Bachelor of Arts and a Master of Business Administration from the University of Louisiana at Monroe.

Before his tenure in the Louisiana House of Representatives, Echols served on the Monroe City Council. His political career took a significant leap forward when he was elected to the Louisiana House of Representatives in the 2019 elections.

In addition to his political career, Echols has also made a mark in the business world. He served as the director of business development for Vantage Health Plan Incorporated. His professional experience in the business sector has undoubtedly contributed to his approach to policy and governance.

Throughout his career, Echols has demonstrated a commitment to serving his community and working towards the betterment of Louisiana. His work in both the public and private sectors showcases his leadership skills and dedication to public service.
