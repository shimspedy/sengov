Kim Carver is a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives, representing District 89. He assumed office on January 8, 2024, and his current term ends on January 10, 2028. He ran for election to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 89 and won in the general election on November 18, 2023.

Kim Carver lives in Mandeville, Louisiana. He earned a B.S. in engineering from Louisiana State University in 1998 and an M.B.A. from the University of New Orleans in 2004. His career experience includes working as a sales engineer with Siemens and as senior vice president of human resources at Gulf Coast Bank and Trust Company.

In the general election for Louisiana House of Representatives District 89, Kim Carver defeated Joshua Allison. Kim Carver received 53.9% of the votes, totaling 4,921 votes. Joshua Allison received 46.1% of the votes, totaling 4,213 votes.

In the primary for Louisiana House of Representatives District 89, Kim Carver and Joshua Allison defeated Scott Nowicki and Hugh Cassidy. Kim Carver received 43.7% of the votes, totaling 5,547 votes. Joshua Allison received 26.9% of the votes, totaling 3,417 votes.

Kim Carver is a leader with over 20 years of experience working with local businesses and donating his time to community organizations across Louisiana. A conservative Republican, Kim was elected as a Louisiana delegate to the Republican National Convention and honored by Congressman Steve Scalise with the Angel in Adoption Award.
