Phillip Eric Tarver is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives from the 36th district. He is a member of the Republican Party and has been in office since January 13, 2020.

Tarver's journey into politics was preceded by a successful career in business. He is the owner of Lake Charles Toyota, a well-known automobile dealership in Louisiana. His leadership skills were further honed during his tenure as a member of the Calcasieu Parish School Board, where he contributed to shaping the educational policies of the region.

In 2019, Tarver decided to take his public service to the next level by running for the Louisiana House of Representatives for District 36. His campaign was successful, and he assumed office on January 13, 2020. His dedication to his constituents and effective leadership led to him being re-elected after running unopposed on October 14, 2023.

Throughout his political career, Tarver has been known for his commitment to the Republican Party's principles and his dedication to serving the people of Louisiana's 36th district. His work in the Louisiana House of Representatives continues to have a significant impact on the state's legislative landscape.
