Jacob Jules Gabriel Landry is an American politician serving as a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives from the 49th district. A member of the Republican Party, Landry has been in office since January 8, 2024.

Jacob Landry is a resident of Erath, where he was born and raised, graduating from Erath High School in 1997. Jacob is a committed family man that married his high school sweetheart, Hannah, from Delcambre. Their two children, Jack and Jules, attend Erath public schools, Our Lady Of Lourdes Catholic Church and play an active role in the community.

Landry first ran to represent District 49 in 2023 and won the October 14, 2023 election with 64% against Sanders Derise's 20% and David Eaton's 16%. His professional experience includes working as a small business owner.

As a member of the Louisiana House of Representatives, he assumed office on January 8, 2024. His current term ends on January 10, 2028. He won the election to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 49 outright in the primary on October 14, 2023, after the general election was canceled.

Jacob Landry's professional experience includes working as a small business owner. His base salary as a representative is $16,800/year with a per diem of $175/day.
