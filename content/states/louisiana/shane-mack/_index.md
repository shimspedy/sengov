Shane Mack is an American politician and a member of the Republican Party. He represents the 95th district in the Louisiana House of Representatives. His district includes parts of Livingston Parish and Tangipahoa Parish.

Mack assumed office on January 8, 2024. His current term is set to end on January 10, 2028. Prior to his political career, Mack worked as a railroad technician, providing him with valuable professional experience.

He won the election to the Louisiana House of Representatives to represent District 95 outright in the primary on October 14, 2023, after the general election was canceled. This victory was a significant achievement in his political career.

In terms of education, Mack earned a bachelor's degree in industrial technology from Southeastern Louisiana University. This educational background has undoubtedly contributed to his understanding of the technological needs and advancements of his district and the state.

As a representative, Mack is committed to serving his constituents and making a positive impact in his community. His work in the Louisiana House of Representatives is a testament to his dedication to public service.
