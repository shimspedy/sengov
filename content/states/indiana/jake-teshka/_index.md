State Rep. Jake Teshka was elected to serve House District 7 in 2020. Born and raised in St. Joseph County, he graduated from St. Joseph High School in South Bend.

After college, Teshka worked in both the private and public sectors. In addition to his career as a business development professional, he continued focusing on making a positive impact in his community. Most recently, he served as Common Council Representative for the city of South Bend's 5th District until elected to the Indiana House of Representatives. While on the city council, Teshka sat on the South Bend Transpo Board of Directors and the South Bend Redevelopment Commission.

Teshka lives in North Liberty with his wife and two children. He and his family attend The Vineyard Church in Mishawaka, and Teshka helps support his community as a reading coach volunteer with the United Way.
