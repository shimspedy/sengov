Indiana State Sen. Ryan Mishler has been serving the constituents of Senate District 9 at the Statehouse since 2004. His district includes portions of Elkhart, Kosciusko, Marshall, and St. Joseph counties.

Mishler holds the position of chair on the Senate Committee on Appropriations and is also a member of the Senate Committee on Tax and Fiscal Policy. Additionally, he serves on the State Budget Committee, playing a crucial role in financial and budgetary matters.

Beyond his legislative responsibilities, Mishler is an active member of various chambers of commerce, including the Kosciusko County Chamber of Commerce and South Bend Regional Chamber of Commerce. He also contributes his expertise as a member of the IU South Bend Advisory Board. His community involvement extends to the United Methodist Church, and he actively participates in the Indiana Funeral Directors Association.

Professionally, Mishler is the president of both Mishler Funeral Homes and Mishler Memorials. He earned a Bachelor of Science in business administration from the University of Southern California.

Mishler and his son, Grant, reside in Mishawaka.
