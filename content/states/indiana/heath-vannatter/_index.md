Heath VanNatter, an American politician and a member of the Republican Party, serves in the Indiana House of Representatives, representing District 38. He assumed office in 2010, and his current term is set to conclude on November 6, 2024.

State Rep. Heath VanNatter was born and raised in Howard County where he currently resides with his wife Felicia and their three children, Madison, Bella and Cole. VanNatter is a graduate of Northwestern High School and has been a small business owner for more than 20 years.

He was the past president of the Howard County Home Builders Association, of which he is a current board member. VanNatter currently serves on the Executive Committee for the Indiana Builders Association, The Board of Directors for National Association of Home Builders. He is also a past member of the Howard County Plan Commission. VanNatter and his family are members of the Faith Church of Christ in Burlington.

As a small business owner and local employer, VanNatter understands the struggles facing the economy and the fiscal responsibility that Indiana must continue to maintain. As a homebuilder, he realizes the need for safe and affordable housing because it helps boost economic growth. VanNatter is devoted to playing an active role in economic development, working with all interested parties to bring new jobs and retaining the ones that exist in District 38.
