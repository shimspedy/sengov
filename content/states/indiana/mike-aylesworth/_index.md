Representative Michael Aylesworth served in the United States Army and made the rank of Sergeant before he was honorably discharged in 1973. He was the co-founder of Iroquois Bio Energy Company, which he later retired from, and now, he and his wife Dolores live in the Hebron area. They have three sons, six granddaughters and two grandsons.

Aylesworth was president of the Indiana Corn Growers Association State from 1997-2002 and president of the Indiana Corn Marketing Council State from 2000-2003.
