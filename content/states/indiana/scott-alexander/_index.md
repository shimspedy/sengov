State Sen. Scott Alexander, elected in 2022, proudly represents Senate District 26, serving the residents of Delaware and Randolph counties.

Within the Senate, Alexander contributes his expertise as a member of the Senate Committees on Agriculture, Judiciary, Local Government, and Natural Resources.

A graduate of Delta High School, Alexander pursued further education at Vincennes University and Ball State University. Professionally, he is the owner and operator of Alexander & Company Real Estate Appraisers Inc. Prior to his role as a state senator, Alexander served as the President of the Delaware County Council.

In his personal life, Alexander, along with his wife, Valerie, has chosen to make Delaware County their home, where they actively raise their two children.
