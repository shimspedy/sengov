Craig Snow is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the 22nd district of the Indiana House of Representatives. He assumed office on November 23, 2022. Prior to this, he was a member of the Indiana House of Representatives from the 18th district from November 4, 2020, to November 22, 2022.

Snow attended Warsaw Community High School and later graduated from Grace College & Seminary, where he earned a Bachelor of Science in business administration. In addition to his political career, Snow has also worked as the CEO of Cedar Holdings and the board chair of Silveus Insurance Group.
