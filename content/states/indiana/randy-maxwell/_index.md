State Sen. Randy Maxwell was elected in 2023 to represent Hoosiers in Senate District 43, which includes Dearborn, Jefferson, Ohio, Scott, and Switzerland counties, as well as a portion of Jennings County.

Maxwell currently serves as a member of the Senate committees on Agriculture, Natural Resources, and Pensions and Labor.

A lifelong Hoosier, Maxwell grew up in Logan and graduated from Indiana University, where he played football from 1995-1999 and received degrees in finance-real estate and entrepreneurship.

He is the CEO of Maxwell Construction and has been active in many community organizations, including the Dearborn County Home Builders Association (former president and current member), Ripley County Chamber of Commerce, Batesville Chamber of Commerce, Indiana Unemployment Insurance Board (appointed by Gov. Mitch Daniels in 2007), Southeast Indiana YMCA Board of Directors, 1Dearborn Economic Development Organization (vice president and founding member), and All Saints Catholic Parish in Dearborn County (former finance council president). He has also coached various local youth organizations for 20 years, and his family is a proud supporter of YoungLife in Dearborn County.

Maxwell and his wife, Robin, live in Guilford, where they raised three children - Alex, Kyla, and Dylan.
