State Rep. Chuck Moseley is a member of the Employment, Labor and Pensions, Financial Institutions, and Veterans Affairs and Public Safety committees of the Indiana House.

Chuck is a resident of Portage and graduated from River Forest High School in Lake Station.

Chuck is a veteran of the Army National Guard, past president of the Portage Chamber of Commerce, and past president of the Portage Township School Board. He is also a past member of the Portage Planning Commission, the Portage Park Board, and the Portage Port Authority.

Rep. Moseley and his wife, Monica, are the parents of two children. They are also the proud grandparents of five.

Chuck was first elected to the Indiana House of Representatives, serving Indiana House District 10, in November 2008.
