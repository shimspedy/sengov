State Rep. Kyle Pierce was elected to the Indiana General Assembly in November 2022. In addition to serving his community as state representative, Pierce works as a consultant for nonprofits and small companies. He received a bachelor's degree from Ball State University and a J.D. from the Indiana University Robert McKinney School of Law.

The grandson of two lifelong General Motors employees, Pierce understands the need for good jobs for Hoosiers and a quality education for their children. As a legislator, he prioritizes economic growth, expanding school options for Indiana's children and keeping communities safe.

Pierce lives in Anderson with his wife, Chloe, and their daughter.
