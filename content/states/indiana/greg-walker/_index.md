State Sen. Greg Walker has served Hoosiers in south-central Indiana since his election in 2006. He represents Senate District 41, which includes Bartholomew County and portions of Johnson County.

Walker serves as chair of the Senate Committee on Family and Children Services. He also serves as the ranking member of the Senate Committees on Elections and Ethics and serves as a member of the Senate Committees on Pensions and Labor, Public Policy, and Tax and Fiscal Policy.

Walker holds a Bachelor of Arts in business finance from Indiana University and a Master of Business Administration from Indiana Wesleyan University. He is also an elder at the Church of Christ in Columbus and a board member of Christian Communicators of America.

He is a widower and is the father of four children, Michaela, Andrew, Hannah, and Rebecca, and lives in Columbus.
