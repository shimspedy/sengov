Mary Madison is an American politician serving as a member of the Iowa House of Representatives for the 31st district. She was elected in November 2022 and assumed office on January 1, 2023.

Early Life and Education:
Mary Madison was born and raised in West Des Moines, Iowa. She is an ordained minister and has had a career as a school counselor and educator. She and her husband Dr. L. Keith Madison have called West Des Moines home for over twenty years. They are the proud parents of three adult children and one delightful granddaughter.

Career:
Before entering politics, Madison's career experience includes working as a minister with an AME church. She has also worked as a school counselor and educator. Her dedication to her community is evident in her extensive volunteer work.

Political Career:
Mary Madison ran for election to the Iowa House of Representatives to represent District 31. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022. Her tenure began on January 1, 2023, and she is currently serving her term which ends in 2025. She represents District 31 and is a member of the Democratic Party. Her predecessor was Rick Olson.

Achievements:
Mary Madison's election to the Iowa House of Representatives in 2022 was a significant achievement. As a representative, her priorities include strengthening working families, ensuring strong schools, addressing mental health issues, providing affordable healthcare, addressing environmental concerns, supporting labor, and advocating for reproductive justice.
