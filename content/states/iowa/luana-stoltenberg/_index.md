Luana Stoltenberg is a member of the Republican Party and a representative of District 81 in the Iowa House of Representatives. She assumed office on January 1, 2023, and her current term is set to end on January 1, 2025.

Stoltenberg ran for election to the Iowa House of Representatives to represent District 81 and won in the general election held on November 8, 2022. Her victory was a narrow one, with Stoltenberg securing 50.1% of the votes, amounting to a total of 5,073 votes.

Before her political career, Stoltenberg was an author, speaker, and anti-abortion activist. She is known for her staunch anti-mask and anti-vaccine stance. Protecting the 2nd amendment and lowering taxes were among the key issues she focused on during her campaign. She received endorsements from the social conservative group, the Family Leader, as well as a public show of support from the governor.

In her role as a state representative, Stoltenberg has been assigned to several committees for the 2023-2024 period. These include the House Local Government Committee, Natural Resources Committee, and House Veterans Affairs Committee. She is also a member of the Economic Growth and Technology committee.

Stoltenberg's base salary as a state representative is $25,000 per year. Legislators who live outside of Polk County receive a per diem of $175 per day, while those who live within Polk County receive $131.25 per day.

Stoltenberg continues to be an author and public speaker alongside her duties as a state representative. She can be contacted via her legislative email: luana.stoltenberg@legis.iowa.gov, and her home email: lu4life@msn.com.
