Brett Barker is a member of the Republican Party and ran for election to the Iowa House of Representatives to represent District 51. His career experience includes working as a pharmacist, which has provided him with a strong background in healthcare and public health. His experience in local government has given him valuable insights into the needs and concerns of his constituents.
