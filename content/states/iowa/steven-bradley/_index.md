Steven P. Bradley is an American politician and dentist who is currently serving as a member of the Iowa House of Representatives from the 66th district. He was elected in November 2020 and assumed office on January 11, 2021.

Bradley was born in Napa, California while his father was serving in the United States Air Force. He later lived in Epworth, Iowa. After graduating from Western Dubuque High School, Bradley attended Loras College and the University of Dubuque before earning a bachelor's degree from the University of Iowa. He earned a dental degree from the Marquette University School of Dentistry.

Outside of politics, Bradley works as a dentist. He was elected to the Iowa House of Representatives in November 2020 and assumed office on January 11, 2021. Bradley also serves as vice chair of the House Human Resources Committee.

Bradley (Republican Party) ran for re-election to the Iowa House of Representatives to represent District 66. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. His current term ends on January 1, 2025.

Bradley's career experience includes working as a dentist and a flight instructor. He served as the president of the Cascade Chamber of Commerce and Iowa Dental Association, as the chair of the Iowa Dental Board, and on the Finley Hospital Foundation Board.
