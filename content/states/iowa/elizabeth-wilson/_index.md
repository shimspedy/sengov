Elizabeth Wilson is a member of the Democratic Party and represents District 73 in the Iowa House of Representatives. She assumed office on January 1, 2023, and her current term ends on January 1, 2025.

Elizabeth Wilson lives in Marion, Iowa. She earned a B.A. in math and computer science from Cornell College in 1986 and an M.B.A. from the University of Iowa, Tippie College of Business, in 1988. Her career experience includes working as the president of Wilson Business Services, a marketing consultant at Sharegate, Inc., marketing communications manager and program manager for Rockwell Collins, and a vice president of sales and marketing for OminiLingua. Wilson previously served on the Linn-Mar school board.

Wilson ran for election to the Iowa House of Representatives to represent District 73 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. In the general election for Iowa House of Representatives District 73, Elizabeth Wilson defeated Susie Weinacht. Elizabeth Wilson received 51.0% of the votes, which equated to 7,297 votes. Susie Weinacht received 48.9% of the votes, which equated to 6,997 votes.

In the Iowa Legislature, Wilson was assigned to the following committees: Economic Growth Committee, House Government Oversight Committee, House Local Government Committee, House Transportation Committee, and House Ways and Means Committee. She also served on the Appropriations Subcommittee, Health and Human Services Appropriations Subcommittee, and the Enhance Iowa Board.
