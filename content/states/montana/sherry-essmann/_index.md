Sherry Essmann is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 52 in the Montana House of Representatives. She assumed office on June 11, 2021, and her current term is set to end on January 6, 2025. Essmann ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 52 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Born and residing in Billings, Montana, Essmann is an alumnus of Billings West High School. She furthered her education at Rocky Mountain College, where she earned a bachelor's degree in applied business management. Professionally, Essmann has experience in the financial services industry.

In her role as a legislator, Essmann has been assigned to several committees, including the House Education Committee, House Local Government Committee, and House Taxation Committee. Her dedication to her role and her constituents is evident in her work and contributions to the Montana House of Representatives.
