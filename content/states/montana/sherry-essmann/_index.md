Sherry Essmann is a dedicated public servant and a prominent member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 49. Born and raised in Billings, Montana, Sherry has deep roots in her community and a strong commitment to serving its residents. She graduated from Billings West High School and went on to earn a bachelor's degree in applied business management from Rocky Mountain College. Her educational background laid the foundation for a successful career in the financial services industry, where she gained valuable experience and insights.

Sherry's journey in public service began with her passion for making a difference in the lives of her fellow Montanans. She first served in the Montana House of Representatives for District 52, where she worked tirelessly to address the needs and concerns of her constituents. Following the redistricting process, she successfully ran for election to represent District 49, assuming office on January 6, 2025. Her dedication and hard work have earned her the trust and respect of her community.

Throughout her legislative career, Sherry has been a strong advocate for conservative values and policies that promote economic growth, public safety, and educational opportunities. She has served on several key committees, including the House Education Committee, the House Local Government Committee, and the House Taxation Committee. Her efforts have focused on property tax reform, support for public safety workers, and the expansion of career and job training programs to benefit students and the existing labor force.

In addition to her legislative work, Sherry is deeply committed to protecting the rights and freedoms of all Montanans. She has championed initiatives that support school choice, defend religious and democratic freedoms, and provide safe havens for newborn infants. Her dedication to these causes reflects her unwavering commitment to the well-being of her constituents.

Outside of her professional life, Sherry enjoys spending time with her family, creating and sewing projects for loved ones, and relaxing in the beautiful Montana mountains. Her strong sense of community and family values continues to inspire her work as a legislator and a leader in her community. Sherry Essmann's dedication, experience, and passion for public service make her a valuable asset to the Montana House of Representatives and the people she represents.
