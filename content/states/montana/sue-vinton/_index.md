Sue Vinton, born on November 26, 1956, is an American politician who has been serving in the Montana House of Representatives from the 56th district since 2017. She started her career as a litigation paralegal and is a business owner and a general contractor in Montana.

On November 8, 2016, Vinton won the election and became a Republican member of Montana House of Representatives for District 56. She won re-elections in 2018 and 2020. In addition to her political career, Vinton's personal life includes her husband, Mike Vinton, and their four children. They reside in Lockwood, Montana. As of January 4, 2021, she holds the position of Majority Leader of the Montana House of Representatives.
