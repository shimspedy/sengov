Wayne Rusk is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 88 in the Montana House of Representatives. He assumed office on January 2, 2023, and his current term is set to end on January 6, 2025. Rusk ran for election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 88 and won in the general election held on November 8, 2022.

Rusk resides in Corvallis, Montana. His career experience includes being a business owner and a tradesman specializing in custom walk-in showers. Rusk is 52 years old and has lived in the Bitterroot Valley for 30 years. He and his wife, Carrie, have been married for 25 years and have two children.

Rusk is an avid hunter and fisherman. He is a self-employed tradesman with deep roots in the Stevensville community. He brings a lifelong interest and several years of local involvement to his role. Rusk is running on a platform of fiscal conservatism. He is pro-Life, a staunch supporter of the 2nd Amendment, believes in responsible forest management, and is against mandates. Rusk is a Constitutional conservative who says he will bring an optimistic spirit to the challenges faced in Ravalli County and beyond.
