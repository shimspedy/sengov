Willis Curdy, born on October 30, 1947, is an American politician. He has served as a Democratic member for the 98th district in the Montana House of Representatives since 2015. Curdy was born in Bitterroot Valley and currently resides in Missoula, Montana. He attended the University of Montana twice, once from 1967 to 1971 and again in 1987.

In addition to his political career, Curdy has also worked as a businessman and a teacher. He is currently a member of the Montana State Senate, representing District 49. He assumed office on January 2, 2023. His current term ends on January 4, 2027.
