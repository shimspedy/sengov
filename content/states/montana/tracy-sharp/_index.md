Tracy Sharp is a dedicated public servant and a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 12. Born in Stanley, Wisconsin, Tracy has a rich background that combines military service, education, and a commitment to community leadership. Her journey to the Montana legislature is a testament to her dedication to serving others and her ability to lead with integrity and vision.

Tracy's academic achievements include earning a bachelor's degree in aeronautical and astronautical engineering from Purdue University. She furthered her education by obtaining a graduate degree in military arts and science from the United States Air Force War College. These academic pursuits laid the foundation for her distinguished career in the U.S. Air Force, where she served with honor and distinction.

During her time in the Air Force, Tracy held various roles that showcased her expertise and leadership skills. She worked as a pilot and strategic planner, contributing to critical politico-military affairs. Her work included treaty negotiations across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa. Tracy's involvement with the Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe (OSCE) during the mid-1990s highlights her commitment to fostering international cooperation and security. She played a significant role in arms reduction efforts in Bosnia and participated in the Dayton Accords Article IV talks in Vienna as a NATO representative.

After retiring from her military career, Tracy transitioned to public service, bringing her wealth of experience and dedication to the Montana House of Representatives. She was elected to represent District 12 in the 2024 general election, where she secured a decisive victory. Tracy's legislative priorities reflect her deep understanding of the challenges facing Montana residents. She is particularly focused on addressing issues such as property taxes, affordable housing, and resource management. Her goal is to create a sustainable and secure future for her constituents while preserving the unique character of Montana.

Tracy Sharp's life and career are marked by a commitment to excellence, service, and leadership. Her diverse experiences and unwavering dedication make her a valuable asset to the Montana legislature and a respected figure in her community.
