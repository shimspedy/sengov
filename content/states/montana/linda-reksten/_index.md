Linda Reksten is a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 12. She assumed office on January 4, 2021, and her current term ends on January 6, 2025. She ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 12 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Linda Reksten was born in Polson, Montana. She earned a degree in biology from Eastern Montana College, a master's degree from Point Loma Nazarene University, and a doctorate from the University of California at Los Angeles. Her career experience includes working as a middle school and high school teacher, an elementary school principal, and an author.

In terms of her legislative work, Reksten has been assigned to various committees, including the House Education Committee, House Local Government Committee, and House State Administration Committee.
