Edie McClafferty, also known as Edith McClafferty, is a member of the Democratic Party and has been serving as a member of the Montana State Senate, representing District 38, since 2017. Her current term is set to end on January 6, 2025.

Before her tenure in the Senate, she was a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 73 from 2009 to 2017. During the 2013-2014 session, she served as a Minority Whip. She did not seek re-election to the Montana House of Representatives in 2016 due to term limits.

McClafferty earned her B.A. in Elementary Education from the University of Montana Western in 2004. Over the years, she has been assigned to various committees including the Education and Cultural Resources Committee, Fish and Game Committee, and Senate Taxation Committee.

She ran for re-election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 38 and won in the general election on November 3, 2020.
