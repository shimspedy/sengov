Chris Friedel is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 26 in the Montana State Senate. He assumed office on January 4, 2021, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Friedel ran for election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 26 and won in the general election on November 3, 2020.

Friedel graduated from Billings West High School and earned a degree in small business marketing and finance from Montana Technological University. His career experience includes owning a business.

In terms of his legislative work, Friedel has been assigned to several committees. For the 2023-2024 term, he serves as the Vice Chair of the Highways and Transportation Committee, a member of the Senate Judiciary Committee, and the Chair of the Senate Local Government Committee.

Before his tenure in the Montana Senate, Friedel was a member of the Billings City Council from 2015 to 2019.
