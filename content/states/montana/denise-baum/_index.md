Denise Baum is an American politician who, as of 2023, serves in the Montana House of Representatives representing the 47th district. Prior to her election to the Montana state house in 2022, Baum was a detective with the Billings, Montana police department.

Denise Baum is a member of the Democratic Party. Denise Baum lives in Billings, Montana. Baum's career experience includes working in law enforcement. She assumed office on January 2, 2023. Her current term ends on January 6, 2025. Baum ran for election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 47. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022.
