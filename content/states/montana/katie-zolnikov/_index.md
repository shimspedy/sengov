Katie Zolnikov is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 45th district. She was elected in November 2020 and assumed office on January 4, 2021. Zolnikov succeeded her husband, Daniel Zolnikov, after he resigned from the House in 2020.

In addition to her political career, Katie Zolnikov is also a business owner. She ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 45 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.
