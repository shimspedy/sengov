Nelly Nicol is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 53 in the Montana House of Representatives. She assumed office on January 2, 2023, and her current term is set to end on January 6, 2025. Nicol ran for election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 53 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Nicol was born in and currently resides in Billings, Montana. She earned a B.S. in public relations, advertising, and applied communication from Montana State University-Bozeman in 2003. Her career experience includes working as a communications manager with Victory Insurance Company. Nicol also served as the secretary-treasurer on the board of directors for the Victory Insurance Company.
