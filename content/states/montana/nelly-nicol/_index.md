Nelly Nicol is a dedicated public servant and a prominent member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 53. Born and raised in Billings, Montana, she has deep roots in her community and a strong commitment to serving its residents. Her journey into public service is marked by a blend of professional expertise, community involvement, and a passion for making a difference.

Nelly earned her Bachelor of Science degree in Public Relations, Advertising, and Applied Communication from Montana State University-Bozeman in 2003. Her academic background laid the foundation for a successful career in communications, where she honed her skills as a manager with Victory Insurance Company. In addition to her professional role, she served as the secretary-treasurer on the board of directors for the same company, showcasing her leadership and strategic thinking.

Her entry into politics was driven by a desire to address the needs of her community and contribute to the betterment of Montana. Nelly assumed office on January 2, 2023, and has since been an active and influential voice in the state legislature. Her tenure is characterized by a focus on issues that matter most to her constituents, including economic development, healthcare, and education.

Beyond her professional and political achievements, Nelly is a devoted family person. She balances her responsibilities as a legislator with her role as a wife and mother, drawing inspiration from her family to work tirelessly for the people of District 53. Her ability to connect with individuals on a personal level and her unwavering dedication to public service have earned her respect and admiration across the political spectrum.

Nelly's story is one of perseverance, leadership, and a steadfast commitment to her community. As she continues to serve in the Montana House of Representatives, she remains focused on creating a brighter future for her constituents and the state as a whole.
