Tyson Running Wolf is a Native American politician serving as a member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 16th district. He was born in Browning, Montana, U.S. and is a member of the Democratic Party. He is also a member of the Blackfeet Nation.

Running Wolf assumed office on January 7, 2019, after being elected in November 2018. He was re-elected in 2020. Earlier in his life, Running Wolf worked as an outdoor guide. He earned a Bachelor of Science degree in forest research management from the University of Montana in 2001.

Running Wolf is an avid outdoorsman and an advocate for conservation of the environment. He grew up with generations of men in his family teaching him about how to respect the land so the land gives back.

He is married to Lona and they have seven children.
