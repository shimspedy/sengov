Jeremy Trebas is a dedicated public servant and a prominent figure in Montana's political landscape. Born in Missoula, Montana, he has deep roots in the state and a strong commitment to its people. Trebas earned a bachelor's degree in business administration, which laid the foundation for his professional and political career. His professional journey includes roles as an accountant, a human resources manager with the Great Falls Rescue Mission, and the owner of a tax preparation and accounting business. These experiences have equipped him with a comprehensive understanding of financial management and organizational leadership.

Trebas's political career began with his election to the Montana House of Representatives, where he represented District 25 from 2017 to 2018 and District 26 from 2021 to 2022. During his tenure in the House, he served on several key committees, including the Local Government Committee, the Taxation Committee, and the Transportation Committee, where he held the position of Vice Chair. His work in these committees demonstrated his dedication to addressing the needs of his constituents and improving the state's infrastructure and governance.

In 2025, Jeremy Trebas assumed office as a member of the Montana State Senate, representing District 10. His current term is set to end in 2027. As a state senator, Trebas continues to focus on issues that matter most to Montanans, including transportation, taxation, and local government. His leadership and commitment to public service have earned him the respect and trust of his colleagues and constituents alike.

Beyond his professional and political achievements, Trebas is a devoted family man. He has been married to his wife, Hannah, for over 16 years, and together they have three children: Ryan, Kaylyn, and Aidan. The family resides near North Kiwanis Park and enjoys exploring Montana's natural beauty through camping trips in their truck and camper. They are also proud pet owners, sharing their adventures with their two dogs, a schnauzer/border collie mix and a border-doodle.

Jeremy Trebas's life and career reflect his unwavering dedication to serving his community and making a positive impact on the lives of Montanans. His blend of professional expertise, political experience, and personal values makes him a respected and influential leader in the state.
