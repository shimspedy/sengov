Courtenay Sprunger is an American politician and public relations executive. She is a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 7. She assumed office on January 2, 2023. Her current term ends on January 6, 2025.

Sprunger is a member of the Republican Party. She was born in 1981 and resides in Kalispell, Montana, U.S. She is the owner and CEO of Big Sky Public Relations in Kalispell.

She was elected in 2022, after defeating Democratic nominee Angela Kennedy in the general election. In addition to her political career, Sprunger's career experience includes founding and working as the CEO of her own public involvement firm.

She has served the board of the Kalispell Chamber of Commerce and the Conrad Mansion and Flathead Area Young Professionals. She also chaired the Montana chapter of the Public Relations Society of America and joined the Kalispell Daybreak Rotary.
