Kim Abbott is an American politician who has been serving as a Democratic member of the Montana House of Representatives for District 83 since 2017. Following the 2020 Montana House of Representatives election, she was selected as the House Minority Leader for the term beginning in 2021. From 2013 to 2021, Abbott served as co-director of the Montana Human Rights Network.

Kim Abbott was born in 1979 in Cleveland, Ohio, U.S. and she completed her education at the University of North Carolina, Wilmington. She resides in Helena, Montana with her domestic partner Tara Veazy. Kim Abbott is openly gay. She has been serving as House minority leader since 2023. Her current term ends on January 6, 2025.
