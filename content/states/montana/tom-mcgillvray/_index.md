Tom McGillvray is a dedicated public servant and a prominent figure in Montana politics. Born in Helena, Montana, he is a fifth-generation Montanan with deep roots in the state. His family’s history in Montana dates back to the 1860s, when his great-great-grandfather arrived in Gold Creek during the gold rush era. This rich heritage has instilled in him a profound appreciation for Montana’s natural beauty and cultural legacy.

Tom graduated from Montana State University in Bozeman in 1980 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Agriculture. His professional career spans over 27 years as a financial advisor with Ameriprise Financial, where he helped individuals and families achieve their financial goals. After retiring from his financial career, he turned his focus to public service, driven by a desire to give back to the community that shaped him.

Tom’s political journey began in 2004 when he was elected to the Montana House of Representatives. Over the years, he has held several leadership positions, including Majority Whip, Deputy Minority Floor Leader, and House Majority Leader. His tenure in the House was marked by his commitment to fiscal responsibility, public health, and the preservation of Montana’s natural resources.

In 2020, Tom was elected to the Montana State Senate, where he continued to serve with distinction. He has chaired several key committees, including the Committee on Committees and the Public Health Committee. His legislative priorities reflect his deep commitment to the well-being of Montanans, focusing on issues such as healthcare, education, and environmental conservation.

Tom is also a passionate advocate for Montana’s outdoor heritage. An avid outdoorsman, he enjoys hunting, fishing, backpacking, and exploring the state’s forests, streams, and lakes. He has worked tirelessly to protect Montana’s public lands and waters from threats such as invasive species and noxious weeds, ensuring that future generations can enjoy the state’s natural treasures.

Beyond his professional and political achievements, Tom is a devoted family man. He and his wife, Margaret, have been married for over four decades and are proud parents to three children and grandparents to six grandchildren. His family shares his love for Montana’s outdoors, and together they have created countless memories exploring the state’s wilderness.

Tom McGillvray’s life and career are a testament to his unwavering dedication to Montana and its people. His leadership, vision, and commitment to public service continue to make a lasting impact on the state he calls home.
