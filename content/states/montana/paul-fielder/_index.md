Paul Fielder is a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 13. He is a member of the Republican Party. He assumed office on January 4, 2021, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. He ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 13 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Paul Fielder's career experience includes working as a wildlife biologist. He has been assigned to several committees including the Energy, Technology, and Federal Relations Committee, Fish, Wildlife and Parks Committee, House Rules Committee, and House Taxation Committee, which he chaired.

He resides in Thompson Falls, Montana, U.S. His education includes a BS and MS from Murray State University.
