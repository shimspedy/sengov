Frank Smith is a member of the Democratic Party and represents District 31 in the Montana House of Representatives. He assumed office on January 4, 2021, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Smith ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 31 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Smith previously held office in the Montana House of Representatives from 1999 to 2005 and from 2011 to 2013. He also served in the Montana State Senate from 2005 to 2009 and from 2017 to 2021.

Frank Smith was born and lives in Poplar, Montana. He graduated from Poplar High School and served in the U.S. Air Force for five years. Smith's career experience includes owning a business.

In terms of committee assignments, Smith has been assigned to various committees over the years, including the Agriculture Committee, House State Administration Committee, and Transportation Committee.
