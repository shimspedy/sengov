Lyn Hellegaard, born Darrellyn Bingham on March 29, 1958, in Moab, Utah, is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 97th district. She is a member of the Republican Party. She assumed office on January 2, 2023. Her current term ends on January 6, 2025. She married Steven Charles "Steve" Hellegaard on April 21, 1979, in Ravalli, Montana. They have two children. She resides in Missoula, Montana.
