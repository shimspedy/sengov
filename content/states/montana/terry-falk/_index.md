Terry Falk is a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 8. He is affiliated with the Republican Party. He assumed office on January 2, 2023, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025.

Falk ran for election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 8 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. He lives in Kalispell, Montana, and his career experience includes working as a real estate lender.

In the 2023 session, Falk sponsored several bills, but some of them died in the standing committee. He is a member of the Appropriations Committee and the House Legislative Administration Committee.

Before his political career, Falk was an entrepreneur and businessman. His understanding of the devastation that bad fiscal policy creates has been noted.
