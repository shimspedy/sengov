Connie Keogh is an American politician and educator who is currently serving as a member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 91st district. She was born in Roscoe, Montana, and has a strong educational background with a Bachelor of Science degree in education from the Montana University System and a Master of Education from Western Governors University. Before her political career, Keogh worked as a science teacher and, after earning her master's degree, she served as a student mentor and program director at Western Governors University. She was elected to the Montana House of Representatives in November 2018 and assumed office on January 7, 2019. Her current term ends on January 6, 2025.
