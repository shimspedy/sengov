Mike Lang is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 17 in the Montana State Senate. He assumed office in 2017 and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Lang ran for re-election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 17 and won in the general election on November 3, 2020.

Before his tenure in the Senate, Lang was a Republican member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 33 from 2013 to 2017.

In terms of committee assignments, Lang has served on several committees over the years. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, he was assigned to the Agriculture, Livestock and Irrigation Committee (as Chair), Committee on Committees, Finance and Claims Committee, Senate Legislative Administration Committee (as Chair), and Senate Natural Resources Committee.

Lang's profession is a certified crop advisor. He completed his Bachelor's degree at Eastern Montana College.
