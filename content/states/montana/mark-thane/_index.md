Mark Thane, a dedicated member of the Democratic Party, serves in the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 89. He assumed office on January 6, 2025, and his current term is set to conclude on January 4, 2027.

Born and raised in Missoula, Montana, Mark Thane has deep roots in his community. He pursued his education at the University of Montana, earning both a bachelor's degree in 1980 and a master's degree in 1985. His professional journey has been marked by a long and impactful career in education, where he worked as a teacher and administrator. His commitment to public service extends beyond the classroom, as he has been actively involved with organizations such as the Montana Federation of Public Employees and the School Administrators of Montana.

Thane's dedication to his constituents is evident in his legislative priorities and committee assignments. He has served on key committees, including the House Education Committee, the House Local Government Committee, and as Vice Chair of the House Taxation Committee. His work in these roles reflects his commitment to addressing critical issues such as education, local governance, and fiscal responsibility.

Beyond his legislative duties, Thane is a devoted family man. He and his wife, Mary, are proud parents of two daughters, Lindsay and Emilie. His community engagement includes serving on various local boards, such as the Missoula Family YMCA, SPARK! Arts Missoula, and the Missoula Education Foundation. These roles highlight his dedication to fostering a vibrant and supportive community.

Mark Thane's career is a testament to his unwavering commitment to public service, education, and the betterment of his community. His leadership in the Montana House of Representatives continues to make a positive impact on the lives of his constituents in District 89.
