Shelley Vance is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 34 in the Montana State Senate. She assumed office on January 2, 2023, and her current term ends on January 4, 2027.

Vance ran for election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 34 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. During her tenure, she has sponsored several bills, although the specific details of these bills are not available.

In the 2023-2024 legislative session, Vance was assigned to several committees, including the Finance and Claims Committee, the Highways and Transportation Committee, and the Senate State Administration Committee.

In the 2022 elections, Shelley Vance defeated Damion Lynn in the general election for Montana State Senate District 34, securing 63.9% of the votes. Prior to this, she also defeated Bryan Haysom in the Republican primary for the same district.
