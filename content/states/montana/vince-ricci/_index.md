Senator Vince Ricci has been a prominent figure in Montana's political landscape, known for his dedication to public service and his commitment to the values of his constituents. Representing District 27 in the Montana State Senate, Ricci has built a reputation as a steadfast advocate for economic growth, energy independence, and limited government intervention.

Before his tenure in the Senate, Ricci served as a member of the Montana House of Representatives, where he represented District 55. During his time in the House, he was actively involved in several key committees, including the Appropriations Committee, the Business and Labor Committee, and the Agriculture Committee. His leadership roles, such as serving as Vice Chair of the Business and Labor Committee and Chair of the Legislative Administration Committee, highlighted his ability to navigate complex legislative processes and build consensus among his peers.

Ricci's professional background extends beyond politics. He is the owner and operator of Ricci's Express, a business that reflects his entrepreneurial spirit and understanding of the challenges faced by small business owners. His hands-on experience in the private sector has informed his policy positions, particularly his emphasis on reducing regulatory burdens and fostering a business-friendly environment in Montana.

A lifelong Montanan, Ricci graduated from Park County High School in Livingston. He is deeply rooted in his community and values the importance of family. This strong family foundation has shaped his perspective on issues such as education, public safety, and healthcare.

His stance on energy policy underscores his belief in the importance of maintaining Montana's independence and leveraging its resources to benefit its residents.
