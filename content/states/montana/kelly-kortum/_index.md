Kelly Kortum is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 65th district. He was elected in November 2020 and assumed office on January 4, 2021. Kortum was raised in Ekalaka, Montana and earned a Bachelor of Science degree in computer science from Montana State University. In addition to his political career, Kortum has worked in construction and as a firefighter. He is also the founder of the Gallatin Progressive Action Network. His current term in office is set to end on January 6, 2025.
