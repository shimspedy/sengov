Scott DeMarois is a Democratic Party member serving in the Montana House of Representatives. He represents District 71 and assumed office on January 6, 2025. His current term is set to end on January 4, 2027.

Born and raised in Anaconda, Montana, Scott DeMarois has deep roots in his community. He graduated from Anaconda High School and pursued further education at Montana Tech. His professional career was dedicated to public service as a firefighter, where he rose through the ranks to retire as a Captain. His commitment to the safety and well-being of his community extended beyond his career, as he has been actively involved with organizations like the Montana State Council of Professional Fire Fighters and the Montana State Fire Fighters Association.

Scott DeMarois entered politics with a vision to bring positive change to his district and to advocate for the needs of his constituents. His campaign focused on issues such as public safety, education, and economic development, which resonated with voters. In the 2024 general election, he secured a decisive victory, earning 61.8% of the vote.

As a legislator, DeMarois is known for his dedication to his district and his collaborative approach to governance. He strives to address the challenges faced by his community while upholding the values of transparency and accountability. His journey from a firefighter to a state representative exemplifies his unwavering commitment to serving the people of Montana.
