Tony Brockman is a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 9. He is affiliated with the Republican Party. His tenure began on January 2, 2023, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. He was last elected on November 8, 2022.

In the 2022 general election for Montana House of Representatives District 9, Tony Brockman won the election. Prior to this, he defeated Constance Neumann and David August in the Republican primary for the same district. His committee assignments for 2023-2024 include the Agriculture Committee, House Legislative Administration Committee, House State Administration Committee, and Transportation Committee.
