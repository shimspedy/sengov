Terry Moore, born on May 25, 1952, is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 54th district. He was elected in November 2018 and assumed office on January 7, 2019.

Terry Moore born and raised in Billings, Montana, Moore earned a Bachelor of Science degree in business with a concentration in accounting from Montana State University. Prior to his political career, Moore worked as the chief financial officer of First Interstate Bank and was also a manager at KPMG. He succeeded Jeff Essmann in the Montana House of Representatives. His current term ends on January 6, 2025.
