Forrest Mandeville is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 29 in the Montana State Senate. He assumed office on January 2, 2023, and his current term ends on January 4, 2027.

Mandeville ran for election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 29 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. Prior to this, he was a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 57 from 2015 to 2020.

Born and raised in Columbus, Montana, Mandeville graduated from Columbus High School in 2002. He earned a bachelor's degree in history and political science from Point Loma Nazarene University in 2006 and a graduate degree from Montana State University-Billings in 2013.

His career experience includes working as a planner in Stillwater County, Montana, and at Engineering West. He also owns his own business.
