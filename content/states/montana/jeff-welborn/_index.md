Jeff Welborn is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 36 in the Montana State Senate. He assumed office in 2017 and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Welborn ran for re-election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 36 and won in the general election on November 3, 2020.

Before his tenure in the Senate, Welborn was a Republican member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 72 from 2009 to 2017. In the 2013-2014 session, he served as a Majority Whip.

Over the years, Welborn has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 session, he was assigned to the Agriculture, Livestock and Irrigation Committee, Committee on Committees (Vice Chair), Finance and Claims Committee, Senate Natural Resources Committee (Chair), and Senate Rules Committee.

Jeff Welborn was born in Dillon, Montana, where he currently resides.
