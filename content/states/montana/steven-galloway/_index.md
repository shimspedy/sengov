Steven Galloway is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 24 in the Montana House of Representatives. He was born in Great Falls, Montana and graduated from CMR High School in 1977. His career experience includes owning and operating businesses.

Steven Galloway assumed office on January 4, 2021 and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Galloway ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 24 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.
