Michele Binkley is an American politician serving as a member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 85th district. She was born in 1968 in Browning, Montana. Binkley is affiliated with the Republican Party and resides in Hamilton, Montana. She was elected in November 2020 and assumed office on January 4, 2021.

Before entering politics, Binkley was an escrow officer at First American Title. She has two children with her spouse, Dwayne. Binkley ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 85 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. Her current term ends on January 6, 2025.
