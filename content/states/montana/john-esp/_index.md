John Esp is a member of the Republican Party and a representative in the Montana State Senate for District 30. He assumed office on January 7, 2019, and his current term ends on January 4, 2027. Esp ran for re-election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 30 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Before his current position, Esp was a Republican member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 61 from 2011 to 2013. He also served in the Montana State Senate, representing District 31 from 2003 to 2011.

Throughout his tenure, Esp has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, he was assigned to the Committee on Committees, Finance and Claims Committee (as Chair), Senate Legislative Administration Committee (as Vice Chair), and Senate Rules Committee.

Esp was born in 1952 in Bozeman, Montana. He is a professional carpenter by trade and resides in Big Timber, Montana with his spouse, Stoney Esp. They have two children.
