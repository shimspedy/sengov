Brian Close, a dedicated member of the Democratic Party, serves in the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 65. He assumed office on January 6, 2025, and his current term is set to conclude on January 4, 2027. 

Born in Highland Park, Illinois, Brian Close has built a career marked by a commitment to public service and a deep understanding of complex legal and legislative matters. He holds a Bachelor’s degree in History from the University of Chicago, earned in 1981, and later pursued advanced legal studies, obtaining a Juris Doctor from IIT-Chicago Kent School of Law in 1986, followed by an LL.M in Taxation from New York University Law School in 1987. His academic journey also includes a Master’s degree in History from Montana State University, completed in 2000.

Professionally, Close has practiced law for over four decades, specializing as a tax attorney. His extensive experience in the legal field has equipped him with the skills to navigate intricate legislative challenges effectively. Beyond his legal career, he has been actively involved in various public service initiatives, focusing on issues such as senior care, public health, transportation, and urban planning. Notably, he contributed to drafting the city charter, showcasing his dedication to improving governance and community development.

As a legislator, Brian Close has prioritized addressing pressing issues facing Montana. He has advocated for property tax reform, emphasizing the need to reduce tax rates and provide relief for individuals on fixed incomes. Additionally, he has championed affordable housing initiatives, calling for modernization of housing authority statutes and exploring government-supported solutions to address housing challenges. Close has also been a strong proponent of Medicaid renewal, highlighting its importance in stabilizing rural hospitals, enhancing employment opportunities, and reducing overall healthcare costs.

Brian Close’s tenure in the Montana House of Representatives reflects his unwavering commitment to serving his constituents and tackling the critical issues that impact their lives. His blend of legal expertise, public service experience, and legislative focus positions him as a dedicated advocate for the people of District 65.
