Ed Byrne is a dedicated public servant and a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 11. His journey to this position is marked by a distinguished career in the military and a commitment to serving his community.

Born in 1965, Ed Byrne grew up with a strong sense of duty and discipline, which later defined his professional and personal life. He pursued his undergraduate education at the University of Montana, where he earned a degree in political science. His thirst for knowledge and leadership skills led him to further his education, obtaining a Master of Public Administration from George Mason University, a Master of Science and Administration from Central Michigan University, and a Master of Strategic Studies from the U.S. Army War College.

Before entering politics, Byrne served in the United States Army, where he rose to the rank of colonel. His military career was marked by dedication, strategic thinking, and a commitment to excellence. These qualities have seamlessly transitioned into his role as a legislator.

In 2024, Ed Byrne ran for election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 11, an area east of Kalispell. His campaign resonated with voters, emphasizing community values, fiscal responsibility, and a vision for a prosperous Montana. He won the election with an impressive 76% of the vote, defeating his opponent Jennifer Allen. Byrne officially assumed office on January 6, 2025.

As a legislator, Byrne is known for his pragmatic approach to governance and his ability to build consensus. He is deeply committed to addressing the needs of his constituents and ensuring that their voices are heard in the state legislature. His priorities include improving infrastructure, supporting education, and fostering economic growth in his district and beyond.

Ed Byrne resides in Bigfork, Montana, where he is an active member of the community. His life and career are a testament to his unwavering dedication to service, both in uniform and in public office. Through his work in the Montana House of Representatives, Byrne continues to make a meaningful impact on the lives of those he represents.
