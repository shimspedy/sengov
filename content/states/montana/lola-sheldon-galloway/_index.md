Lola Sheldon-Galloway is a Montana politician and currently serves in the Montana House of Representatives. She was born in 1959 in Great Falls, Montana. She attended Montana State University in 2010. Her husband, Steven Galloway, was elected to the state House of Representatives in 2020. They both have six children.

Lola Sheldon-Galloway is a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 22. She assumed office in 2017. Her current term ends on January 6, 2025. She ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 22 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

She has served in the legislature since the 2017 session and is currently the Vice Chair of the Montana GOP. She was Vice Chair of the House Human Services Committee and was also on the Taxation and Rules Committees during the 2021 session.
