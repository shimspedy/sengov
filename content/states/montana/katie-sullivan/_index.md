Katie Sullivan is an American politician and a member of the Democratic Party. She represents the 89th district in the Montana House of Representatives. She assumed office on January 7, 2019. Her current term ends on January 6, 2025. In March 2018, former Representative Dave Severson was appointed to replace Nate McConnell in the Montana House, and decided not to file for election.

Sullivan filed for election and won a four-way Democratic primary with 49.8% of the vote. She went on to win the general election with 62.4% of the vote. As of July 2020, Sullivan sits on the following committees: Energy, Telecommunications, and Federal Relations Rules Business and Labor Agriculture. Sullivan has a degree in human biology from the University of Montana, a JD from the University of Montana School of Law, and an LLM from the University of Colorado.
