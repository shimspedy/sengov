Morgan Thiel is a dedicated public servant and a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 30. A Republican, she assumed office on January 6, 2025, following her successful election in November 2024. Her term is set to conclude on January 4, 2027. 

Born and raised in Sidney, Montana, Morgan Thiel graduated from Sidney High School in 2013. She pursued further education at Sheridan College and Evangel University, where she honed her skills and broadened her understanding of the challenges facing her community. Her professional background includes working as a project coordinator at Thiel Brothers Roofing, a role that allowed her to engage directly with the needs of local businesses and residents.

Morgan's journey into politics was driven by her commitment to serving her community and addressing the issues that matter most to her constituents. Her campaign focused on fostering economic growth, supporting local businesses, and ensuring that the voices of her district are heard in the state legislature. Her dedication to these principles resonated with voters, earning her a decisive victory in both the Republican primary and the general election.

As a legislator, Morgan Thiel brings a fresh perspective and a strong work ethic to the Montana House of Representatives. She is passionate about creating opportunities for the people of her district and is committed to working collaboratively with her colleagues to achieve meaningful results. Her leadership and vision continue to inspire those around her, making her a respected figure in Montana's political landscape.
