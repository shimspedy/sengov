Amy Regier is an American politician and a Republican member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 6th district. She assumed office on January 4, 2021, succeeding Carl Glimm.

Born in 1978 in Kalispell, Montana, U.S., Amy was educated at Flathead High School and received a degree from Montana State University's College of Nursing. She has worked as a nurse at Kalispell Regional Medical Center. Her father is Keith Regier.

Amy Regier's current term ends on January 6, 2025. She won the general election on November 8, 2022, and her career experience includes working as a registered nurse with Kalispell Regional Medical Center.
