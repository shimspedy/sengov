Anthony Nicastro is a dedicated public servant and a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 50. A Republican, Nicastro assumed office on January 6, 2025, following his victory in the general election held on November 5, 2024. His current term is set to conclude on January 4, 2027.

Nicastro's academic journey began at Wake Forest University, where he earned both his bachelor's degree and his law degree. His educational background laid a strong foundation for his career in law and public service. As a lawyer, Nicastro has demonstrated a commitment to justice and a deep understanding of the legal system, which he brings to his role as a legislator.

In the 2024 election, Nicastro emerged victorious against his opponent, Shawn Giesick, securing 66.2% of the votes. This decisive win highlighted the trust and confidence that the residents of District 50 placed in his leadership and vision for the community.

As a state representative, Nicastro is focused on addressing the needs and concerns of his constituents. He is known for his pragmatic approach to governance and his dedication to finding solutions that benefit the people of Montana. His legislative priorities include economic development, education, and healthcare, reflecting his commitment to improving the quality of life for all Montanans.

Beyond his professional achievements, Nicastro is deeply rooted in his community. He values the importance of family, community engagement, and public service. His work in the Montana House of Representatives is driven by a desire to make a positive impact and to ensure that the voices of his constituents are heard and represented.

Nicastro's journey in public service is a testament to his dedication, resilience, and unwavering commitment to the people of Montana. As he continues to serve in the Montana House of Representatives, he remains focused on building a brighter future for his district and the state as a whole.
