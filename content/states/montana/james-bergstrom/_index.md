James Bergstrom is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 30 in the Montana House of Representatives. His tenure began in 2023 and his current term is set to end in 2025.

Bergstrom ran for election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 30 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

He earned an associate degree in computer science and a bachelor's degree in business administration and accounting from The College of Great Falls in 1994. His career experience includes owning and operating his own business and working as a rancher/farmer, a financial advisor with Ameriprise Financial, a software sales representative, and a teacher at Dawson Community College, Judith Gap Public School, and Hobson Public School.

Bergstrom served as an FFA advisor for 12 years and served for two terms as a school board member at Judith Gap Public School.
