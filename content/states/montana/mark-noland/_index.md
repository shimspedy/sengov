Mark R. Noland is an American politician and a member of the Republican Party. He was a member of the Montana House of Representatives, where he represented District 10, including Bigfork, Montana.

Born in 1959 in Whitefish, Montana, Noland is a business owner by profession. He is married to Kathy and they have six children. They reside in Bigfork, Montana. Noland is an alumnus of the University of Montana.

Noland was first elected to represent District 10 in the Montana House of Representatives in 2014. During his tenure, he served on several committees including Business and Labor (chair), Natural Resources, and Legislative Administration.

His electoral record is impressive, having won the general elections in 2014, 2016, and 2018 with a significant majority of votes. In the 2020 Republican primary for the Montana House of Representatives, District 10, Noland secured 55.9% of the votes.
