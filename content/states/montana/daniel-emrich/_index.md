Daniel Emrich is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 11 in the Montana State Senate. He assumed office on January 2, 2023, and his current term is set to end on January 4, 2027.

Emrich ran for election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 11 and won in the general election held on November 8, 2022. He defeated the incumbent, Tom Jacobson, in this election.

In terms of committee assignments for the years 2023-2024, Emrich was assigned to the Education and Cultural Resources Committee, Fish and Game Committee, and the Senate Judiciary Committee.

Emrich was born in Great Falls, Montana. For further contact, he has an official website and also maintains a presence on Facebook and Twitter.
