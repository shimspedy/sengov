Jerry Schillinger is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 37 in the Montana House of Representatives. He assumed office on January 4, 2021, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Schillinger ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 37 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Schillinger graduated from Circle High School and earned a degree in accounting from the University of Montana. His career experience includes working as a farmer and a Certified Public Accountant (CPA).

In terms of legislative service, Schillinger was assigned to several committees. For the 2023-2024 term, he served on the Appropriations Committee, House Ethics Committee (as Chair), House Rules Committee, and Legislative Audit Committee. He had similar assignments in the 2021-2022 term.
