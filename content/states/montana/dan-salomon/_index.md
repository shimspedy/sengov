Daniel Salomon is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 47 in the Montana State Senate. He assumed office in 2017 and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Salomon ran for re-election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 47 and won in the general election on November 3, 2020.

Before his tenure in the Senate, Salomon was a Republican member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 93 from 2011 to 2017. He did not seek re-election to the Montana House of Representatives in 2016.

Daniel Salomon was born on January 27, 1957. He has served on various committees throughout his legislative tenure, often as chair. These committees include Agriculture, Livestock and Irrigation Committee; Education and Cultural Resources Committee; and Finance and Claims Committee.
