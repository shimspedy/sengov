Fiona Nave is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 57th district. She was born in 1954 in Manitowoc, Wisconsin, U.S. and is a member of the Republican party. Fiona Nave was elected to the Montana House of Representatives in November 2020 and assumed office on January 4, 2021. She was re-elected to a second term in November 2022 and in April 2023, she was inducted into the Freedom Caucus of Montana.

Nave was raised in Laurel, Montana and earned a Bachelor of Science degree in applied mathematics from California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. She served as an operations research analyst and computer specialist for the United States Navy for 26 years. She has also served as the chair of the Stillwater County, Montana Republican Central Committee since 2015 and was a member of her local ambulance district.

In her personal life, Fiona Nave's husband, Brent Nave, worked as an engineer for the Navy. They have two children and reside in Columbus, Montana.
