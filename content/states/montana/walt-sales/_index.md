Walt Sales is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 35 in the Montana State Senate. He assumed office on January 4, 2021, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Prior to this, he served in the Montana House of Representatives for District 69.

Sales graduated from Manhattan High School in 1978 and has a career as a rancher. He has also served with Hearts and Homes, Manhattan Christian School Athletics, Board of Bozeman Health, and Gallatin Water Resources Task Force.

In the 2023-2024 legislative session, Sales was assigned to the Business, Labor and Economic Affairs Committee, the Energy and Telecommunications Committee (as Chair), and the Senate Natural Resources Committee (as Vice Chair).

Sales ran for election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 35 and won in the general election on November 3, 2020.
