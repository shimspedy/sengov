Marilyn Marler is an American politician and a member of the Democratic Party. She represents the 90th district in the Montana House of Representatives. Marler's political career began with her service on the City Council of Missoula, Montana from 2006 to 2018. In 2018, when Ellie Hill, the former District 90 representative in the Montana House of Representatives, was unable to run for reelection due to term limits, Marler ran for the open seat. She won the election, defeating Republican Nick Knowles with 67.8% of the vote.

As of 2023, Marler continues to serve in the Montana House of Representatives. In addition to her political career, Marler holds a Bachelor's degree from the University of California, Davis and a Master's degree from the University of Montana, both in Biology. She has been a Natural Areas Specialist at the University of Montana since 1998.
