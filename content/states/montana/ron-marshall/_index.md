Ron Marshall is an American politician and a Republican member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 87th district. He was born in 1962 at McClellan Air Force Base, California. Marshall served in the United States Army from 1982 to 1996. After his military service, he owned and operated small businesses in Victor, Montana. He is also the vice president of the Montana Smoke Free Association and vice-chair for public relations for the Ravalli County, Montana, Republican Central Committee. Marshall was elected to the Montana House of Representatives in November 2020 and assumed office on January 4, 2021, succeeding Nancy Ballance. His current term ends on January 6, 2025.
