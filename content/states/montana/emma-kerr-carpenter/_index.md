Emma Kerr-Carpenter is a dedicated public servant and a prominent figure in Montana's political landscape. Representing District 23 in the Montana State Senate, she has consistently demonstrated her commitment to her constituents and her passion for creating positive change in her community.

Born in Watertown, New York, Emma's journey into public service began with a strong foundation in education. She earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in international relations and religion from Boston University, where she cultivated her understanding of global issues and the importance of community engagement. After completing her studies, Emma embarked on a journey that took her to Belo Horizonte, Brazil, where she taught English and gained valuable insights into diverse cultures and perspectives.

Emma eventually made her way to Billings, Montana, where she became deeply involved in community work. She served as a family support specialist for the Family Support Network, providing essential assistance to families in need. Her dedication to supporting youth and families continued when she joined Youth Dynamics of Montana in 2015. There, she worked as a youth case manager and later as the organization's marketing and education coordinator, showcasing her ability to connect with people and advocate for meaningful causes.

Emma's political career began in 2018 when she was appointed to fill a vacancy in the Montana House of Representatives for District 49. Her appointment marked the start of a dynamic and impactful tenure in state politics. She quickly established herself as a thoughtful and effective legislator, earning the trust and respect of her colleagues and constituents alike. Emma won a full term in the House in November 2018 and was re-elected in 2020, further solidifying her reputation as a dedicated advocate for her district.

In December 2024, Emma transitioned to the Montana State Senate, representing District 23. Her leadership and vision have been instrumental in addressing critical issues facing Montana, including education, healthcare, and economic development. Emma's ability to collaborate across party lines and her unwavering commitment to the well-being of her constituents have made her a respected voice in the Senate.
