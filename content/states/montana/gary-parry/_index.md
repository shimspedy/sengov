Gary Parry is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 39 in the Montana House of Representatives. He assumed office on January 2, 2023, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025.

Gary Parry was born in Helena, Montana, and currently resides in Colstrip. He graduated from Helena Senior High School. He earned a bachelor's degree in geology from the University of Montana in 1975 and an M.B.A. from Sam Houston State University in 1990.

His career experience includes working as a senior contract analyst with Western Energy Company and Westmoreland Coal Company. He ran for election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 39 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.
