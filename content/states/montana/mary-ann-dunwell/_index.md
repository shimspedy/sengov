Mary Ann Dunwell is an American politician who serves as a Democratic member of the Montana House of Representatives for District 84. She was born in 1954 in Wilmington, Delaware and earned a Bachelor of Arts in speech and communications.

Dunwell was elected to the Montana House of Representatives in 2014, and assumed office in January 2015. She lives in Helena, Montana and has one son.

In addition to her service in the House, Dunwell is also a member of the Montana State Senate, representing District 42. She assumed this office on January 2, 2023. Her current term ends on January 4, 2027. Dunwell ran for election to the Montana State Senate to represent District 42 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.
