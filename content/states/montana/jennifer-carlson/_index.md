Jennifer Carlson is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the Montana House of Representatives from the 69th district. She was elected in November 2020 and assumed office on January 4, 2021. Born and raised in Montana, Carlson pursued her education at Montana State University, where she earned a Bachelor of Science degree in biomedical sciences.

Jennifer Carlson lives in Churchill, Montana with her husband, Don, and their five children. In addition to her role as a representative, Carlson has also been assigned to various committees, including the Human Services Committee, which she chairs, and the House Judiciary Committee. She ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 69 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. Her current term ends on January 6, 2025.
