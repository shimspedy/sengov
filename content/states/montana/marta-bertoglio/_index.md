Marta Bertoglio is a member of the Montana House of Representatives, representing District 75. She assumed office on January 4, 2021, and her current term ends on January 6, 2025. She ran for re-election to the Montana House of Representatives to represent District 75 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Marta Bertoglio was born in Deer Lodge, Montana, and currently resides in Montana City, Montana. She served in the U.S. Air Force and the U.S. Air Force Reserve from 1991 to 2003. Her career experience includes working as a space operations officer, an executive officer with a U.S. ambassador, and co-owning Uncommon Ground Realty.

In her tenure, she has been assigned to various committees including the House Education Committee, House Local Government Committee, and House State Administration Committee. She has made significant contributions to the legislation during her term.
