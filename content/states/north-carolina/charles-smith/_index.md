Charles Smith is a Democratic member of the North Carolina House of Representatives, representing District 44. He assumed office on January 1, 2023. His current term ends on January 1, 2025. Smith is running for re-election to the North Carolina House of Representatives to represent District 44. He is on the ballot in the general election on November 5, 2024.

Smith was born in Fayetteville, North Carolina. He earned a bachelor's degree from Emory and Henry College in 2014 and a J.D. from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2017. His career experience includes working as the Cumberland County assistant district attorney and then as an attorney in private practice.

Smith was assigned to the following committees for the 2023-2024 term: Appropriations Committee, Appropriations on General Government Committee, Families, Children, and Aging Policy Committee, State Government Committee, Wildlife Resources Committee, and Military and Veterans Affairs Committee.

As of the 2020 Census, North Carolina state representatives represented an average of 87,116 residents. After the 2010 Census, each member represented 79,715 residents. North Carolina House of Representatives District 44 includes portions of central Cumberland County.

Smith's legislative office is located at 16 West Jones Street, Rm. 1021, Raleigh, NC 27601-1096. His main phone number is 910-977-1602 and his email is Charles.Smith@ncleg.gov. His legislative assistant is Leigh Lawrence.
