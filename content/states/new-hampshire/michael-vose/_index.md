Michael Vose is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Rockingham 5th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He is currently the chair of the Science, Technology and Energy Committee.

Born and raised in Epping, New Hampshire, Vose has always been deeply connected to his community. He received a Bachelor of Arts in History from Keene State College and later pursued a Master of Arts in Public Administration from the University of Maine in 1975.

Before his political career, Vose served in the US Air Force from 1966 to 1970. He also worked as a software technical writer, demonstrating his versatility and adaptability in different professional fields.

Vose's political career is marked by his dedication to public service. He served on the Peterborough Budget Committee from 1984 to 1989, as an Epping Library Trustee from 2012 to 2015, and has been on the Epping Planning Board since 2018. His commitment to his community led him to the New Hampshire House of Representatives, where he has served since 2014.

As a representative, Vose has been a strong advocate for science, technology, and energy, leading him to his current position as the chair of the Science, Technology and Energy Committee. His leadership in this role has been instrumental in shaping policies and initiatives in these areas.

In addition to his political career, Vose is also known for his educational pursuits. He is a proud alumnus of Keene State College and the University of Maine, where he earned his bachelor's and master's degrees respectively.

With the 2024 election just a few months away, Vose has announced that he will once again be a candidate for re-election to the NH House of Representatives. His five previous terms have equipped him with the institutional knowledge and the trust and respect of his legislative colleagues, making him a strong candidate for continued future success advocating for the citizens of Epping.

His leadership in the New Hampshire House of Representatives and his commitment to advancing science, technology, and energy policy demonstrate his unwavering commitment to his constituents and his state.
