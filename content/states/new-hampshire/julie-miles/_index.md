Julie Miles is a Republican member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough District 12. She assumed office on December 4, 2024, and her current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Born and raised in New Hampshire, Julie Miles has deep roots in her community. She pursued her education at NHTI and the University of New Hampshire, earning a Bachelor of Science in Nursing. Her professional career is marked by her dedication to healthcare, having worked as a critical care nursing leader and a medical underwriting nurse specializing in health insurance. Her expertise spans areas such as catastrophic disease states, pharmacy benefit management, and improving patient outcomes through access to quality healthcare.

In addition to her professional achievements, Julie is actively involved in local government and community initiatives. She serves as a member of the Hillsborough County Republican Committee and the Merrimack Republican Town Committee. Her legislative focus includes commerce, consumer affairs, and economic development, reflecting her commitment to fostering a thriving business environment and addressing the needs of her constituents.

Outside of her professional and political life, Julie enjoys open-water sailing and scuba diving, showcasing her adventurous spirit. Her dedication to both her community and personal growth makes her a dynamic and impactful representative in the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
