David Lundgren is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 16. He is affiliated with the Republican Party.

David Lundgren was born in Chelmsford, Massachusetts, and currently resides in Londonderry, New Hampshire. He earned a doctor of chiropractic degree from the Palmer College of Chiropractic in 1974. His career experience includes working as a chiropractor at Lundgren Chiropractic.

He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024. Lundgren ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 16 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Throughout his tenure, Lundgren has been assigned to the State-Federal Relations and Veterans Affairs Committee. This committee considers all matters pertaining to the National Guard and other military or veterans' organizations within the state, potential actions by the U.S. Congress which will have a major impact on the state and the New England region, and all proposed resolutions petitioning the Congress on any matter.

In his personal life, Lundgren has been married and has three children. He has held many positions in the Londonderry area including serving on the Hospice board, soup kitchen, Londonderry Chamber of Commerce, and Londonderry Rotary. He is also a Paul Harris Fellow.
