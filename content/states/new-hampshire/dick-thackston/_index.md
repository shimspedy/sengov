Dick Thackston is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Cheshire 12th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022. His current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Thackston's political journey began when he ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Cheshire 12. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

In addition to his political career, Thackston has a professional background in real estate. He works as a managing partner and real estate broker with R.H. Thackston & Company. His commitment to public service extends beyond the political sphere as he served as the chairman of the Monadnock Regional School District.

Thackston resides in Troy, New Hampshire. His contributions to his community go beyond his political and professional roles. He has served his local community in various capacities such as a Monadnock School Board Member, a Selectman for Troy, and a Moderator for Troy.

Throughout his tenure, Thackston has been involved in various legislative activities and has sponsored numerous bills. His work in the New Hampshire House of Representatives continues to impact the lives of the residents of the Cheshire 12th district and the state of New Hampshire.
