Jessica Grill is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 18. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024. Jessica Grill is affiliated with the Democratic Party and ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 18. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Jessica was born in Manchester, New Hampshire. She is proud to call New Hampshire both her original and chosen home. She earned a B.S. in psychology, magna cum laude, from Northeastern University in 2018. Her career experience includes working in advertising as a marketing associate, assistant strategist, and media coordinator and supervisor.

Jessica is a first-term state representative serving Manchester's Ward 12. She works in advertising as an integrated media supervisor and has led strategic planning efforts across a wide range of vertical markets, including consumer goods, pharmaceuticals, and B2B food service. Besides public service and advertising, Jessica enjoys spending her time skiing, boating, and cheering on her alma mater (Northeastern) in the Beanpot.

She is currently serving as a Clerk in the Election Law Committee. It is the duty of the Committee on Election Law to consider all matters relating to the election laws of the state, including campaign finance, the Ballot Law Commission, redistricting, and such other matters as may be referred to it.

Jessica Grill's involvement in local government includes her current role as a NH State Representative and her former role as a ballot clerk. She represents Manchester Ward: 12.
