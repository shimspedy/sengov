Tanya Donnelly is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 25. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Tanya Donnelly is a member of the Republican Party. She ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 25 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Tanya Donnelly earned a B.S. in computer science from Northeastern University in 2001. Her career experience includes founding Canobie Landing LLC and working as its president. She has also worked as the global social media director of Schneider Electric, and the IT director of APC by Schneider Electric.

She has served on the Town Of Salem Budget Committee, as the president of the Canobie Lake Protective Association, and as the president of the Greater Salem Federation of Republican Women.

In addition to her political career, Tanya Donnelly is an experienced Executive and Entrepreneur with over 30 years of success leading business process re-engineering, business transformation, and staying up-to-date with technology changes for B2B organizations in various industries such as manufacturing, energy management, technology, energy & chemical, food & beverage, mining & minerals.

She is a business owner and proud mother of a Republican son studying to be a mechanical engineer. Today she serves on the Salem's Budget Committee, President of Greater Salem Federated Republican Women's Club, Vice Chair Salem Republicans, Regional Vice-Chair at NH State Republican Party, and President of Canobie Lake Protection Association.
