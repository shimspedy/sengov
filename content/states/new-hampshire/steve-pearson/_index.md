Stephen Pearson is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Rockingham 13th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He has been in office since December 7, 2022. His current term is set to end on December 4, 2024.

Pearson has been a part of the Republican Party and has made significant contributions to the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He has represented two districts during his tenure. Initially, he was a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives from the Rockingham 6th district. He served this district from 2018 until December 7, 2022. Post this, he assumed office for the Rockingham 13th district.

In the 2022 elections, Pearson ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 13. He won the general election held on November 8, 2022. This victory marked the beginning of his current term.

During his tenure, Pearson has been assigned to various committees. For the tenure of 2023-2024, he was assigned to the Legislative Administration Committee. In the previous tenure of 2021-2022, Pearson was assigned to the House Executive Departments and Administration Committee. He was also a part of the same committee during the tenure of 2019-2020.

Pearson's work in the New Hampshire House of Representatives has been marked by his commitment to his party and his constituents. His contributions to the Rockingham 13th district and his previous service to the Rockingham 6th district highlight his dedication to public service.
