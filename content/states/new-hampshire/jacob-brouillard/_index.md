Jacob Brouillard is a member of the Republican Party and currently serves in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 1. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term is set to end on December 4, 2024.

Brouillard ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 1 and won in the general election held on November 8, 2022. He lives in Nottingham, New Hampshire.

He graduated from St. Joseph's College of Maine with a B.S. in Environmental Science and a minor in Chemistry in 2007. His career has been spent in the Environmental Health & Safety field for the private sector. He has worked as the EHS manager for a polyurethane dispersions manufacturer, showcasing his strong ability to interpret administrative rules and regulations.

In addition to his professional career, Brouillard is also a member of the Nottingham Republican Committee. His legislative duties include serving on the Environment and Agriculture Committee in the New Hampshire House of Representatives.

His commitment to public service and his expertise in environmental science make him a valuable member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
