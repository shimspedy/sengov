Gary Merchant is an American politician based in New Hampshire. He is a member of the Democratic Party and represents the Sullivan 4 district in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. His political career began on November 6, 2018, when he was elected to the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 5, 2018.

In addition to his political career, Merchant has an impressive educational background. He graduated from Stevens High School in Claremont, NH. He then earned a degree in pharmacy from Northeastern University in 1977 and later pursued an M.B.A. from the University of New Hampshire in 1995.

Merchant's professional experience is equally noteworthy. He has worked as a regional manager of MedAssets (then Purchase Connection), a manager of business and technology at Dartmouth-Hitchcock, and an administrative director for the New England Pharmacy Collaborative.

As a legislator, Merchant has been assigned to several committees. These include the Health, Human Services and Elderly Affairs Committee and the House Executive Departments and Administration Committee. His work in these committees reflects his commitment to public service and his dedication to the people of New Hampshire.

Merchant resides in Claremont, New Hampshire, where he continues to serve his community and contribute to the political landscape of the state.
