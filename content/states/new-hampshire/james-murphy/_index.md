James M. Murphy is an American politician who serves as a Democratic member for the Grafton 12th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 2, 2020. His current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Murphy ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Grafton 12. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. 

James M. Murphy lives in Hanover, New Hampshire. His career experience includes working as a physician, an orthopedic surgeon, and the chief medical officer of New London Hospital. 

In the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Grafton 12 on November 8, 2022, Murphy was one of the four incumbents who won the election. The other three were Russell Muirhead, Mary Hakken-Phillips, and Sharon Nordgren.

Murphy's tenure as a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives has been marked by his commitment to the Democratic Party's principles and values. He has been a strong advocate for healthcare reform, education, and economic development in his district.
