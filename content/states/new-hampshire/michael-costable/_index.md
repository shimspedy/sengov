Michael Costable is an American politician who is a member of the Republican Party. He currently serves as a representative for the Carroll 8th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term is set to end on December 4, 2024.

Before his current tenure, Costable was a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing the Rockingham 3rd district from 2016 to 2020. However, he resigned from the state legislature on August 1, 2020.

In the 2022 elections, Costable ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Carroll 8. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. In this election, he and incumbent Mark McConkey defeated incumbent Jerry Knirk and Sandra Ringelstein for the two seats in the New Hampshire House of Representatives Carroll 8.

During his time in office, Costable has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 term, he was assigned to the Legislative Administration Committee. In the 2019-2020 term, he was assigned to the Commerce and Consumer Affairs Committee. At the beginning of the 2017 legislative session, he served on the Commerce and Consumer Affairs committee.
