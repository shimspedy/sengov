Vanessa Sheehan is a dedicated public servant who is currently serving as a State Representative for the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 43. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Born and raised in Milford, New Hampshire, Vanessa has deep family roots in the town. She has been married to her husband John for over 23 years, and they have a 14-year-old son. Vanessa has witnessed the growth and changes in Milford over the years, but she believes that the town's unique charm and strong community spirit have remained the same.

Vanessa is a fiscal conservative who is committed to keeping taxes low, living within the state's means, and maintaining a balanced budget. She values the qualities that make her town and state special and works tirelessly to ensure that these qualities are preserved.

Before her political career, Vanessa earned an associate degree in communications and worked as a realtor. Her professional experience has equipped her with valuable skills and insights that she brings to her role as a State Representative.

Vanessa ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 43 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. As a legislator, she has been assigned to various committees, including the Legislative Administration Committee.

Vanessa is passionate about serving her community and state. She is honored to represent the residents of Milford and is dedicated to making a positive impact in their lives and the future of New Hampshire.
