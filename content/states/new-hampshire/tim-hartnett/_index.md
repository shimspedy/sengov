Tim Hartnett is a dedicated public servant and a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough District 41. With a career spanning over four decades, Hartnett has consistently demonstrated a commitment to addressing the pressing needs of his community, particularly in the areas of healthcare, mental health services, and substance abuse treatment.

Hartnett's professional journey is marked by a diverse range of roles, including schoolteacher, direct-care worker, program supervisor, and senior executive in community mental health and substance abuse programs. His work has taken him across New Hampshire and Oregon, where he has been instrumental in creating and directing programs that serve the most vulnerable members of society. One of his notable achievements includes serving as the Director of the State of New Hampshire Division of Alcohol and Drug Abuse, where he played a pivotal role in establishing the state's first Drug Court Program.

In addition to his professional accomplishments, Hartnett has a strong academic background. He holds a Bachelor of Arts degree from New England College, a Master of Social Work from the University of Connecticut School of Social Work, and a Master of Health Administration from Suffolk University. His academic pursuits have also led him to roles in academia, including serving as an adjunct faculty member at Portland State University School of Social Work and as a Visiting Research Fellow at Queen's University in Belfast, Northern Ireland.

Hartnett's dedication to community service extends beyond his professional and academic endeavors. He has been an active board member for organizations such as "Makin' It Happen" and Webster House in Manchester, New Hampshire. His work with these organizations reflects his deep commitment to improving the lives of individuals and families in his community.

As a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, Hartnett continues to advocate for policies that address the health and well-being of his constituents. His extensive experience and unwavering dedication make him a respected and effective leader in the legislature.
