Dru Fox is an American politician who is currently serving as a Democratic member for the Cheshire 2nd district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Before her current position, she served as a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives from the Cheshire 6th district from 2020 until December 7, 2022. 

Dru Fox lives in Keene, New Hampshire. Her career experience includes working as a school district administrator. 

In the 2022 elections, Dru Fox ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Cheshire 2. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022, defeating Wesley Felix. In the general election, Dru Fox received 70.9% of the votes, totaling 1,289 votes, while Wesley Felix received 29.1% of the votes, totaling 529 votes.

In terms of committee assignments, during the 2023-2024 term, Fox was assigned to the House Transportation Committee. She was also assigned to the same committee during the 2021-2022 term.
