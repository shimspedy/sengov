Brian Seaworth is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Merrimack 12th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He has been a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives from the Merrimack 7th district from 2010 to 2012, and from the Merrimack 20th district from 2014 to December 7, 2022. He assumed his current office from the Merrimack 12th district on December 7, 2022.

Seaworth is a member of the Republican Party. His political career in the New Hampshire House of Representatives has been marked by his service in various districts. He has represented the Merrimack 7th district, the Merrimack 20th district, and currently, the Merrimack 12th district.

In terms of education, Seaworth holds a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, which he earned in 1988. His professional career includes working as an engineer at American Electric Power, McDonnell Douglas Aerospace, Gensym, and as a consultant for Rohn Rogers Associates. He also founded Financial Robotics, a software platform.

Throughout his tenure, Seaworth has been assigned to various committees. He has served on the Labor, Industrial and Rehabilitative Services Committee in multiple roles, including as Vice Chair. His commitment to public service and his contributions to the New Hampshire House of Representatives have been significant.

Seaworth ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Merrimack 12 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. His current term ends on December 4, 2024. He continues to serve his constituents with dedication and integrity.
