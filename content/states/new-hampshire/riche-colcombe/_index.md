Riche Colcombe is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She represents the Hillsborough 30 district, which covers Hillsborough and Bennington. She is affiliated with the Republican Party.

Riche Colcombe was first elected to the New Hampshire House of Representatives in 2022. Her tenure began in December 2022 and her current term is set to end in December 2024. She has been serving in this position for over a year now.

Before her political career, Riche Colcombe earned an associate degree from Springfield Technical Community College. She has had a diverse career, including roles as a manager of admin services and vice president & general manager of EMCOR Services New England Mechanical.

In the 2022 general election for the New Hampshire House of Representatives Hillsborough 30, she ran for election and won, securing her current position. The election saw a total of six candidates, with three seats available. Riche Colcombe secured 18.4% of the votes, amounting to 2,268 votes in total.

As a legislator, Riche Colcombe has been assigned to the Labor, Industrial and Rehabilitative Services Committee for the 2023-2024 term. Her work in the committee involves reviewing and making decisions on legislation related to labor, industrial, and rehabilitative services.

Riche Colcombe's dedication to her role and her constituents is evident in her work. She continues to serve the people of Hillsborough 30 with commitment and integrity.
