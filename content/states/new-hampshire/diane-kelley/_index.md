Diane Kelley is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 32. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024. 

Kelley is a member of the Republican Party and ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 32. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

She earned a B.S. from California State University at Channel Islands in 2009. Kelley's career experience includes working as a real estate agent. 

In terms of committee assignments, Kelley was assigned to the following committees:
- Municipal and County Government Committee (2023-2024)
- House Judiciary Committee (2021-2022)

In the 2022 elections, Kelley ran in the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Hillsborough 32 on November 8, 2022. She was one of the winners along with Jim Kofalt (R) and Shane Sirois (R).

On a personal note, Kelley represents Temple, New Ipswich, and Sharon. She is a mother of three humans and one dog. She is also a real estate agent.
