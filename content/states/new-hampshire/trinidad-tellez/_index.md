Trinidad Tellez is an American politician who serves as a Democratic member for the Hillsborough 40th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She assumed office on December 7, 2022. Her current term ends on December 4, 2024. 

Tellez ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 40 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. She is a member of the Democratic Party and represents the Hillsborough 40 district, which includes Wards 1, 3, 10, 11, and 12 in Manchester.

She earned a Bachelor of Arts degree from Harvard University and a Doctor of Medicine degree from Dartmouth College. In addition to her political career, Tellez is a business owner and has worked as the director of the Office of Health Equity of the New Hampshire Department of Health and Human Services. She has also served as an adjunct instructor with the Dartmouth Institute for Health Policy & Clinical Practice, and as a physician.

Tellez's career experience includes owning Health Equity Strategies, LLC. She is also known as "Dr. Trini" and is a working mom, caregiver daughter, wife, active community member, and a 20-year resident of Manchester.

In her role as a legislator, Tellez has been assigned to the Health, Human Services and Elderly Affairs Committee for the 2023-2024 term. She is committed to serving her constituents and making a positive impact in her community.
