Toni Weinstein is a member of the Democratic Party serving in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She represents Rockingham District 10, having assumed office on December 4, 2024. Her current term is set to conclude on December 2, 2026.

Toni Weinstein has a strong background in community service and local governance. Before joining the New Hampshire House of Representatives, she served as a Town Councilor in Newmarket from 2013 to 2022, even taking on the role of Chair from 2019 to 2022. Her dedication to her community is evident through her involvement in various organizations, including the Newmarket Community Development Corporation, the Newmarket Community Education Partnership, and the Newmarket Alliance for Substance Abuse Prevention. She has also volunteered with the New Hampshire Food Bank and coordinated efforts at the Newmarket Food Pantry.

Her professional experience includes working as a food pantry coordinator, showcasing her commitment to addressing food insecurity and supporting vulnerable populations. Toni's educational journey began at Torrington High School in Connecticut, and she later attended Albertus Magnus College in New Haven.

Toni Weinstein's passion for public service is matched by her dedication to her family. She resides in Newmarket with her husband, Josh, and their two sons, Caleb and Nate. Her multifaceted career and personal life reflect her deep commitment to making a positive impact on her community and beyond.
