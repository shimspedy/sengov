Karen Ebel is an American politician who serves as a Democratic member for the Merrimack 7th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She was first elected to the state house in 2012 for the Merrimack 5th district, the numbering for which was changed after the 2021 redistricting. Ebel currently serves as Minority Leader Pro Tempore.

Ebel was the deputy speaker during the 2019-2020 biennium. She currently serves as the ranking member of the Special Committee on Commissions. She has endorsed Cinde Warmington for governor in the 2024 New Hampshire Gubernatorial Election. She also endorsed Amy Klobuchar in the 2020 Democratic Presidential Primary.

On a personal note, Ebel earned a degree from Trinity College in Vermont and a Juris Doctor from Georgetown University Law Center. She resides in New London, New Hampshire.

In her professional life, Ebel has had a successful career as an attorney. Her political career has been marked by her commitment to her constituents and her dedication to public service.
