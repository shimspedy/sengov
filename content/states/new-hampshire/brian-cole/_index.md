Brian D. Cole is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 26. He assumed office on December 7, 2022. His current term ends on December 4, 2024. 

Brian Cole was born in Concord, New Hampshire, and currently resides in Manchester. He graduated from Raymond High School in 1990. He then went on to attend Southern New Hampshire University where he earned an associate degree in accounting and a bachelor's degree in economics and finance in 1995.

In addition to his political career, Brian Cole has also had a successful professional career. He is the CEO of JC International, a wholesale tool company that sells to retailers throughout the USA. This experience in the business world has undoubtedly provided him with valuable insights that he brings to his role as a state representative.

Before his current term, Brian Cole ran for election to the Manchester Board of School Committee to represent Ward 7 in New Hampshire. Although he did not win this election, his commitment to public service remained undeterred.

In his personal life, Brian Cole is known to enjoy a variety of hobbies. He enjoys outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, and hunting. He also enjoys indoor activities like bowling, traveling, playing board games, cribbage, and cooking.
