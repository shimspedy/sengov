Dylan Germana is a Democratic member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Cheshire District 1. He assumed office on December 4, 2024, and his current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Born in Boston, Dylan Germana has been a resident of Keene, New Hampshire, since 2006. Before entering politics, he worked as a traveling manager for Jersey Mike's Subs, where he gained valuable experience in leadership and organizational management. His deep connection to the Keene community inspired him to get involved in local governance, and he has been an active participant in shaping policies that benefit his constituents.

In addition to his role in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, Germana also serves on the Keene Board of Education, where he has been a strong advocate for improving educational opportunities and resources for students. His commitment to public service is evident in his efforts to address issues such as local revenue, property taxes, and municipal governance through his work on the House Committee on Municipal and County Government.

Dylan Germana's dedication to his community and his ability to connect with people from all walks of life have made him a respected figure in New Hampshire politics. He continues to work tirelessly to represent the interests of his district and to contribute to the betterment of the state as a whole.
