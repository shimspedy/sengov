Cam Kenney is a dedicated public servant and a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He represents the Strafford - District 10 and his contact information is PO Box 26 Durham, NH 03824-0026. He can be reached at 508.677.5141. His seat number is 3103.

He is currently serving his third term as a State Representative. In addition to his legislative duties, he is also a law student at the University of New Hampshire (UNH). He serves on the Judiciary Committee, which is responsible for considering all matters relating to the judicial system, right-to-know law, guardianships, life and death (such as abortion or assisted death), landlord/tenant rights, civil proceedings, tort law, forfeiture, immunity, and probate.

In his time in office, Rep. Kenney has held several leadership positions. He has served as Vice Chair and Legislative Director for the Young Dems Caucus. He currently serves as an Assistant Democratic Leader.

Cam Kenney is also a graduate of the University of New Hampshire, having completed his degree in 2018. His political career began in earnest when he was elected to the New Hampshire House of Representatives on November 6, 2018. He assumed office on December 5, 2018. As a Democrat, he has been a strong advocate for his constituents and his party.

In the 2020 Democratic Party presidential primaries, Kenney endorsed Bernie Sanders. He continues to be an active participant in the political process, championing the causes and issues that matter to his constituents and the state of New Hampshire.

Cam Kenney resides in Durham, New Hampshire, where he is an active member of his community.
