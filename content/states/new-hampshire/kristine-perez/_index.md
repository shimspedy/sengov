Kristine Perez is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 16. She is affiliated with the Republican Party. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Kristine Perez lives in New Hampshire. Her career experience includes working as a registered nurse in cardiac intensive care, emergency room, and recovery room. She has specialized in Cardiac Intensive care, Emergency Room and Recovery Room for 42 years. 

She is married and has two adult children and three grandchildren. In addition to her professional career, she has served on the Hollis/Brookline School Board and the Cooperative School task force, both multiple years ago. 

In the 2022 elections, Kristine Perez ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 16. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022. 

As a legislator, she is a member of the Judiciary Committee. It is the duty of the Committee on Judiciary to consider all matters relating to the judicial system, right-to-know law, guardianships, life and death (such as abortion or assisted death), landlord/tenant rights, civil proceedings, tort law, forfeiture, immunity, and probate; and such other matters as may be referred to it.
