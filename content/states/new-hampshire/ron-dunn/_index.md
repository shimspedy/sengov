Ron Dunn is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 16. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Ron Dunn is affiliated with the Republican Party and ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 16. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Ron Dunn lives in Londonderry, New Hampshire. He earned a B.S. in business administration from the University of New Hampshire. His career experience includes working as a pharmaceutical retail store manager and a certified pharmacy technician. Dunn is also a member of the National Rifle Association (NRA).

In his role as a legislator, Dunn has sponsored several bills, although the specific details of these bills are not available at this time. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, Dunn was assigned to the Resources, Recreation and Development Committee.

In the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Rockingham 16 on November 8, 2022, Ron Dunn was one of the seven candidates who won the election. Other winners included David Lundgren, Tom Dolan, Sherman Packard, Kristine Perez, Doug Thomas, and Wayne MacDonald.
