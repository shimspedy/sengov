Kenneth Vincent is an American politician who serves as a Democratic member for the Strafford 12th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. 

He began his political career in the New Hampshire House of Representatives from the Strafford 17th district, serving from 2016 until December 7, 2022. He then assumed office in the Strafford 12th district on December 7, 2022. His current term is set to end on December 4, 2024.

In addition to his political career, Kenneth Vincent has a rich personal and professional background. He was born in and continues to reside in Somersworth, New Hampshire. He is a proud graduate of Somersworth High School. Beyond politics, he has made significant contributions to his community through his work as a firefighter and business owner.

Over the years, Vincent has been assigned to several committees. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, he was assigned to the Commerce and Consumer Affairs Committee. In the 2021-2022 and 2019-2020 sessions, he served on the Science, Technology and Energy Committee.

In the most recent election for the New Hampshire House of Representatives Strafford 12, which took place on November 8, 2022, Vincent was victorious. He won the election alongside Cecilia Rich, Gerri Cannon, and Jeffrey Rich, securing 14.8% of the votes.
