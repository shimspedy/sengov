Linda Ryan is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 4. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024. 

Before her political career, Linda Ryan earned a B.A. from The College of Saint Rose in 1979. She has had a diverse career experience which includes working as a public school teacher, a track and field coach in middle school and high school, and a case worker. 

In addition to her professional roles, she has served her community as a Nashua city conservation commissioner and a Nashua child care commissioner. Her commitment to public service is evident in these roles.

In the political arena, Linda Ryan ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 4. She emerged victorious in the general election held on November 8, 2022. 

As a legislator, she has sponsored several bills, demonstrating her active participation in the legislative process. Her committee assignments for the 2023-2024 term include the Resources, Recreation and Development Committee.

Linda Ryan's dedication to her constituents and her active role in legislation make her a valued member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
