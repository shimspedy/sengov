Mark McConkey is an American politician who is a member of the Republican Party. He represents the Carroll 8th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Mark McConkey is also running for election to the New Hampshire State Senate to represent District 3. He declared his candidacy for the Republican primary scheduled on September 10, 2024.

Before his political career, Mark McConkey was a business owner. His career experience includes owning and operating a business. He has served on the Lakes Region Planning Commission Executive Board and as the chairman of the Ossipee Zoning Board of Adjustment.

In terms of his legislative duties, Mark McConkey has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, he was assigned to the Public Works and Highways Committee as the Chair. In the 2021-2022 session, he was assigned to the Capital Budget Overview Committee and the Public Works and Highways Committee as the Vice-Chair.

Mark McConkey's local government involvement includes his role as a Commissioner of the Lakes Region Planning Commission in Freedom and as a former Chairman of the Ossipee Zoning Board of Adjustment. He was also a former Executive Board member of the Lakes Region Planning Commission.

Mark McConkey resides at 10 Clover Lane #1, Freedom, NH 03836-4205. He can be contacted via email at mrkmcconkey@gmail.com or by phone at 603.520.8275.
