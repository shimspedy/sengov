Jeffrey Rich is a dedicated public servant and a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Strafford District 12. He is affiliated with the Democratic Party.

Rich resides in Somersworth, New Hampshire, at 212 Maple Street. His seat number in the House of Representatives is 4048. He represents the towns of Rollinsford and Somersworth, specifically Wards 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5.

Before his political career, Rich was a woodworker, a profession that requires precision, attention to detail, and creativity. These skills have undoubtedly been beneficial in his role as a legislator.

Rich assumed office on December 7, 2022. His current term is set to end on December 4, 2024. During his tenure, he has sponsored several bills, demonstrating his active participation in legislative matters.

In the general election held on November 8, 2022, Rich ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Strafford 12. He emerged victorious, securing 13.4% of the votes. His win was a part of a successful run by the Democratic Party in this district, as they clinched all four seats.

Rich's commitment to his constituents and his active role in the House of Representatives make him a valued member of the New Hampshire General Court. His journey from a woodworker to a legislator is a testament to his dedication and hard work.
