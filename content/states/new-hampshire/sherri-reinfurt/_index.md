Sherri Reinfurt is a Republican member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough District 29. She assumed office on December 4, 2024, and her current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Sherri Reinfurt's journey into public service reflects her commitment to her community and her dedication to addressing the needs of her constituents. As a representative, she has focused on issues that resonate deeply with the residents of Hillsborough District 29, including housing, local economic development, and community well-being. Her role as Clerk of the Housing Committee underscores her active involvement in legislative matters that directly impact the lives of her constituents.

Before entering politics, Reinfurt gained valuable experience as an administrator at Manchester Chiropractic & Sports Injuries. This role allowed her to develop a strong understanding of organizational management and community health, skills that she has carried into her legislative work. Her professional background has equipped her with a unique perspective on the challenges faced by small businesses and healthcare providers, enabling her to advocate effectively for policies that support these sectors.

Reinfurt's dedication to public service is also evident in her active engagement with local initiatives and her efforts to foster collaboration among community stakeholders. Her approachable demeanor and willingness to listen have made her a respected figure in her district. As a legislator, she continues to prioritize transparency, accountability, and the well-being of her constituents, striving to make a positive impact through her work in the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
