Flanagan holds a Bachelor’s degree from Boston University and a Master’s in Business Administration from Southern New Hampshire University. His professional background includes work in financial management and software design, which has equipped him with a strong foundation for his legislative responsibilities.

He also served on the Brookline Finance Committee for eight years, including a term as chairman. His commitment to education is evident from his service on the Brookline School Board and the Hollis-Brookline Cooperative School Board, where he held leadership positions.

In the New Hampshire House, Flanagan has been an active member of several committees, including the Labor, Industrial, and Rehabilitative Services Committee. He has also served as the House Majority Leader, showcasing his leadership skills and dedication to his party's values. Throughout his career, Flanagan has focused on issues such as education, infrastructure, and community development.
