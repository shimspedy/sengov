Nicholas Germana, also known as Nick, is a member of the Democratic Party and represents Cheshire 1 in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Germana ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Cheshire 1 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. He defeated Aria DiMezzo in the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Cheshire 1.

Nicholas Germana earned a Ph.D. in European intellectual history from Boston College in 2006. His career experience includes working as a professor of history with Keene State College and being a book writer. He has written three books on German intellectual history in the late-18th and early-19th centuries.

In addition to his academic pursuits, Germana is a member of the Environment and Agriculture Committee in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. The committee considers all matters concerning agricultural and farm problems of the state, physical land use including current use, the various agricultural organizations, the protection of the state's environment from forms of pollution other than air or water pollution, and waste management issues.

Germana is a professor of history at Keene State College, and a Keene State alumnus (Class of '97). He is married and has three children.
