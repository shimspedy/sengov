Sayra DeVito is a member of the Republican Party serving in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She represents Rockingham District 8, having assumed office on December 4, 2024. Her current term is set to conclude on December 2, 2026.

Sayra DeVito is a dedicated public servant and community advocate. She is deeply committed to ensuring that government remains transparent, efficient, and responsive to the needs of its citizens. Her platform emphasizes the importance of small government, fiscal responsibility, and empowering families and businesses to thrive without unnecessary interference. DeVito believes in fostering an environment where individuals and communities can make decisions that best suit their unique circumstances.

Beyond her legislative duties, DeVito is actively involved in her local community. She has served on the Danville Forestry Committee and the Danville Heritage Commission, showcasing her dedication to preserving the natural and cultural heritage of her town. Her work in these roles reflects her passion for conservation and her commitment to maintaining the character and history of her community.

As a wife and mother of three, DeVito brings a personal perspective to her work in the legislature. She understands the challenges faced by families and strives to create policies that support and uplift them. Her approach to governance is rooted in the belief that a responsible, limited government can create a foundation for individuals and families to flourish.

DeVito's tenure in the New Hampshire House of Representatives is marked by her focus on practical solutions and her unwavering dedication to the principles of liberty and self-determination. She continues to work tirelessly to represent the interests of her constituents and to uphold the values that define the Granite State.
