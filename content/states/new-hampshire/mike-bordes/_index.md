Mike Bordes is an American politician serving as a Republican member for the Belknap 5th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He is currently in his second term in the NH House. 

Mike Bordes represents Laconia, Wards 1,3,4,5,6. He ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Belknap 3 in 2020 and again ran for election to represent Belknap 5 in 2022. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. His current term ends on December 4, 2024.

In addition to his political career, Mike Bordes is also the Vice Chairman of Brigid's House of Hope. His involvement in local government and non-profit organizations demonstrates his commitment to serving his community.

His contact information is as follows:

- Email: mike.bordes@leg.state.nh.us
- Seat #: 2067

During his tenure, Mike Bordes has sponsored several bills in the session. He has been assigned to the Public Works and Highways Committee for the 2021-2022 term.

Mike Bordes's base salary as a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives is $100/year. However, no per diem is paid.

In the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Belknap 5 on November 8, 2022, Mike Bordes secured 14.2% of the votes, making him one of the four elected representatives from the district.
