Lino Avellani is an American politician and a member of the Republican Party. He currently serves as a representative for the Carroll 4th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. 

Avellani assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term is set to end on December 4, 2024. Prior to his current position, he served as a representative for the Carroll 5th district from 2014 until December 7, 2022. 

In addition to his political career, Avellani is a business owner and has worked as a chef at his own establishment, Lino's Restaurant. His career experience includes owning and working as a chef with Lino's Restaurant. 

Avellani has also been involved in various committees throughout his tenure. From 2015 to 2024, he was assigned to the Labor, Industrial and Rehabilitative Services Committee. 

In the 2022 elections, Avellani ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Carroll 4. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. 

Outside of his political and professional life, Avellani is a Range Safety Officer for the International Defensive Pistol Association and is ranked as a Marksman.
