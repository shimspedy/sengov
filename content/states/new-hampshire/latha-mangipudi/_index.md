Latha Mangipudi is a distinguished member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing the Hillsborough 35th district. She assumed office on November 13, 2013, and has been serving her constituents with dedication and commitment ever since.

Born and raised in India, Latha moved to the United States where she pursued her higher education. She graduated from Boston University and from the All India Institute of Speech and Hearing. Her educational background laid a strong foundation for her future career and political endeavors.

In her professional life, Latha has made significant contributions in the field of healthcare. She is a Speech & Language Pathologist and has worked with the Greater Nashua Mental Health Association. She also founded Shanti Energy LLC, demonstrating her entrepreneurial spirit.

Latha's political career began when she was elected to the New Hampshire House of Representatives on November 5, 2013. As a Democrat, she has been actively involved in various legislative activities and has been a strong advocate for education reform, changes in healthcare policies to help senior citizens, and various community needs.

Apart from her political and professional life, Latha is a resident of Nashua, New Hampshire. She is married and has two children. She has been an active member of the Nashua community, participating in the local Parent Teacher Organization (PTO), and even served on her district's school board.

Latha's journey from India to the New Hampshire House of Representatives is a testament to her determination, hard work, and commitment to public service. Her contributions to her community and the state of New Hampshire are commendable and she continues to be a strong voice for her constituents in the Hillsborough 35th district.
