Lilli Walsh is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 15. She is a member of the Republican Party. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Walsh ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 15 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. She lives in Hampstead, New Hampshire.

She earned a bachelor's degree in business administration from Southern New Hampshire University in 1995. Her career experience includes owning Technology Sales, Inc. and working as one of its sales managers, a commodity manager/procurement specialist with Hewlett-Packard Laboratories, and a senior purchasing agent with Wang Laboratories, Inc.

Walsh has served as the vice chair of the Rockingham County Republican Committee, as the community engagement director of the New Hampshire Federation of Republican Women, and on the Hampstead Budget Committee.

In the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Rockingham 15 on November 8, 2022, she and incumbent Joe Guthrie defeated Laurie Warnock and Lisa DeMio. Walsh received 28.9% of the votes.

She is a New Hampshire native with a strong business background in Procurement/Risk Management/Supply Chain Management at major computer manufacturing corporations. She has also worked in sales for a manufacturing sales rep firm specializing in semiconductors.

Walsh has been a Hampstead resident since 2010. She has served on the Hampstead Budget Committee for the 2021-2022 and 2022-2025 terms. She has also been involved with Friends of Big Island Pond since 2010. She served as the Vice Chair of the Rockingham County Republican Committee and as the Community Engagement Director of the New Hampshire Federation of Republican Women for the 2021-2022 term.

Her interests include golf, gardening, cooking, and travel.
