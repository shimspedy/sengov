Gary Daniels is a dedicated public servant and a long-time resident of Milford, New Hampshire. With over five decades of residency in Milford, he has built a strong connection to his community and has consistently worked to represent its interests.

Gary's professional background includes a career as an independent insurance agent and a Certified Long-Term Care Specialist (CLTC). His educational journey began at Milford Area High School, followed by studies at the New Hampshire Technical Institute and New Hampshire College, where he honed his skills and knowledge to serve his community effectively.

Gary's commitment to public service is evident in his extensive political career. He has served in various capacities, including as a State Senator for the districts of Amherst, Merrimack, Milford, and Wilton. His tenure in the New Hampshire State Senate spanned multiple terms, during which he held key positions such as Chair of the Senate Capital Budget Committee and member of the Senate Judiciary and Transportation Committee. Gary's legislative experience also includes serving as a State Representative for several districts, where he contributed to important committees like the House Labor Committee, which he chaired during multiple terms.

Beyond his legislative roles, Gary has been actively involved in local government. He served on the Milford Board of Selectmen for over two decades and was a member of the Milford School Board. His dedication to education and community development was further demonstrated through his role as the Milford School District Moderator.

Gary's commitment to public service extends to his involvement in various boards and councils. He has been a member of the New Hampshire Workers' Compensation Advisory Council, the New Hampshire Workforce Opportunity Council, and the New Hampshire Highway Layout Commission. His contributions to these organizations reflect his dedication to improving the lives of his constituents.

In addition to his professional and political achievements, Gary has a strong connection to his family and community. He is married to Loreen (Saucier) Daniels, and together they have raised six children. Gary's military service includes time in the U.S. Army, the New Hampshire Army National Guard, and the U.S. Army Reserve, showcasing his commitment to serving his country.

Gary Daniels' extensive experience, dedication to public service, and strong ties to his community make him a respected and influential figure in New Hampshire's political landscape. His work continues to have a positive impact on the lives of those he represents.
