Benjamin C. Baroody is a prominent politician from New Hampshire. He is a member of the Democratic Party and currently represents the Hillsborough 39 district in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. His tenure began on December 7, 2022, and his current term is set to end on December 4, 2024.

Born and raised in Manchester, New Hampshire, Baroody's educational journey started at Manchester Central High School, from where he graduated in 1964. He later pursued higher education at La Salle Extension University, graduating in 1969.

Baroody's political career has been extensive and impactful. He served in the New Hampshire House of Representatives from 1992 to 2012. After a brief hiatus, he was re-elected to the House on November 4, 2014, representing the Hillsborough 43 district. He assumed office on December 3, 2014. His dedication and commitment to public service led to his re-election in 2022, this time representing the Hillsborough 39 district.

In addition to his political career, Baroody has also worked as a realtor, bringing a unique perspective and set of skills to his role as a representative. Throughout his tenure, he has been assigned to several committees, including the Labor, Industrial and Rehabilitative Services Committee.

On a personal note, Baroody is a resident of Manchester, New Hampshire. He is divorced and is a proud father of two children. His life and career are a testament to his dedication to serving his community and making a difference through public service.
