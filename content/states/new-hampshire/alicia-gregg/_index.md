Alicia Gregg is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 7. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024. She ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 7 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Alicia lives in Nashua, New Hampshire. Her career experience includes working as a domestic violence victim's advocate. This role involves helping victims directly, educating about domestic violence, and helping to break the stigmas surrounding it. She and her husband live in Nashua with their three children.

In addition to her role as a representative, Alicia is also involved in local government as a State Representative for Nashua, Ward 7. She is a member of the Children and Family Law Committee and the Special Committee on the Division for Children, Youth and Families (DCYF) and the Family Division of the Circuit Court.

Alicia's commitment to her community and her advocacy work make her a dedicated public servant. Her work in the New Hampshire House of Representatives continues to have a significant impact on her constituents and the state of New Hampshire.
