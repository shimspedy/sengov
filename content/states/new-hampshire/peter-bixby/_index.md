Peter Bixby is a dedicated and experienced member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He represents the Strafford 17 district and is a member of the Democratic Party. His political journey began on November 6, 2012, when he was elected to the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 5, 2012, and has been serving the people of New Hampshire since then.

Born and raised in the United States, Bixby completed his early education at Attleboro High School. He then pursued higher education at Yale University, where he earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in 1984. His thirst for knowledge didn't stop there. He went on to earn a Ph.D. in Comparative Literature from Indiana University Bloomington in 1999.

Before stepping into politics, Bixby had a diverse career. He has worked as a public and private school teacher, an editor for an internet company, and a tutor for an SAT prep company. These experiences have undoubtedly enriched his perspective and approach to public service.

Bixby resides in Dover, New Hampshire. He is a family man, married, and blessed with two children. His personal life reflects his commitment to community and family values.

In his political career, Bixby has shown a particular interest in environmental issues. He has been assigned to the Environment and Agriculture Committee multiple times, reflecting his commitment to these crucial areas.

In the 2022 elections, Bixby ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Strafford 13. He won the general election on November 8, 2022, and assumed office on December 7, 2022. His current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Peter Bixby's journey is a testament to his dedication to public service and his commitment to the people of New Hampshire. His work in education and politics, coupled with his academic background, make him a valuable member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
