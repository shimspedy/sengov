Laura Telerski is an American politician who serves as a Democratic member for the Hillsborough 11th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Laura Telerski was born and raised in Nashua, New Hampshire, the neighborhood she now represents. She is a product of Nashua public schools. After completing her schooling, she pursued higher education and received a BA in History from Georgetown University in 1993. She furthered her studies and earned a MA in Middle Eastern Studies from the University of Chicago in 1995.

Before her political career, Laura served as the New Hampshire State Chair for the Georgetown University Alumni Admission Program since 2006. In November 2017, Laura became a Selectman for Ward 8. Her dedication to public service led her to run for office in the New Hampshire House of Representatives.

In the House, Laura has been assigned to various committees throughout her tenure. In the 2023-2024 term, she was assigned to the House Finance Committee and the Finance - Division III Committee. In the 2021-2022 term, she was assigned to the House Transportation Committee, a role she also held in the 2019-2020 term.

Laura Telerski lives in Nashua, New Hampshire. She is married and has three children. Her commitment to her community and her extensive experience in public service make her a dedicated representative for the people of the Hillsborough 11th district.
