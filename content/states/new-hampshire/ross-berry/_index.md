Ross Berry is a Republican member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He represents Hillsborough District 44. Berry assumed office on December 4, 2024, and his current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Ross Berry has a background in economics and political science, having earned his bachelor's degree from the University of the South. His professional career includes serving as the executive director of the New Hampshire Republican State Committee and working as the president of RevT Strategies, a company he founded. Berry has also been involved in data management roles within the GOP, showcasing his expertise in political strategy and campaign management.

In addition to his legislative duties, Berry has been an active participant in community initiatives. He serves on the board of the Elliot Perry Foundation, which focuses on supporting children and young adults through projects like funding handicap-accessible playgrounds and youth sports teams. His commitment to public service extends beyond politics, reflecting his dedication to improving the lives of those in his community.

Berry resides in Weare, New Hampshire, with his family. His personal experiences, including navigating challenges like his son's emergency medical care, have shaped his perspective and strengthened his resolve to make a positive impact. Outside of his professional and legislative responsibilities, Berry enjoys rural living and cherishes the opportunities New Hampshire has provided him and his family.
