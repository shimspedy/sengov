Bruce Tatro is an American politician who serves as a Democratic member for the Cheshire 10th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Tatro has a rich history of public service. He served as a Town of Swanzey selectman and on the Monadnock School District school board. He was also the superintendent of the City of Keene Public Works and was vice president of New Hampshire Road Agents.

In terms of education, Tatro graduated from Murdock High School in Massachusetts. He furthered his education by earning an associate degree from Keene State College.

Over the years, Tatro has held various positions in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He was a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives from the Cheshire 6th district from 2010 to 2012, and from the Cheshire 15th district from 2012 to 2020. Currently, he represents the Cheshire 10th district.

In the 2022 elections, Tatro ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Cheshire 10. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Throughout his tenure, Tatro has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, he was assigned to the Municipal and County Government Committee. He also served on the same committee in the 2019-2020 session, where he held the position of Vice chair.

Tatro's dedication to public service and his commitment to the people of New Hampshire are evident in his work. His contributions to the state and his district are commendable, and he continues to serve with distinction.
