Santosh Salvi is a Democratic Party member serving in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He represents Hillsborough District 9 and assumed office on December 4, 2024. His current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Santosh Salvi has been a resident of Nashua, New Hampshire, since 1997. Over the years, he has established himself as a successful entrepreneur and a dedicated community leader. His journey began as a software engineer, working with Fortune 500 companies. However, during the financial crisis of 2008, he faced a significant challenge when he was laid off from a major financial investment company. Undeterred, he founded his own software company, Unique System Skills LLC, which became a cornerstone of his professional success.

Beyond his entrepreneurial ventures, Salvi has shown a deep commitment to his community. He invested in creating a licensed technical institute to help the local workforce enhance their skills and remain competitive in the job market. His passion for helping others extended to his real estate ventures, where he supported tenants by leveraging his network to find them jobs and even hired some of them in his company. He guided many to complete their college degrees and transition into corporate roles or start their own businesses.

Salvi's dedication to social work is evident in his involvement with the Nashua Soup Kitchen project. Leading a team of 48 local families, he has been instrumental in preparing and serving over 20,000 meals to those in need over the past four years. His contributions to the community also include roles as a Sunday school teacher, temple volunteer, and provider for the homeless.

In the New Hampshire House of Representatives, Salvi serves on the Commerce and Consumer Affairs Committee. His work focuses on matters related to commerce, banking, insurance, and economic development, reflecting his professional expertise and commitment to fostering growth and opportunity for his constituents.

Santosh Salvi's journey is a testament to resilience, innovation, and a profound dedication to community service. His multifaceted career and unwavering commitment to helping others make him a respected figure in both his professional and personal life.
