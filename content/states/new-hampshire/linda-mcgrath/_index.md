Linda McGrath is a Republican member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham District 40. She assumed office on December 4, 2024, and her current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Linda McGrath has a strong educational background, having earned a pharmacy degree from the University of Connecticut. Her professional and personal experiences have shaped her commitment to public service. She grew up in Connecticut, where she learned the values of hard work and personal responsibility from her father, who was raised by a single mother. These early lessons instilled in her a deep sense of community and a desire to contribute meaningfully to society.

Linda's decision to run for office was inspired by her family, particularly her three daughters, their spouses, and her six grandchildren. She is deeply committed to ensuring that future generations can enjoy the freedoms and opportunities that she was fortunate to have. Her guiding principle in her political career is a steadfast commitment to the Constitution, which she views as the cornerstone of American democracy.

In addition to her legislative duties, Linda has been actively involved in her local community for many years. She has volunteered in various capacities, including as a Girl Scout leader, a PTA and school volunteer, and a family mediator in Rockingham County. Her dedication to community service extends to her roles as a hospice volunteer, a synagogue vice president, and a youth group leader. She has also been an active participant in political and civic organizations, serving as a delegate and state committee member for the New Hampshire GOP.

Linda McGrath's legislative focus includes health, human services, and elderly affairs, reflecting her professional background in pharmacy and her commitment to the well-being of her constituents. Her work in the New Hampshire House of Representatives is driven by a desire to protect and enhance the quality of life for the residents of Rockingham District 40. Through her efforts, she aims to build a prosperous, nurturing, and safe community for all.
