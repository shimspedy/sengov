Linda DiSilvestro is a member of the Democratic Party and currently serves in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 17. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

She has had a diverse career experience which includes working as a realtor with Coldwell Banker Realty, an associate with Baroody Real Estate & Associates, and a sales associate with Elmgrove Realty. DiSilvestro has been affiliated with the Canadian Trade Council, the National Association of Realtors, and the Commercial Investment Board of Realtors.

Throughout her tenure, DiSilvestro has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, she was assigned to the Labor, Industrial and Rehabilitative Services Committee. She also served on the same committee during the 2021-2022, 2019-2020, and 2017 legislative sessions.

DiSilvestro moved to Manchester 33 years ago. She raised her two children with her husband and has been an active participant in the schools her children attend. She served on the Board of the "Friends of the Library" for many years and supported the many books which raised money for the Library fund.

She has worked as a union member, worked in retail, worked for a Municipality, and currently is with the REALTORS' selling Real Estate for the past 11 years. Linda loves to spend time with her family and friends. She enjoys supporting her local restaurants, theatres, and shops.

In addition to her professional and personal life, DiSilvestro has been involved in local government. She served on the executive board of Hillsborough County and was a part of Manchester City Democrats. She has also volunteered at the Food Bank, and Meals on wheels and supported candidates running for local positions.
