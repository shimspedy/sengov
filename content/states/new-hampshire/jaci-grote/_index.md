Jaci Grote is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 24. She assumed office on December 5, 2018. Her current term ends on December 4, 2024. Grote is a member of the Democratic Party and ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 24.

She has been assigned to various committees throughout her tenure. In 2023-2024, Grote was assigned to the House Executive Departments and Administration Committee. In 2021-2022, she was assigned to the Information Technology Oversight Committee and the House Executive Departments and Administration Committee. In 2019-2020, she was assigned to the House Executive Departments and Administration Committee.

Grote has also sponsored several legislations during her time as a legislator. However, the details of these bills are not available at the moment.

In the elections of 2022, Grote ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 24. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022. In the same year, she also advanced from the Democratic primary for New Hampshire House of Representatives Rockingham 24 on September 13, 2022.

Before her political career, Grote was an Engineer at Huntsman Polyurethane. She has also served as the Chairman of the Rye Budget Committee, former Chairman of the Rye Planning Board, and a former member of the Rye Conservation Commission.
