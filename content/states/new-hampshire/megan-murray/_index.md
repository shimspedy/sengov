Megan A. Murray is an American politician from the New Hampshire Democratic Party. She has been a Democratic member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives for Amherst, New Hampshire since 2018.

Murray was first elected to the New Hampshire House of Representatives in 2018 for Hillsborough District 22 and served for that district until 2022. She currently serves for the Hillsborough 37th district. She serves on the Environment and Agriculture Committee and has served as the Deputy Ranking Member since 2022. 

Murray is married and has three children. She is a former school teacher. Her passions include being outdoors in nature, near water or in it, and focusing on policies that help make the lives of Granite Staters better, more equal, and more accessible.

In the 2022 elections, Murray ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 37. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022. Her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Murray's career experience includes working as a public school educator. She is known for her commitment to the environment and agriculture, serving on related committees throughout her tenure. She is also the Vice Chair of the Hillsborough County Delegation.

Murray's dedication to her constituents and her passion for improving the lives of the people in New Hampshire are evident in her work. She continues to serve with distinction, bringing her experience and commitment to the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
