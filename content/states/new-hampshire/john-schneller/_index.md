John Schneller is a Republican member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough District 2. He assumed office on December 4, 2024, and his current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

John Schneller has a rich background in financial services and community involvement. He is currently a partner and Chief Financial Officer at Finanova Financial Services, a financial technology firm. With nearly three decades of experience in financial analysis, banking, and executive management, John has cultivated a deep understanding of both the private sector and public service. His academic credentials include a Bachelor of Arts in History from the University of Massachusetts Amherst, a Master of Business Administration from Cornell University, and a Master of Public Administration from Suffolk University.

Beyond his professional achievements, John has been an active member of his local community in Bedford, New Hampshire. He has served on the Bedford Town Council, the Bedford School Board, the Energy Commission, and the School Curriculum Committee. His dedication to education and local governance reflects his commitment to fostering a collaborative and thriving community.
