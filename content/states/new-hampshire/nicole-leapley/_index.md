Nicole Leapley is a dedicated and accomplished member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough District 22. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024. Nicole is a member of the Democratic Party and has been serving her constituents with commitment and integrity.

Born in rural Nebraska, Nicole's journey has been one of continuous learning and growth. She graduated from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln and then moved to Philadelphia, Colorado, St. Louis, and different parts of France before finally settling in New Hampshire in 2005. Her diverse experiences have enriched her understanding of different cultures and perspectives, making her a well-rounded individual.

Nicole holds a doctorate in French literature from the prestigious University of Pennsylvania. She spent over 20 years working in higher education, dedicating her career to nurturing minds and shaping futures. As an educator, she has touched many lives and continues to inspire her students and colleagues with her passion for knowledge.

In addition to her academic pursuits, Nicole is also deeply involved in her local community. She is serving her second term on the Manchester Board of School Committee, where she sits on the Policy Committee and chairs the Student Conduct Committee. Her work in local government reflects her commitment to creating a better future for the next generation.

As the spouse of a small-business owner and mother of two school-age children, Nicole understands the challenges and rewards of balancing professional and personal responsibilities. She is committed to building a sustainable and equitable Granite State where families and young people can thrive.

Nicole's interests extend beyond her professional and political life. She enjoys outdoor sports, plants, and making pottery. These hobbies allow her to connect with nature and express her creativity, providing a balance to her busy life.

Nicole Leapley's life and career are a testament to her dedication, resilience, and commitment to serving her community. Her journey from rural Nebraska to the New Hampshire House of Representatives is an inspiration to all who aspire to make a difference in their communities.
