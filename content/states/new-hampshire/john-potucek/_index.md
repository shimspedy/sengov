John Potucek is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Rockingham 13th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He has been in office since December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Before his current term, Potucek was a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 6 from 2014 to 2016. He ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 13 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Throughout his political career, Potucek has been assigned to several committees. In the 2023-2024 term, he was assigned to the Commerce and Consumer Affairs Committee. He also served on the same committee in the 2021-2022 term as Vice-Chair and in the 2019-2020 term. At the beginning of the 2015 legislative session, Potucek served on the Resources, Recreation and Development committee.

In addition to his political career, Potucek is known for his involvement in various legislative activities. He has sponsored numerous bills, demonstrating his active participation in shaping the state's laws and policies.
