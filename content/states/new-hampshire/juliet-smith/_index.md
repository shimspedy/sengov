Juliet Smith is a member of the Democratic Party and currently serves in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 18. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Born in Waltham, Massachusetts, Juliet Smith now resides in Manchester, New Hampshire. She completed her high school education at Middlebury High School. Furthering her education, she earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Truman State and a Master of Education degree from the University of Missouri in Columbia.

Before her political career, Juliet Smith worked as an educator, teaching fifth grade and special education. This experience has fueled her interest in the students of New Hampshire and the financing of schools.

In the 2022 elections, Juliet Smith ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 18. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Beyond her role as a legislator, Juliet Smith is deeply interested in a variety of issues affecting New Hampshire. Her major priority is education, both the curriculum and funding, as she believes in finding ways to keep young people in New Hampshire, instead of moving away. She is also an advocate for rights for all'minorities, women, etc.

Health care costs, particularly nursing home costs and medicines, are another area of concern for Juliet Smith. She believes these are too expensive for many people who have to make unsafe choices.

On the topic of gun control, Juliet Smith believes in the 2nd amendment, but questions the necessity of automatic weapons. She advocates for the registration of guns and owners, plus background checks before purchase.
