David Milz is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 13. He is a member of the Republican Party. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

David Milz was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois. After graduating from Carl Schurz High School in Illinois, he served in the United States Air Force from 1968 to 1972, during which he was deployed to Vietnam. After his military service, he earned a B.A. in English from the University of Illinois.

Milz has had a diverse career. He worked for Continental Airlines from 1967 to 1987, retiring as General Manager. He then worked for Marriott Inflight Services from 1988 to 1996, and Gate Gourmet Flight Services from 1996 to 2001.

In addition to his professional career, Milz has been actively involved in local government. He has served as an East Derry Fire Commissioner, a Town Councilor for Derry District 2, and a Commissioner for the Derry Housing & Redevelopment Authority. He has also served on the Public Works and Highways Committee in various capacities, including as Clerk.

David Milz is married and does not have children. He currently resides in New Hampshire.
