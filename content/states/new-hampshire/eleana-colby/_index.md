Eleana Colby is a member of the Democratic Party serving in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She represents Merrimack District 9 and assumed office on December 4, 2024. Her current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Eleana Colby is a dedicated public servant and a lifelong resident of New Hampshire. She has lived in Bow, New Hampshire, for over two decades, where she has been deeply involved in her community. Before her tenure in the state legislature, she served as the Vice Chair of the Bow Board of Selectmen, showcasing her commitment to local governance. Her professional background includes significant experience in the automotive industry, where she managed teams in both sales and service departments. Additionally, she has contributed to the educational sector as a paraprofessional in the special education department at Bow High School.

Beyond her professional and legislative roles, Eleana has been actively engaged in various community initiatives. She has served on nonprofit boards and committees, including the Bow Historical/Heritage Commission and the Hazard Mitigation and Planning Committee. Her involvement extends to youth sports, where she has been a coach and past president of the Bow Youth Baseball and Softball organization. Eleana is also a certified State of New Hampshire Public Supervisor, further emphasizing her dedication to public service.

As a mother of five, Eleana Colby balances her professional and personal commitments with grace. Her passion for community service and her dedication to improving the lives of her constituents make her a respected figure in New Hampshire politics.
