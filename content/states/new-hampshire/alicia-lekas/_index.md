Alicia Lekas is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 38. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024. She is a member of the Republican Party.

Alicia Lekas graduated from West Geauga High School. She earned a B.A. in mathematics from Rivier College in 1994. She co-founded the Women's Defense League of New Hampshire.

In terms of her political career, Lekas ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 38. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

She has been assigned to the House Education Committee for the years 2023-2024, 2021-2022, and 2019-2020.

In the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Hillsborough 38, which had 2 seats, incumbent Tony Lekas and incumbent Alicia Lekas defeated Cynthia Koutroubas and Timothy Wyatt on November 8, 2022.
