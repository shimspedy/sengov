Tony Lekas is an American Republican politician who serves in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He represents Hillsborough County - District 38 alongside Alicia Lekas. 

Tony Lekas is currently serving his second term and represents the towns of Hudson and Pelham. He is a member of the Committee on Executive Departments and Administration, which is responsible for considering matters pertaining to the general administration of state laws and changes therein, matters of policy pertaining to the executive departments, matters relating to the New Hampshire Retirement System, matters pertaining to the administration of professional licensing, review of performance audits, and such other matters as may be referred to it.
