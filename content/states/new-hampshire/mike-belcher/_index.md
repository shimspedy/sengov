Mike Belcher is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Carroll 4th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022.

Born in Portsmouth, New Hampshire, Mike Belcher now resides in Wakefield, New Hampshire. He completed his high school education at Portsmouth High School. His career is diverse and includes experience in heavy industry, as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT), and paramedic with the Durham Ambulance Corps. In addition to these roles, he is also a writer.

Mike Belcher ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Carroll 4. He emerged victorious in the general election held on November 8, 2022. His current term in office is set to end on December 4, 2024.

In the 2022 elections, Mike Belcher and incumbent Lino Avellani defeated Knute Ogren and Max Gehring in the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Carroll 4. Mike Belcher secured 29.7% of the votes.

As a legislator, Mike Belcher has been assigned to the House Education Committee for the 2023-2024 term. He has sponsored several bills, contributing to the legislative process.

Mike Belcher's commitment to public service continues to impact the lives of the residents of the Carroll 4th district and the state of New Hampshire.
