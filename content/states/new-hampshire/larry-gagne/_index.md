Larry Gagne is a member of the Republican Party and is currently serving as a representative in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 16. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Gagne ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 16 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. Prior to this, he served in the Hillsborough 13 District of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, having been elected on November 4, 2008, by finishing third and taking one of the three potential seats there.

Throughout his tenure, Gagne has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, he was assigned to the State-Federal Relations and Veterans Affairs Committee. In the 2021-2022 legislative session, he served as the Vice-Chair of the House Transportation Committee. He also served on the House Transportation Committee in the 2019-2020 legislative session. At the beginning of the 2017 legislative session, he served on the Criminal Justice and Public Safety committee. He also served on this committee in the 2013-2014 and 2011-2012 legislative sessions, during which he was the Vice Chair.

Gagne's contact information is as follows: 126 Lakeside Drive, Manchester, NH 03104-5801, lgagne25@comcast.net, 603.625.9692. His seat number is 2052.

In terms of his personal biography, Gagne is serving his 7th term in the NH House and represents Manchester, Ward 6. He is a graduate of Central HS (Manchester, NH) and St. Anselm College (Manchester, NH). He served in the US Navy from 1961-66. He is a lifetime member of the NRA, GO-NH, and Pro-Gun NH. He is also a retired Postal Service worker.
