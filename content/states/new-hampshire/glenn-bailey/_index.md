Glenn Bailey is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Strafford 2nd district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He has been a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives from the Strafford 1st district from 2020 until December 7, 2022. After that, he became a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives from the Strafford 2nd district and is currently serving in this position.

Bailey's contact information is available as follows: 
- Address: 156 Park Place, Milton, NH 03851
- Email: grbailey@metrocast.net
- Seat #: 3019

In terms of his committee involvement, Bailey is a member of the Committee on Executive Departments and Administration. This committee is responsible for considering matters pertaining to the general administration of state laws and changes therein, matters of policy pertaining to the executive departments, matters relating to the New Hampshire Retirement System, matters pertaining to the administration of professional licensing, review of performance audits, and such other matters as may be referred to it.

Bailey represents the towns of Middleton and Milton, and Rochester Ward 5. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024. He ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Strafford 2.

Bailey's political career is marked by his dedication and service to the people of New Hampshire. His contributions to the state's legislative processes and his commitment to his constituents exemplify the qualities of a dedicated public servant.
