Cindy Bennett is a Republican member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham District 4. She assumed office on December 4, 2024, and her current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Cindy Bennett has deep roots in New Hampshire, where she was born and raised. She has been a resident of Raymond for over 26 years, actively contributing to her community in various capacities. A dedicated educator, Cindy has worked as a math teacher and tutor, sharing her passion for learning and inspiring countless students. Beyond her professional life, she is a devoted mother of 13 children and a proud grandmother to nine grandchildren.

Her commitment to public service extends beyond her role in the state legislature. Cindy has served on the Raymond School Board and as a Library Trustee Chair, showcasing her dedication to education and community development. She is also a member of the Ethics Committee, where she works to uphold integrity and transparency in governance.

In her personal life, Cindy enjoys knitting, reading, and gardening. She is musically inclined, playing the piano and flute, and finds joy in singing traditional hymns. Her faith and interest in biblical studies play a significant role in her life, and she strives to inspire others through her actions and words.

Cindy Bennett's journey in public service reflects her unwavering commitment to her constituents and her passion for making a positive impact in her community. Her diverse experiences and strong values make her a respected and influential figure in the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
