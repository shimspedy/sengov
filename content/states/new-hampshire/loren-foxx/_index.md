Loren Foxx is an American politician and a member of the Democratic Party. He serves as a representative for the Hillsborough 2nd district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Born in Newport Beach, California, Loren Foxx now resides in Bedford, New Hampshire. After graduating from Corona del Mar High School in 1991, he pursued higher education and earned a B.S. in business from the University of Southern California in 1996. Later, he obtained a master's degree in performance psychology from National University in 2022.

Before entering politics, Foxx had a diverse career. He worked as an auditor with Deloitte and Touche, and later transitioned to pharmaceutical sales with Eli Lilly & Co. He also worked in the front offices of several baseball teams, served as the vice president of marketing for the New Hampshire Fisher Cats, and owned a marketing agency.

In the 2022 general election for the New Hampshire House of Representatives Hillsborough 2, Foxx ran for election and won, securing his current position. His victory was part of a larger election where multiple seats were contested. Foxx's win contributed to the Democratic Party's representation in the New Hampshire House of Representatives.

As a legislator, Foxx has been assigned to various committees, including the Fish and Game and Marine Resources Committee. His legislative work and committee assignments reflect his commitment to serving the people of the Hillsborough 2nd district and the state of New Hampshire.
