John Janigian is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Rockingham 25th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Before his current term, he served as a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives from the Rockingham 8th district from 2016 to December 7, 2022. He is a member of the Republican Party.

John Janigian lives in Salem, New Hampshire. He earned a bachelor's degree in chemistry from Tufts University in 1980 and a master's degree in computer science from Boston University in 1985. His career experience includes working as an associate broker with Berkshire Hathaway Verani Realty, the CEO of property development and management with Janigian Associates, and the director with professional services of Bowstreet, Inc.

In addition to his political career, Janigian spent almost 25 years of his career in the computer software business. He is currently a Real Estate Broker and also a Property Manager. John is married and has three adult children.

During his tenure, Janigian has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 term, he was assigned to the House Ways and Means Committee as Vice Chair. In the 2019-2020 term, he was assigned to the Public Works and Highways Committee. At the beginning of the 2017 legislative session, he served on the Public Works and Highways Committee.

John Janigian ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 25. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022.
