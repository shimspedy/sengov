Tim Cahill is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing the Rockingham 4th district. He is affiliated with the Republican Party. Cahill assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Cahill ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 4. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. During his tenure, Cahill has been assigned to the Municipal and County Government Committee for the years 2023-2024.

In addition to his political career, Cahill is a New Hampshire Patriot from Raymond. He is an avid Surfer, Musician, Gardener, Keeper of Chickens, and Outdoorsman. He is a staunch defender of The Republic and works to preserve both the State Constitution and The United States Constitution.

Cahill's base salary as a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives is $100/year. However, no per diem is paid.

In the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Rockingham 4 on November 8, 2022, Cahill secured 20.7% of the votes, amounting to 2,508 votes in total.
