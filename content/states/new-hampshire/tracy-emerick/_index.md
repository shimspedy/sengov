Tracy Emerick is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Rockingham 29th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He has been in office since 2022 and his current term ends in 2024. Prior to this, he served for Rockingham's 29th district since 2012 before the 2020 redistricting. He currently holds the position of chair of the Finance Division II Committee.

Emerick resides in Hampton, New Hampshire. He is married and has two children and five grandchildren. He graduated from Ayer Senior High School in 1965. He earned a B.S. in philosophy from the College of William and Mary in 1969, an M.B.A. from Northeastern University in 1974, and a Ph.D. in business administration from California Coast University.

In addition to his political career, Emerick has had a successful career in business. He has been a self-employed marketing consultant for twenty-five years and has written two business books and one self-help book. His career experience also includes working as an author and owning a business.

Emerick has been serving his fifth term in the New Hampshire House. He has been married for 54 years. He has also served as a member of the Town of Hampton Planning Board.

In the 2022 elections, Emerick ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 29. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022.
