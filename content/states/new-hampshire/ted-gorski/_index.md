Ted Gorski is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough District 2. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024. 

Ted Gorski has been an entrepreneur for over two decades. He is a father, husband, and grandfather of three wonderful grandchildren. He has lived in New Hampshire for over 35 years and is serving his second term in the New Hampshire House.

In addition to his role as a representative, Ted Gorski has been involved in local government as a member of the Transportation Committee. He has also held the position of Vice Chair on the Committee on Transportation. The committee is responsible for considering all matters pertaining to the development, operation, and regulation of all means of air, land, and water transportation, except for the capital improvement and repair of public highways and the motor vehicle code.

Before his political career, Ted Gorski had a diverse professional background. He has worked as a software development manager, director of database and software support, and director of continuing engineering. He was also an adjunct professor with Hesser College and served as the president of and a leadership coach with Get Your Edge, LLC.

Ted Gorski lives in Bedford, New Hampshire. He has been a resident of the state for over 35 years, demonstrating his commitment to serving the community. His dedication to public service and his extensive professional experience make him a valuable member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
