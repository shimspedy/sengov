Paul Tudor is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Rockingham 1st district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 2, 2020. His current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Before his political career, Paul Tudor served in the U.S. Air Force. He also has over twenty-five years of experience leading General Electric's test operation and jet engine facility design team. During that time, he was responsible for controlling the day-to-day operations and expense budget for the Jet engine test department.

In the political arena, Tudor ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 1. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. As a legislator, he has been involved in various committees and has sponsored several bills.

Tudor's dedication to public service and his extensive experience in the corporate world have equipped him with the skills and knowledge necessary to effectively represent his constituents in the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
