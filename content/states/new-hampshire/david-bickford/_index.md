David Bickford is an American politician who is a member of the Republican Party. He is currently serving as a representative for the Strafford 3rd district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. His tenure began on December 7, 2022, and his current term is set to end on December 4, 2024.

Bickford has had a long and distinguished career in politics. Before his current term, he served as a Republican member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Strafford 3 from 2012 to 2016. He also represented Strafford 3 from 1996 to 2006 and from 2008 to 2010.

In addition to his political career, Bickford served in the U.S. Army from 1970 to 1976. He has also been involved in local governance, serving on the New Durham Budget Committee, the New Durham Planning Board, and the New Durham Zoning Board of Adjustments.

During his time in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, Bickford has been assigned to several committees. In the 2023-2024 session, he was assigned to the Children and Family Law Committee. In the 2015 legislative session, he served on the Municipal and County Governments committee. In the 2013-2014 legislative session, he served on the Municipal and County Government committee. And in the 2009-2010 legislative session, he served on the Children and Family Law committee.

Bickford ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Strafford 3 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. He defeated David Wessel in the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Strafford 3.
