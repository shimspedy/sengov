Erica de Vries has a rich professional background and a strong commitment to her community. She owns a consulting company, Accessio Strategies, which specializes in providing fundraising guidance to nonprofit organizations across New England. Her work spans a variety of sectors, including environmental organizations, schools, colleges, animal welfare groups, arts organizations, and museums. Before establishing her consulting firm, Erica worked at an academic medical center in Boston and led a consultancy in London that served clients across the UK and mainland Europe.

Erica's educational journey reflects her dedication to public service and law. Her academic foundation has been instrumental in shaping her approach to governance and community engagement.
