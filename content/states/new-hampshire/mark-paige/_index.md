Mark Paige is a member of the Democratic Party and represents Rockingham 11 in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024. Paige ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 11.

Mark Paige earned a B.A. in political science and government from Tufts University in 1997, a J.D. from the University of Wisconsin at Madison in 2007, and a Ph.D. from the University of Wisconsin at Madison in 2011. Paige's career experience includes working as an associate professor and department chair with the University of Massachusetts at Dartmouth, a school law attorney with Drummond Woodsum, and a program supervisor with the University of Wisconsin. He has served on the board of directors of the Great Bay Kids Early Learning Center and the Southern District YMCA.

In terms of committee assignments, Paige was assigned to the House Judiciary Committee for the years 2023-2024. He also sponsored legislation during his tenure, although the specific bills are not listed.

In the general election for the New Hampshire House of Representatives Rockingham 11 on November 8, 2022, Paige won alongside Gaby Grossman, Julie Gilman, and Linda Haskins, all from the Democratic Party.

In addition to his political career, Mark retired from Nashua fire as a lieutenant after 30 years of service. He has also worked in private EMS off and on for 25 years, rising to the level of advanced EMT. Mark is also a member of Massachusetts one Disaster Medical Assistance Team. He also served four terms as New Hampshire State Representative.
