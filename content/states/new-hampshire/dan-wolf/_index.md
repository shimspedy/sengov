Dan Wolf is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Merrimack 7. He is a member of the Republican Party. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Wolf lives in New Hampshire. He earned a Bachelor of Arts degree from Nasson College in 1970. His career experience includes working as president of Hodan Properties, deputy chief of the Newbury Fire Department, and photographer at the New London Inn and United Press International.

In addition to his work in real estate and public service, Wolf has also served in various roles within the community. He previously served as chairman of the Kearsarge Regional School Board. He currently serves as chairman of the Newbury Trustee of the Trust Fund.

Wolf has been assigned to several committees during his tenure. For the 2023-2024 term, he was assigned to the Resources, Recreation and Development Committee. In the 2021-2022 term, he was assigned to the Fish and Game and Marine Resources Committee. In the 2019-2020 term, he was assigned to the House Education Committee.

In the 2022 elections, Wolf ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Merrimack 7. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. The election saw incumbent Karen Ebel and incumbent Dan Wolf defeating Gregory Sargent and Claire Ann Ketteler in the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Merrimack 7.

Wolf resides in Newbury, New Hampshire. He is married and has two children.
