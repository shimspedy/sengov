Karel Crawford is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Carroll 3rd district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She assumed office on December 7, 2022. Her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Before her current term, she served as a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives from the Carroll 4th district from 2012 until December 7, 2022. 

In addition to her political career, Karel Crawford is also a business owner. Her career experience includes owning a business. She has also served on the Moultonborough School Board and the Moultonborough Selectboard.

Crawford ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Carroll 3. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022. In the election, Karel Crawford and Richard Brown defeated Gabrielle Watson and Mary Peaco Todd. Karel Crawford received 27.8% of the votes, amounting to 3,000 votes.

Throughout her political career, Crawford has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, she was assigned to the House Transportation Committee. In the 2021-2022 legislative session, she served as the Clerk of the House Transportation Committee. She also served on the House Transportation Committee in the 2019-2020 legislative session and at the beginning of the 2017 and 2015 legislative sessions.
