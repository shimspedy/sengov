Tony Piemonte is a member of the Republican Party and currently serves in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Rockingham 9. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term is set to end on December 4, 2024.

Piemonte ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 9 and won in the general election held on November 8, 2022. Prior to this, he served in the New Hampshire House of Representatives for Rockingham 4.

Before his political career, Piemonte served in the U.S. Navy from 1968 to 1972. After his military service, he became involved in local government, serving on the Sandown Budget Committee.

In terms of committee assignments, Piemonte was assigned to the following committees during his tenure:
- State-Federal Relations and Veterans Affairs Committee (2023-2024)
- Municipal and County Government Committee, Vice-Chair (2021-2022)
- Municipal and County Government Committee (2019-2020)
