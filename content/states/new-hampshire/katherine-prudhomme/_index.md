Katherine Prudhomme O'Brien is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Rockingham 13th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She has been in office since December 7, 2022. Her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Katherine Prudhomme O'Brien lives in New Hampshire. She graduated from Northeast Regional Vocational and Technical High School in Wakefield, Massachusetts, in 1988. She earned a B.A. in American history and legal studies from the American College of History and Legal Studies.

Before her political career, Katherine worked for ten years as a machinist and professional level, handmade flute and piccolo maker. She then taught her two daughters at home until they went to high school.

In her political career, Katherine has served in various capacities. She previously served a term in the NH House in 2015-2016 and was then a member of The Labor, Industrial and Rehabilitative Services Committee. She ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Rockingham 13 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Katherine Prudhomme O'Brien is a dedicated public servant with a passion for her community and a commitment to making a difference.
