Harry Bean is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Belknap 6. He is a member of the Republican Party. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Harry Bean lives in Gilford, New Hampshire. He was a member of the Elks, Rod and Gun, and American Legion. Prior to his current tenure, he served in the New Hampshire House of Representatives for Belknap 2.

In terms of his committee assignments for the years 2023-2024, Bean was assigned to the House Finance Committee and the Finance - Division II Committee. In the years 2021-2022, he was assigned to the House Finance Committee and the Finance - Division III Committee. In the years 2019-2020, he was assigned to the Environment and Agriculture Committee.

Harry Bean ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Belknap 6. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

In the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Belknap 6, which had 4 seats, Harry Bean secured 14.5% of the votes, making him one of the winners. The other winners were Russell Dumais (R) with 14.4% votes, David Nagel (R) with 13.5% votes, and Richard Beaudoin (R) with 12.3% votes.

Harry Bean has a high school education. He is married and has three children. Apart from his political career, he has experience as a landlord and in construction.
