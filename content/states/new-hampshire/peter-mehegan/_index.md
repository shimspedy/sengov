Peter Mehegan is a member of the Republican Party serving in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He represents Merrimack District 12. Mehegan assumed office on December 4, 2024, and his current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Peter Mehegan has a rich background in education and community involvement. A longtime resident of Pembroke, New Hampshire, he has been a dedicated teacher at Pembroke Academy since 1987. Over the years, he has become well-known for his contributions to drama and the arts, fostering creativity and engagement among his students. His passion for education extends beyond the classroom, as he has been an active participant in local government and community events. Mehegan's voice has been a consistent presence at town meetings since 1988, except for the occasions when his students were competing in state drama festivals.

In his role as a state representative, Mehegan focuses on issues related to education funding and community development. His commitment to these causes reflects his deep understanding of the challenges and opportunities within his district. Through his work, he aims to create a positive impact on the lives of his constituents and ensure that the voices of Merrimack District 12 are heard in the state legislature.

Mehegan's dedication to public service and education highlights his unwavering commitment to making a difference in his community. His leadership and advocacy continue to inspire those around him, reinforcing his reputation as a devoted educator and public servant.
