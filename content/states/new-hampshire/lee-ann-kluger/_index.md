Lee Ann Kluger is a Democratic Party member serving in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She represents Hillsborough District 6 and assumed office on December 4, 2024. Her current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Lee Ann Kluger has a rich background in education, having spent her professional career as an elementary school teacher. She has taught various grades, including kindergarten, third, fourth, fifth, and sixth, focusing on both gifted and challenged students. Her dedication to education has been a cornerstone of her career, and she continues to contribute to the field as a children's book author, drawing inspiration from childhood memories.

Kluger has been a resident of New Hampshire for over three decades, living in Nashua with her husband, Rob, and their son, Sam. Her deep connection to the community is evident through her active involvement in local government and political campaigns. She has frequently participated in poll work and community events, such as "Coffee with the Mayor" in Nashua, showcasing her commitment to civic engagement.

Beyond her professional and political endeavors, Kluger enjoys a variety of hobbies, including cooking, gardening, tennis, kickboxing, and reading. Her multifaceted interests and dedication to public service make her a well-rounded and approachable representative for her constituents in Hillsborough District 6.
