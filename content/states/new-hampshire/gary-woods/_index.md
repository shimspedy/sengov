Gary Woods is a member of the Democratic Party serving in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He represents Merrimack District 30, having assumed office on December 4, 2024. His current term is set to conclude on December 2, 2026.

Gary Woods has a rich and diverse background that has shaped his career in public service. Originally from California, he pursued an impressive academic journey, earning a degree in Physics from UC Berkeley, followed by a Master's in Biophysics from Johns Hopkins University, and finally an MD from the University of Rochester. His professional career as an orthopedic hand surgeon spanned 39 years, during which he made significant contributions to the medical field before retiring in 2014.
