Karen Reid is a member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 27. She is a member of the Republican Party. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024. 

Reid ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 27 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. 

Karen Reid was born in Connecticut and currently resides in Deering, New Hampshire. Her career experience includes working in the healthcare industry. She co-founded the Deering Francestown Republican Committee and has been affiliated with the New Hampshire Farm Bureau. 

Reid was assigned to the following committees: Criminal Justice and Public Safety Committee. 

In the 2022 elections, Karen Reid defeated Susan Kane in the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Hillsborough 27. 

Personal Biography: Born and raised in Connecticut, Reid moved to Deering, New Hampshire in 2017. She is married to Michael Reid and they have two grown children and one grandchild. She has worked in healthcare since 1985 in various roles. She graduated from Bridgeport Hospital School of Nursing in 1991 and from Housatonic Community college in 1993.
