Glenn Cordelli is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Carroll 7th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He currently holds the position of vice chair of the Education Committee.

Cordelli resides in Tuftonboro, New Hampshire. He is married and has three children. His journey in politics began when he was first elected to the New Hampshire House of Representatives in 2012 as a Republican for Carroll's 4th district. After the 2020 redistricting, Cordelli now serves for Carroll's 7th district.

In addition to his political career, Cordelli is also an Information Technology Business Owner and a Member of the Carroll County Delegation. His dedication to public service and his constituents is evident in his work.

Cordelli ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Carroll 7. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. His current term ends on December 4, 2024. Throughout his tenure, Cordelli has been assigned to various committees, with a consistent presence in the House Education Committee. He has served as the Vice Chair of the Education Committee since 2020.

Cordelli's commitment to his role and his constituents is evident in his work and his continued service in the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
