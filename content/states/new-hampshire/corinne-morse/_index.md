Corinne Morse is an American politician who serves as a Democratic member for the Grafton 9th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Morse ran for election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Grafton 9. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022. Morse lives in Canaan, New Hampshire. She has been affiliated with the Canaan Planning Board and the Canaan Elementary School PTA.

In the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Grafton 9, Morse defeated Donald McFarlane. Morse advanced from the Democratic primary for New Hampshire House of Representatives Grafton 9 on September 13, 2022.
