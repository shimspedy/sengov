Mary Murphy is a Republican member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough District 27. She assumed office on December 4, 2024, and her current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Mary Murphy has a diverse and accomplished background that spans multiple industries and roles. A long-time resident of New Hampshire, she has lived in the state for over three decades and currently resides in Francestown with her husband, Mike. Together, they built their "forever" home, Black Shoals Farm, where they enjoy a peaceful life surrounded by nature. Mary is also a proud owner of Jubilee Orpington chickens and a Belgian Malinois dog, reflecting her love for animals and rural living.

Professionally, Mary has achieved significant success in various fields. She has worked for major organizations such as IBM and Eversource, gaining valuable experience in technology and utilities. She also served in the public school system in Bedford, New Hampshire, where she was recognized for her dedication to education. Mary later ventured into the financial services industry, where she built and sold a highly successful wealth management company. Her entrepreneurial spirit continues as she embarks on a new journey with her software company, Cost Cures, which aims to address the high cost of healthcare through price transparency.

Mary's commitment to her community is evident through her involvement in local governance and nonprofit organizations. She has served on the Board of Trustees for Rivier University and St. Joseph Hospital, contributing her expertise to improve education and healthcare in the region. Her dedication to public service extends to her role in the Francestown Broadband Committee, where she works to enhance connectivity for her community.

As a state representative, Mary is focused on addressing key issues that impact the lives of New Hampshire residents. Her priorities include reducing the high cost of living, making the state more business-friendly, and tackling challenges in healthcare, housing, and education. Mary believes in fostering economic growth by attracting businesses to the state and creating opportunities for young and old residents alike.

Mary Murphy's journey is a testament to her resilience, leadership, and unwavering commitment to making a positive impact. Her diverse experiences and accomplishments make her a valuable asset to the New Hampshire House of Representatives and the constituents she serves.
