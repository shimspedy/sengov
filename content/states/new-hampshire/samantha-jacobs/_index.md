Samantha Jacobs is a member of the Democratic Party serving in the New Hampshire House of Representatives. She represents Cheshire District 15 and assumed office on December 4, 2024. Her current term is set to end on December 2, 2026.

Samantha Jacobs hails from Fitzwilliam, New Hampshire, where she spent her early years. She pursued her education at Franklin Pierce University, earning a bachelor's degree in Social Work and Counseling. During her college years, she balanced her studies with full-time work in retail and restaurants, showcasing her dedication and resilience. Samantha later moved to Keene, New Hampshire, around 2011, where she began her career in procurement for a grocery wholesaler. This move also brought her closer to her husband, with whom she has shared eight years of marriage.

Beyond her professional life, Samantha is deeply involved in her community. She serves as the Secretary for the Keene Democrats and volunteers at The Community Kitchen, reflecting her commitment to public service and local engagement. In her leisure time, she enjoys tending to her flower garden, traveling with her husband, and collecting vintage dishes. Her multifaceted life and dedication to her constituents make her a valued member of the New Hampshire House of Representatives.
