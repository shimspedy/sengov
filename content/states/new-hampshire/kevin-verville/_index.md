Kevin Verville is an American Republican politician. He serves in the New Hampshire House of Representatives in Rockingham County alongside Jason Osborne and James Spillane. He is currently serving his third term representing the communities of Candia, Deerfield, and Nottingham.

In his professional life, Kevin Verville is the Technical Director at Summit Packaging Systems, a family-owned multinational manufacturer of aerosol valves with world headquarters in Manchester, NH. He holds eight patents related to aerosol valve technology, demonstrating his expertise in this field.

In 2023, he introduced legislation that would legalise psychedelic drugs like LSD and psilocybin mushrooms, marking a significant moment in his political career.

In addition to his work in the state legislature, Kevin Verville has been involved in local government. He served on the Deerfield Municipal Budget Committee from 2011 to 2018, the Deerfield Safety Services Steering Committee in 2016, and as a Trustee of the Deerfield Trust Funds from 2011 to 2014. He was also a member of the Deerfield Open Space Committee from 2009 to 2010.

Kevin Verville earned a B.S. in Physical Science Secondary Education, Chemistry Option from Plymouth State College, Plymouth, NH in 1994. He held an Experienced Educator certificate from the NH Department of Education from 1994 to 2003.

Outside of his political and professional life, Kevin Verville is a NH Fish & Game Hunter Safety Instructor, a member of Kinnicum Fish & Game Club, and a current board member. He is also a member of the Deerfield Fair Association and holds a General Class License for amateur radio with the call sign KC1DBR.
