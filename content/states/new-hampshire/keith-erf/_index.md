Keith Erf is a member of the Republican Party and currently serves in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 28. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Erf ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 28 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. His career experience includes owning a business.

In terms of committee assignments, Erf has been assigned to several committees over the years:

- 2023-2024: House Finance Committee, Vice Chair Finance - Division III Committee
- 2021-2022: House Finance Committee, Finance - Division III Committee, Vice-Chair
- 2019-2020: Finance - Division III Committee, House Finance Committee

Erf has also sponsored several legislations as a legislator. However, due to an error, the list of bills he sponsored could not be retrieved.

In the 2022 elections, Erf and incumbent Leah Cushman defeated William Politt and David Erikson in the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Hillsborough 28 on November 8, 2022. Erf received 30.4% of the votes, amounting to 2,342 votes.

Before his tenure in the House of Representatives, Erf served on the Weare Finance Committee. He is also a business owner, which adds to his diverse experience and understanding of the economic landscape.

As a representative, Erf is committed to serving his constituents and making a positive impact in his community. His dedication to public service is evident in his active participation in various committees and his efforts to sponsor meaningful legislation.
