Seth King is an American politician who serves as a Republican member for the Coos 4th district of the New Hampshire House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022.

King is known for his views on certain social issues. For instance, he opposes gender affirming care to transgender youth under the age of 18. He argues that children are unable to consent, may later regret the decision, and that irreversible surgeries or drugs may have negative long-term health problems.

In terms of his election history, King ran unopposed in the primary election. He successfully defeated Democrat Suzy Colt, winning 54.9% of the votes on November 8, 2022.

Beyond his political career, King has a diverse background. He has experience in financial technology and is a world traveler. He is also a life-long libertarian activist, a devourer of books, and a veteran debater. 

King's educational background includes majoring in Physics. He studied at San Diego State University, University of Stuttgart, and University of Maryland College Park.

He is currently married and resides in Whitefield, New Hampshire.
