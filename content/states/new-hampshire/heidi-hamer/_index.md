Heidi Hamer is a member of the Democratic Party and currently serves in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, representing Hillsborough 19. She assumed office on December 7, 2022, and her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Before her current tenure, she served in the New Hampshire House of Representatives for Hillsborough 17. Her service in this position ended on December 7, 2022. She ran for re-election to the New Hampshire House of Representatives to represent Hillsborough 19 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Heidi Hamer is a lifelong resident of Manchester's Ward 10 and has been an active member of the Moore Center Family Support Council. She has also served as a Manchester Ward 10 election official since 2014. In addition to her political career, she is married to Gary Hamer, a member of the Manchester Board of School Committee.

In terms of her educational background, Hamer graduated from Manchester High School West in 1977. During her time in the New Hampshire House of Representatives, she has served on the Election Law Committee for four years.

In the general election for New Hampshire House of Representatives Hillsborough 19 held on November 8, 2022, Heidi Hamer secured 27.0% of the votes, amounting to 1,471 votes, and emerged victorious. Her fellow Democratic Party member, Jane Beaulieu, also won with 26.2% of the votes, amounting to 1,425 votes.

Heidi Hamer's commitment to her community and her dedication to public service are evident in her work. She continues to serve her constituents with integrity and passion.
