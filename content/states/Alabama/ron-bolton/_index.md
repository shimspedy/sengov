Ron Bolton is an American politician who has served as a member of the Alabama House of Representatives since November 8, 2022. He represents Alabama's 61st House district. He was elected on November 8, 2022, in the 2022 Alabama House of Representatives election against Libertarian opponent Damon Pruet. He assumed office the next day on November 9, 2022.

Bolton is a retired police officer. He retired in 2011. He used to work at Northport Police Department. He is a Baptist. He graduated from Holt High School, and obtained an Associate degree from Shelton State Community College, and attended University of Alabama.

Bolton's career experience includes working as a captain with the Northport Police Department and the managing principal of The Warwick Group LLC. Bolton has served as the camp secretary of Gideons International and on the board of directors of Tuscaloosa Shooting Sports Inc.

In the general election for Alabama House of Representatives District 61, Ron Bolton defeated Damon Pruet. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022. His current term ends on November 4, 2026.
