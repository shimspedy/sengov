Chris Sells is a member of the Alabama House of Representatives, representing District 90. He is a member of the Republican Party. He assumed office on November 5, 2014, and his current term ends on November 4, 2026. He ran for re-election to the Alabama House of Representatives to represent District 90 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Chris Sells was born on December 6, 1963. He graduated from Vigor High School. After graduation, he moved to Greenville in 1990 and founded Sells Medical Equipment. He is a successful small businessman who has owned Sells Medical Equipment since 2008.

In terms of his political career, Sells has been active in various committees throughout his tenure. In the 2023-2024 legislative session, Sells was assigned to the Health Committee, Montgomery County Legislation Committee, House Rules Committee, State Government Committee (where he served as Vice Chair), and Ways and Means General Fund Committee. In the previous sessions, he served on similar committees, demonstrating his commitment to these areas of legislation.

In the 2022 general election for Alabama House of Representatives District 90, incumbent Chris Sells won the election with 98.8% of the votes, totaling 11,567 votes.

Chris Sells is a husband and father with conservative values. He is pro-life and believes strongly in our 2nd Amendment. Four years ago, Chris was fed up with the nonsense in Montgomery and decided to do something about it.
