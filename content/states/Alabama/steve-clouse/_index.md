Steve Clouse is a member of the Alabama House of Representatives, representing District 93. He assumed office in 1994 and his current term ends on November 4, 2026. Clouse is affiliated with the Republican Party.

Born on February 7, 1956, Clouse has a professional background in business. He serves as the Vice President of the Clouse Marketing Company. His educational credentials include a Bachelor's degree from the University of Alabama.

Over the years, Clouse has been assigned to several committees. For the 2023-2024 term, he was assigned to the Economic Development and Tourism Committee, Financial Services Committee, Military and Veterans' Affairs Committee, Ways and Means General Fund Committee (as Chair), and the Reapportionment Committee. In the past, he has also served on the Permanent Joint Legislative Advisory Committee on Economic Incentives, Contract Review Legislative Oversight Committee, and Energy Policy Committee.

Clouse ran for re-election to the Alabama House of Representatives to represent District 93 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. His base salary as a legislator is $53,913 per year.

In addition to his political career, Clouse is a devout Christian and identifies as a Methodist.
