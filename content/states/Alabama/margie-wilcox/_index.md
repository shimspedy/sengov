Margie Wilcox is a member of the Alabama House of Representatives, representing District 104. She is a member of the Republican Party. She assumed office on February 5, 2014, and her current term ends on November 4, 2026. She ran for re-election to the Alabama House of Representatives to represent District 104 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Margie Wilcox's professional experience includes owning Mobile Bay Transportation and Pensacola Bay Transportation. She has been in the position for over 10 years and receives a base salary of $53,913/year. No per diem is paid to legislators whose permanent residence is less than six hours away.

Throughout her tenure, Wilcox has been assigned to various committees. In the 2023-2024 term, she was assigned to the Boards, Agencies and Commissions Committee (as Chair), Mobile County Legislation Committee, Transportation, Utilities and Infrastructure Committee, Urban and Rural Development Committee, and License Plates Legislative Oversight Committee.

In the 2021-2022 term, she was assigned to the Transportation Committee, House County and Municipal Government Committee (as Vice Chair), Transportation, Utilities and Infrastructure Committee, Urban and Rural Development Committee, and Mobile County Legislation Committee.

In the 2019-2020 term, she was assigned to the House County and Municipal Government Committee (as Vice chair), Mobile County Legislation Committee, Transportation, Utilities and Infrastructure Committee, and Urban and Rural Development Committee.

At the beginning of the 2015 legislative session, Wilcox served on the Children and Senior Advocacy, Economic Development and Tourism, Mobile County Legislation, and Transportation, Utilities and Infrastructure committees.

Margie Wilcox is a longtime small business owner who began her career in transportation right out of high school as a bookkeeper at a local company in 1977. She worked her way to management and bought a division of the company in 1992. She was born and raised in Theodore, Alabama, and graduated from Theodore High School in 1980.
