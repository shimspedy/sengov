Leigh Hulsey is a member of the Alabama House of Representatives, representing District 15. She assumed office on November 9, 2022, and her current term ends on November 4, 2026. Hulsey ran for election to the Alabama House of Representatives to represent District 15 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Hulsey's career experience includes owning CrossFit Alabaster and Henley Developments, LLC. She has served as the president of the Alabaster Business Alliance, the vice president of campaigns of the Republican Women of North Shelby County, and an executive committee member of the Shelby County GOP.

In the 2022 elections, Leigh Hulsey defeated Richard Rouco in the general election for Alabama House of Representatives District 15. She also defeated Brad Tompkins in the Republican primary for Alabama House of Representatives District 15 on May 24, 2022.

Hulsey was assigned to the following committees in 2023-2024: Ethics and Campaign Finance Committee. She is a life-long active resident of Alabama and a champion of conservative causes. She is also a member of the Helena city council.

Hulsey is a Christian and a business owner by profession. She is a member of the Republican Party and her base salary as a legislator is $53,913/year. No per diem is paid to legislators whose permanent residence is less than six hours away. Legislators who are 6-12 hrs from their permanent residence receive $12.75/day. Legislators who are over 12 hours away with no overnight receive $34/day.
