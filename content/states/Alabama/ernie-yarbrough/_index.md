Ernie Yarbrough is an American politician from the state of Alabama. He currently serves as a member of the Alabama House of Representatives for District 7. He is a member of the Republican Party.

Ernie Yarbrough was born on August 30, 1981. Prior to Yarbrough's tenure in the Alabama House of Representatives, Yarbrough was raised in rural Lawrence County, Alabama. He received a degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Alabama in Huntsville and worked for 10 years at Nucor Steel in Decatur, Alabama.

He assumed office on November 9, 2022. His current term ends on November 4, 2026. Yarbrough ran for election to the Alabama House of Representatives to represent District 7. He won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

In the 2022 Republican Primary Election, Ernie Yarbrough received 4,303 votes, which was 54.2% of the total votes. His opponent, Proncey Robertson (Inc.), received 3,631 votes, which was 45.8% of the total votes.

In the 2022 General Election, Ernie Yarbrough received 11,225 votes, which was 76.5% of the total votes.
