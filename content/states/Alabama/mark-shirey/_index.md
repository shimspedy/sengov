Mark Shirey is an American politician who has served as a member of the Alabama House of Representatives since November 8, 2022. He represents Alabama's 100th House district.

Born in DeKalb County, Alabama, Shirey is a member of the Republican Party. He is married to Mary and they have a daughter named Kate. The family resides in Mobile, Alabama.

Shirey has a rich educational background. He earned a bachelor's degree in Medical Technology from the University of Alabama Birmingham, and later pursued a Doctorate in Optometry at Southern College of Optometry.

Before his political career, Shirey was a successful optometrist. He co-operated an optometry practice and worked as an optometrist for many years. He also served as the president of the Alabama Optometric Association. His dedication to his profession is evident in his ownership of an optometry service.

Shirey's political journey began when he ran for election to the Alabama House of Representatives to represent District 100. He won the general election on November 8, 2022, and assumed office the next day on November 9, 2022. His current term ends on November 4, 2026.

In the past, Shirey also ran for the Republican nomination for Alabama Senate district 34, but lost to Jack Williams. Despite the setback, he continued his political journey with determination.

Shirey's electoral history is noteworthy. In the 2022 Alabama House of Representatives election, he competed against Libertarian opponent Peyton Warren and emerged victorious. He assumed office the next day, marking the beginning of his tenure as a member of the Alabama House of Representatives.
