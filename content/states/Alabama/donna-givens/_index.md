Donna Givens is a member of the Alabama House of Representatives, representing District 64. She is a member of the Republican Party. She assumed office on November 9, 2022, and her current term ends on November 4, 2026.

Donna Givens ran for election to the Alabama House of Representatives to represent District 64. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Donna Givens graduated from Robertsdale High School. She furthered her education by earning a degree from Mobile College. Her career experience includes working with Baldwin EMC.

In addition to her professional career, Givens has served as the chair of the North Baldwin Chamber of Commerce and the Central Baldwin Chamber of Commerce.

In the 2022 elections, Donna Givens defeated Jeff May in the general election for Alabama House of Representatives District 64. She received 90.3% of the votes, totaling 11,041 votes.

In the Republican primary for Alabama House of Representatives District 64, Donna Givens defeated Angelo Jacob Fermo with 51.8% of the votes, totaling 3,129 votes.

Donna Givens has been assigned to the following committees for the 2023-2024 term: Boards, Agencies and Commissions Committee and Baldwin County Legislation Committee.
