Mike Kirkland is an American politician who has served as a member of the Alabama House of Representatives since 2022. He represents Alabama's 23rd House district since the election on November 8, 2022.

Born in Jackson County, Alabama, Kirkland graduated from Scottsboro High School. He furthered his education by obtaining a Bachelor of Arts from Athens State University and a Master of Business Administration from the University of North Alabama.

In addition to his political career, Kirkland has had a successful professional career. He has worked at a company called Vulcan Materials for 30 years, holding various positions such as an area sales manager, a district sales manager, and a senior sales representative.

Kirkland is a family man. He and his wife Pamela are Baptists. They are proud parents of two children, Erica and Michael Jr., and are blessed with three grandchildren.

Kirkland's dedication to his community extends beyond his political and professional life. He served as the president of the Northeast Alabama Community College Foundation Board of Directors, the Top of Alabama Regional Council of Governments (TARCOG), and the Scottsboro Rotary Club.

His tenure as a member of the Alabama House of Representatives began on November 9, 2022, and his current term ends on November 4, 2026. He has been serving his constituents diligently, and his work continues to impact the lives of the people in Alabama's 23rd House district.
