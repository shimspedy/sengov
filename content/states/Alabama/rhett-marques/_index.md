Rhett Marques is a member of the Alabama House of Representatives, representing District 91. He assumed office on November 7, 2018, and his current term ends on November 4, 2026. He is a member of the Republican Party.

Marques was born and raised in Spanish Fort, Alabama. After graduating from Fairhope High School in 1990, he earned a Bachelor of Science degree in marketing from the University of Alabama in 1994.

In his professional career, Marques served as the owner and manager of Goodson Tire & Auto. His business acumen and leadership skills were instrumental in his successful election to the Alabama House of Representatives in 2018.

During his tenure, Marques has been assigned to several committees. In the 2023-2024 term, he served as the Vice Chair of the Commerce and Small Business Committee and was also a member of the Health Committee. In the 2021-2022 term, he was assigned to the Commerce and Small Business Committee, Fiscal Responsibility Committee, and Health Committee. Similarly, in the 2019-2020 term, he was assigned to the Commerce and Small Business Committee, Health Committee, and Fiscal Responsibility Committee.

Marques ran for re-election to the Alabama House of Representatives to represent District 91 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. His dedication to his constituents and commitment to public service continue to guide his work in the Alabama House of Representatives.
