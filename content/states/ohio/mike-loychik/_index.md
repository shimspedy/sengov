State Representative Mike Loychik is serving his second term in the Ohio House of Representatives. The 65th House District encompasses nearly all of Trumbull and Ashtabula Counties. Along with his various committee assignments, Loychik also serves as Co-Chair of the Sportsmen's Caucus for Ohio.

Loychik is a committed public servant having dedicated eight years of his life to the United States Air Force. He was a C-130 Crew Chief with a special operations group, as well as a combat search and rescue group. He retired as a Staff Sergeant.

Mike works as a small business owner and operates Atlantic Pressure Washing Solutions LLC, which offers professional pressure washing and soft washing services. Recently, he was also appointed to Trumbull County Executive Committee Member at Large.

Mike previously graduated from Champion High School before earning an associate degree in aviation and maintenance technology at Community College of the Air Force. Subsequently, he earned a Bachelor of Applied Science in Environmental Management at Columbia Southern University.
