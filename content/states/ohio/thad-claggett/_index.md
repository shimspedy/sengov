State Representative Thad Claggett is serving his first term in the Ohio House of Representatives. He represents the 68th Ohio House District, which encompasses the central and western region of Licking County.

Claggett is a Civil Engineer and President at Claggett & Sons, Inc., a fourth-generation commercial and industrial construction contractor in Newark.  

Claggett is also very involved in his community. Currently, he serves as President of the Licking County Library Board, a director of the area action council for the National Federation of Independent Business (NFIB Ohio), and a board member of the Central Ohio and Ohio chapters of the Associated Builders and Contractors.
