State Representative Gary Click is currently serving his second term in the Ohio House of Representatives. He represents the 88th House District, which encompasses all of Sandusky and Seneca Counties.

In 2006, Click accepted the position of pastor to the Fremont Baptist Temple and has held that position ever since. In over thirty years of ministry, Pastor Click has served in many aspects including youth ministry, bus ministry, writing, radio, Christian education, and lobbying Congress on behalf of churches and Christian schools. He has also served as a chaplain for law enforcement and in hospice care.

Representative Click earned a Bachelor of Religious Education from Midwestern Baptist College in 1990.  It was there that he met and married his wife Nanette. They are the proud parents of four boys: Nathaneil, Zachary, Garrison, and Micah and are the proud grandparents of four.

Pastor Click and his wife reside in Vickery, Ohio.
