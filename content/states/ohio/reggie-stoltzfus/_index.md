State Representative Reggie Stoltzfus is currently serving his third term in the Ohio House of Representatives. He represents the 50th House District, which includes portions of Stark County.

Representative Stoltzfus was born and raised in Hartville, a community of Stark County. Along with his wife of 20 years, Jennette, Rep. Stoltzfus owns and operates several local businesses, and his family attends church at Grace Chapel in Minerva.

In addition to his personal understanding of growing and supporting a healthy small business sector, Rep. Stoltzfus is actively involved in various community organizations, including being elected as former Paris Township Trustee and Board president, serving as a member and past president of the Minerva Area Chamber of Commerce, organizing the Paris Township cleanup day for several years.

Rep. Stoltzfus is also a member of the Stark County Township Association, National Rifle Association, Stark County Farm Bureau, and Right to Life (Stark County).

A proud graduate of Lake High School, Rep. Stoltzfus also attended Malone University where he studied Political Science. Rep. Stoltzfus resides on a small farm in Paris Township with his wife and four children.
