Marc T. Lombardo is an American politician who represents the 22nd Middlesex District in the Massachusetts House of Representatives. He is a member of the Republican Party and has been serving since 2011.

Before his tenure in the House of Representatives, Lombardo was a member of the Billerica, Massachusetts Board of Selectmen from 2005 to 2010, and a Billerica town meeting member from 2004 to 2010.

In terms of education, Lombardo earned his B.S. in business administration from Merrimack College and has attended the MBA program at the University of Massachusetts, Lowell. Professionally, he has experience working as a sales account manager at Saba Software.

His current term in the Massachusetts House of Representatives is set to end on January 1, 2025.
