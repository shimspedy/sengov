Bill MacGregor is a member of the Democratic Party and currently serves in the Massachusetts House of Representatives, representing the 10th Suffolk District. He assumed office on June 7, 2023, and his current term ends on January 1, 2025.

MacGregor ran in a special election to the Massachusetts House of Representatives to represent the 10th Suffolk District and won in the special general election on May 30, 2023.

He graduated from Catholic Memorial High School in 1997 and earned a B.A. in economics from Assumption University in 2001. His career experience includes working as a loan officer with CrossCountry Mortgage, LLC, the chief of staff to former Boston City Councilman Matt O'Malley, and a residential leasing associate with Boston Realty Advisors. MacGregor also served on the Roslindale Parade Committee.

In the special general election for Massachusetts House of Representatives 10th Suffolk District, MacGregor defeated Robert Orthman. He received 93.1% of the votes, totaling 2,579 votes.
