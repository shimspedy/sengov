Rep. Mark Perry is serving his sixth term and previously served in the Arkansas House for the 2009, 2011, and 2013 terms. He represents District 66 which includes portions of Pulaski County.

For the 94th General Assembly, Rep. Perry is the House Vice Chair for the Joint Committee on Public Retirement and Social Security Programs. Rep. Perry also serves on the House Public Health, Welfare, and Labor Committee and the House Insurance and Commerce Committee. Rep. Perry also serves on the Joint Budget Committee.

Rep. Perry is a Financial Representative for Modern Woodmen Fraternal Financial. He graduated from the University of Central Arkansas with a B.B.A. in Marketing. Rep. Perry has been involved with multiple civic and professional organizations including serving as the past president at the Jacksonville Chamber of Commerce, the past president for Jackson Rotary Club, a past president at Jacksonville Boys & Girls Club and the Jacksonville Sertoma Club.
