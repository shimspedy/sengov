State Representative Chris Aniskovich represents the 35th District, serving the towns of Clinton, Killingworth, and Westbrook in the Connecticut General Assembly. In his first term in office, Rep. Aniskovich will serve on the legislatures Banking, Commerce, and General Law Committees.

For the past twenty years, Chris has lived in Clinton with his two children. During that time, he’s maintained an active role in the community, serving as the President of the Clinton Youth Football, volunteer coach and board member of the Harry Swan Little League, and volunteer coach in the town’s youth basketball program.

Since 2019, he has served as the Chair of the Clinton Town Council, having been reelected to the position after the 2021 election. Prior to that, Chris was a member of the Clinton Planning and Zoning Commission and Board of Assessment Appeals.

He graduated with a BS in Business Administration from Merrimack College. He is a mortgage broker/owner of Security Financial Mortgage Corp., LLC. in Clinton.

Rep. Aniskovich’s legislative focus includes making Connecticut more affordable, improving public safety, protecting the shoreline, supporting costal resiliency initiatives, and retaining local zoning/educational control.
