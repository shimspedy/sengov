As a lifelong Connecticut resident, Tom Arnone is proud to represent Enfield’s 58th District. For the 2023 Legislative Session, he will serve on the Committee on Children, the Executive & Legislative Nominations Committee, and the Regulations Review Committee. Additionally, Representative Arnone was appointed as Assistant Majority Whip.

Before his role in the Connecticut General Assembly, Tom served four terms on the Enfield Town Council, where he was committed to improving opportunities for children. Arnone was also a member of the Board of Education and Finance Committees.

Representative Arnone is the Founder and President of Kids First, a nonprofit charity dedicated to helping at-risk youth with after-school activities and filming community events. The organization also facilitated the renovation of the youth center kitchen to provide cooking and nutrition classes.

Arnone pursued his higher education through the distance learning program at California State University, Sacramento College of Engineering and Computer Science.

Above all else, Arnone is a devoted husband, father, and grandfather.
