State Representative Farley Santos is proud and privileged to serve Danbury’s 109th District in the Connecticut General Assembly.

He attended Danbury’s public schools and graduated from Henry Abbott Technical High School. He was first elected to the Danbury City Council, representing the 4th Ward in 2019. Representative Santos previously served on the Danbury Board of Education and has over a decade of private-sector and community-driven experience. He has served on the Board of Directors of several nonprofit organizations, including the Danbury Student and Business Connection and Junior Achievement of Greater Fairfield County. Representative Santos recently served as President of the Danbury Police Activities League and is also President of the Landon Jack Verissimo Santos Memorial Scholarship, Inc.

He is currently Vice President, Community Development Manager, and Bank Ambassador with Savings Bank of Danbury. Representative Santos lives in Danbury, and together, he and his wife Kathleen are raising their daughter, Khloë.

For the 2023 Legislative Session, Representative Santos was proud to be appointed as Assistant Majority Leader and Vice Chair of the Banking Committee. Additionally, he serves as a member of the Veterans and Military Affairs Committee, and member of the General Law Committee.
