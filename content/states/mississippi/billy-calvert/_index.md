Billy Adam Calvert is an American politician and businessman serving as a member of the Mississippi House of Representatives from the 83rd district. Elected in November 2019, he assumed office on January 7, 2020.

Calvert was born and raised in Meridian, Mississippi. He earned an associate degree from East Mississippi Community College and a Bachelor of Science degree in applied sciences from the University of Mississippi.

Career:
Calvert owns Southern Business Supply, an office supplies store. In addition to his political role, he actively contributes to economic development and infrastructure initiatives. His commitment to fostering growth and improving the quality of life for Mississippians has been evident throughout his tenure.

Representative Calvert is a member of the NRA and Gun Owners of America. He is also affiliated with the University of Mississippi's Mclub. His dedication to public service extends beyond legislative duties, as he actively engages with constituents and advocates for their interests.

Personal Life:
Calvert is married to Jessica Courvelle, and they share a strong Christian faith. Their commitment to family values and community well-being underscores his approach to governance.

As a legislator, Calvert continues to champion policies that promote economic prosperity, education, and public safety. His vision for Mississippi centers on sustainable growth, innovation, and collaboration across party lines.

Billy Adam Calvert's contributions extend beyond the political arena, making him a respected figure in both business and public service. His leadership exemplifies the values of integrity, hard work, and dedication to the betterment of his constituents and the state as a whole.
