Andrew Stepp is an American politician who currently represents the 23rd district in the Mississippi House of Representatives. He was elected in 2023 and is a member of the Republican Party.

Andrew Stepp lives in Bruce, Mississippi. His career experience includes working as a pharmacist and small business owner of Stepp-Saver Pharmacy in Bruce. He also serves as a clinical instructor in pharmacy practice at the University of Mississippi.

Stepp ran for election to the Mississippi House of Representatives to represent District 23. He won in the general election on November 7, 2023. Prior to this, Stepp also ran in a special election to the Mississippi House of Representatives to represent District 23. He lost in the special general runoff election on January 31, 2023.

In the 2023 regular election, Andrew Stepp defeated Danny Lampley and Andy Clark in the general election for Mississippi House of Representatives District 23. He received 71.9% of the votes.

Stepp's personal life includes his residence in Bruce, Mississippi. He has 2 daughters and 5 grandchildren. He is a member of the First Baptist Church of Bruce.
