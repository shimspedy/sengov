Kenji Holloway

Kenji Holloway is a member of the Mississippi House of Representatives, representing District 27. He is affiliated with the Democratic Party. He assumed office on January 2, 2024, and his current term ends on January 4, 2028.

Early Life and Career

Kenji Holloway was born on December 23, 1978. Before his political career, he worked as an area manager with the Vincit Group. His career experience includes various roles in management and leadership.

Political Career

Kenji Holloway ran for election to the Mississippi House of Representatives to represent District 27. He won in the general election on November 7, 2023. He assumed office on January 2, 2024. His current term ends on January 4, 2028.

Elections

In the 2023 general election for Mississippi House of Representatives District 27, Kenji Holloway won the election. He received 100.0% of the votes, totaling 6,538 votes.

In the Democratic primary for Mississippi House of Representatives District 27, Kenji Holloway defeated James Woods on August 8, 2023. He received 50.5% of the votes, totaling 1,470 votes.

Personal Life

Kenji Holloway is married to Temeka Townsend. They reside in Carthage, Mississippi.
