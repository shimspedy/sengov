Jim Estrada - A Detailed Biography

Jim Estrada is a member of the Mississippi House of Representatives, representing District 33. He is affiliated with the Republican Party and has been serving in this position since January 2, 2024.

Born in Biloxi, Mississippi, Estrada has a strong educational background. He earned his bachelor's degree from Mississippi College in 1984 and later pursued a graduate degree from LSU in 2000.

Before entering politics, Estrada had a successful career as a bank executive. His professional experience in the banking sector has undoubtedly contributed to his understanding of financial matters, which is a valuable asset in his role as a representative.

Estrada ran for election to the Mississippi House of Representatives to represent District 33 and won in the general election held on November 7, 2023. His victory was a testament to his popularity and the trust that the people of District 33 have in him.

In the 2023 elections, Estrada faced off against Dennis Nowell and Jonathan Dantzler in the Republican primary for Mississippi House of Representatives District 33. He emerged victorious, securing 55.2% of the votes.

As a representative, Estrada is known for his dedication and commitment to serving the people of his district. He is focused on addressing the issues that matter most to his constituents and is always ready to listen to their concerns.

Estrada's current term ends on January 4, 2028. As he continues to serve in his role, he remains committed to upholding the values of the Republican Party and working towards the betterment of his district and the state of Mississippi.
