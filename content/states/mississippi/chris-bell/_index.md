Christopher M. "Chris" Bell (born August 13, 1970) is an American politician serving as a member of the Mississippi House of Representatives from the 65th district. He was elected in November 2015 and assumed office on January 5, 2016.

Early Life and Education:
Bell was born in Chicago, Illinois, on August 13, 1970. He earned a Bachelor of Science degree in criminal justice from Jackson State University.

Career:
Outside of politics, Bell works in the insurance industry. He was elected to the Mississippi House of Representatives in November 2015 and assumed office on January 5, 2016. Bell also serves as vice chair of the House Executive Contingent Fund Committee.

In addition to his political career, Bell has been actively involved in various committees throughout his tenure. These include the Banking and Financial Services Committee, House Energy Committee, House Executive Contingent Fund Committee, House Insurance Committee, Public Health and Human Services Committee, and House Tourism Committee.

Re-election:
Bell ran for re-election to the Mississippi House of Representatives to represent District 65. He won in the general election on November 7, 2023. His current term ends on January 4, 2028.

Personal Details:
Bell is a member of the Democratic Party. He was first elected to the chamber in 2015. His base salary as a member of the Mississippi House of Representatives is $23,500/year with a per diem of $157/day.
