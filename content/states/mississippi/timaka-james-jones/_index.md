Timaka James-Jones is a member of the Mississippi House of Representatives, representing District 51. She assumed office on January 2, 2024, and her current term ends on January 4, 2028. She ran for election to the Mississippi House of Representatives to represent District 51 and won in the general election on November 7, 2023.

Born in Mississippi, Timaka James-Jones earned a bachelor's degree from Jackson State University in 1995. Her career experience includes serving on the Humphreys County Justice Court and as Humphreys County Circuit Court Clerk & County Registrar.
