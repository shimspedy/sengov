Donald A. Guardian (born June 12, 1953) is an American Republican Party politician who has represented the 2nd Legislative District in the New Jersey General Assembly since taking office on January 11, 2022. His historic achievement as the first openly gay Republican legislator in the state underscores his commitment to public service and advocacy.

Early Life and Education:
Raised in Palisades Park, New Jersey, and West New York, Guardian graduated from Don Bosco Preparatory High School. In 1975, he earned his bachelor's degree in history from Upsala College in East Orange. His educational background laid the foundation for his future career in politics and public administration.

Career Path:
Before entering politics, Guardian held various roles that contributed to his community and professional experience. Notably, he worked with the Boy Scouts of America until 1980. His commitment to youth development and community engagement shaped his values and approach to leadership.

Mayor of Atlantic City:
In 2013, Guardian announced his candidacy for mayor, challenging incumbent Lorenzo Langford. He secured the Republican primary unopposed and went on to defeat Langford in the general election. As Atlantic City's first openly gay mayor and the first Republican mayor since 1990, Guardian brought fresh perspectives and a dedication to revitalizing the city.

During his mayoral tenure from 2014 to 2017, Guardian focused on economic development, tourism, and community well-being. His leadership left a lasting impact on Atlantic City's trajectory.

Legislative Role:
Guardian's commitment to public service continued when he won the 2021 New Jersey General Assembly election alongside fellow Republican Claire Swift. Their victory marked a significant milestone, as Guardian became the first openly gay Republican legislator in the state's history. His dedication to representing the 2nd Legislative District reflects his passion for effective governance and positive change.

Committees:
As an assemblyman, Guardian actively participates in legislative committees. His current assignments include:

1. **Environment and Solid Waste**
2. **Special Committee on Infrastructure and Natural Resources**
3. **Tourism, Gaming, and the Arts**

Donald A. Guardian's journey from mayor to legislator exemplifies his unwavering commitment to public service, inclusivity, and progress.
