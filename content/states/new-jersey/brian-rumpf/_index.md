His commitment to public service and dedication to his constituents have made him a respected figure in New Jersey politics.

## Early Life and Education

Rumpf earned his Bachelor of Arts degree in politics from the Catholic University of America in 1986. His passion for political science and governance led him to pursue a law degree, which he obtained from the Washington and Lee University School of Law in 1989. Armed with legal knowledge and a strong sense of civic duty, Rumpf embarked on a career that would shape the lives of many New Jersey residents.

## Political Career

### Assemblyman and Parliamentarian

Brian Rumpf's tenure as an assemblyman began in 2003, and he has consistently worked to represent the interests of his constituents. His commitment to bipartisan collaboration and effective governance has earned him respect from colleagues on both sides of the aisle. In January 2024, Rumpf assumed the role of parliamentarian, further solidifying his influence within the legislative body.

### Republican Policy Co-Chair

From 2014 to 2023, Rumpf served as the Republican policy co-chairman. His ability to bridge ideological gaps and find common ground has been instrumental in advancing meaningful legislation.
