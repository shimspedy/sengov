Joseph A. Lagana Biography

Joseph A. Lagana, born on December 15, 1978, is an American attorney and public servant. He has dedicated his career to advocating for his community and addressing critical issues. Let's delve into his background and accomplishments:

1. **Early Life and Education:**
   - Lagana was raised in Ridgefield, New Jersey, and graduated from Ridgefield Memorial High School.
   - He pursued higher education at Fordham University, where he earned a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in history in 2000.
   - Later, he attended Western Michigan University Cooley Law School, where he obtained his Juris Doctor (J.D.) degree in 2004.

2. **Legal Career:**
   - After completing his legal studies, Lagana served as a clerk to Superior Court Judge John A. Conte.
   - He then joined the law firm Chasan Leyner & Lamparello, where he became a partner.
   - His legal expertise spans various areas, including criminal law and civil litigation.

3. **Public Service and Political Journey:**
   - Lagana's commitment to public service led him to the Paramus borough council.
   - In 2011, he and his Democratic running mate defeated two incumbent Republican councilmembers, securing a majority for Democrats.
   - As a result, Lagana was named council president in January 2013.

4. **New Jersey General Assembly (2014-2018):**
   - In 2014, Lagana declared his candidacy for the 38th district in the New Jersey General Assembly.
   - He won the election and was sworn in on January 14, 2014.
   - During his tenure, Lagana served as the Assembly's deputy parliamentarian from 2016 to 2017.

5. **New Jersey Senate (2018-present):**
   - Following Robert M. Gordon's resignation in April 2018, Lagana was appointed to the New Jersey Senate.
   - He won a special election on November 6, 2018, to complete Gordon's unexpired term.
   - Lagana's legislative efforts have focused on health care access, public safety, and combating the opioid epidemic.

6. **Committee Assignments:**
   - In the 2023-2024 legislative session, Lagana serves as the chair of the Senate Labor Committee and is a member of the Senate Judiciary Committee.
   - His dedication to improving labor policies and ensuring justice underscores his commitment to constituents.

7. **Elections:**
   - In the 2023 general election, Joseph Lagana secured re-election to the New Jersey State Senate, representing District 38.

Joseph Lagana continues to be a tireless advocate for his community, working diligently to address critical issues and improve the lives of New Jersey residents.
