Roger Albert is a Republican politician from Maine, who serves as the state representative for District 2. He was elected to the Maine House of Representatives in 2022, defeating Democrat Bernard Paradis in the general election. He is a member of the Transportation Committee.

Albert is a retired veteran who served in the Maine Army National Guard. He lives in Madawaska, Maine, with his wife Jacqueline. He has three grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.

Albert is passionate about improving the infrastructure, economy, and quality of life in his district. He supports lower taxes, less regulation, and more local control. He is also a strong advocate for veterans, seniors, and rural communities.
