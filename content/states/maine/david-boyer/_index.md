Representative David Boyer is presently in his inaugural term in the Maine House, representing District 87, which includes the town of Mechanic Falls and part of Poland. He actively serves on the 131st Legislature’s Joint Standing Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs.

In addition to his legislative responsibilities, Rep. Boyer is an eagle scout and operates an antique dealership. He is affiliated with various organizations, including the Sportsman’s Alliance of Maine, Maine Organic Farmers and Gardeners, the Androscoggin Land Trust, and Ellis’s Legacy Animal Foundation.

David and his spouse, Sarah, are parents to two children. During his leisure hours, David finds enjoyment in outdoor activities such as foraging, hiking, and skiing. He also has an appreciation for attending live music events.
