Representative Randall “Randy” Greenwood, a resident of Wales, is in his third term of service for House District 56, which encompasses the towns of Monmouth, Wales, and Litchfield. He holds the position of Rankin Member on the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local Government for the 131st Legislature. This is his third term on the committee and his second as the Republican Lead.

Randy is an alumnus of the University of Southern Maine, where he earned his Bachelor of Arts degree in Business Management, Leadership, and Organizational Studies. His professional journey includes owning Mixers Nightclub & Lounge and serving as the General Manager of Elizabeth Ann. He has also served as an Androscoggin County Commissioner for eight years. Currently, he manages a small business and serves as a Selectman for the Town of Wales.

In addition to his political and professional roles, Randy is deeply involved in his local community. He serves as the Treasurer for the Pleasant Hill Cemetery Association and is a member of the Sabattus Community Baptist Church. He has previously been a part of the Sabattus Recreation Club and has coached both baseball and soccer.

Randy is a father to three children: Adam, Isaac, and Gabby. Raised on a dairy farm with 100 heads, he now enjoys tending to his own goat and chicken farm. An outdoor enthusiast, Randy enjoys target shooting, hunting, and ice fishing.
