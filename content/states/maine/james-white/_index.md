James White is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 30 in the Maine House of Representatives. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Before his political career, White served in the U.S. Navy and the U.S. Navy Reserves1. His career experience includes working as a gunsmith. He ran for election to the Maine House of Representatives to represent District 30 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

In the 2023-2024 legislative session, White was assigned to the Transportation Committee1. He defeated W. Louis Sidell in the general election for Maine House of Representatives District 30.

His contact details are available on the official website and his campaign is also active on Facebook1. He resides at 306 Wharff Road, Guilford, ME 044432. He is married to Tricia and they have three children and two grandchildren. His occupation is listed as a gunsmith.
