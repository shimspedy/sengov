Anne Perry is a Democratic politician from Maine, who serves as the state representative for District 9. She assumed office on December 7, 2022. Her current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Perry is a retired family nurse practitioner who worked at Calais Regional Medical Services. She holds a bachelor’s degree in nursing from the University of Southern Maine and a master’s in nursing from Husson University. She is a member of the St. Croix Area Rotary Club, a board member of Northern Light Health Systems, and a past president of the Maine Nurse Practitioner Association.

Perry serves as the House chair of the Health Coverage, Insurance and Financial Services Committee. She previously served in the Maine Legislature from 2003-2010, and was House chair of the Joint Standing Committees on Insurance and Financial Services and Health and Human Services. She is passionate about health care reform, prescription drug affordability, and rural development.

Perry lives in Calais, Maine, with her husband. She has three children, nine grandchildren, and one great-grandchild.
