Representative Randall "Randy" Hall, based in East Dixfield, is currently in his third term representing House District 74, which encompasses a diverse range of towns including Industry, New Vineyard, Strong, Wilton, Avon, Carthage, Temple, Weld, Perkins Township, Washington Township, New Portland, and South Franklin. Continuing his dedicated service, he remains a member of the 131st Legislature's Joint Standing Committee on Agriculture, Conservation, and Forestry, a role he has fulfilled for the two preceding legislative terms.

Rep. Hall holds a Bachelor's degree from the University of New Hampshire and has established himself as a seasoned farmer, specializing in dairy, beef, and maple syrup production. Throughout his career, he has been affiliated with the U.S. Belted Galloway Society, achieving notable success with one national championship and two reserve national championships. Beyond his legislative duties, Randy has diligently served his community as the Fire Chief of East Dixfield for the past 25 years.
