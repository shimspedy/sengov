Thomas Lavigne is a member of the Republican Party serving in the Maine House of Representatives for District 148. He began his term on December 7, 2022, and it is set to conclude on December 4, 2024. Lavigne’s journey into politics saw him winning the general election held on November 8, 2022.

Residing in Berwick, Maine, Lavigne’s professional background includes being a business owner and the principal broker at Keay Brook Associates. His legislative focus is channeled through his assignment to the Taxation Committee.

As a state representative, Lavigne represents the interests of his constituents from Berwick and parts of North Berwick. His commitment to public service is reflected in his active engagement with legislative processes and community issues. Lavigne’s role in the House Minority Office and his expertise as a real estate broker contribute to his multifaceted approach to governance and policy-making.
