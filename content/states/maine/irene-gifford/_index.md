Irene Gifford is a Republican politician from Maine, who serves as the state representative for District 28. She was elected in 2022, defeating Democrat Natalie DiPentino with 70.9% of the vote. She is a member of the House Engrossed Bills Committee and the Marine Resources Committee. She lives in Lincoln, Maine, and works as a hairdresser. She has sponsored or co-sponsored bills on topics such as firearms, parental liability, and education. She is a newcomer to politics and has no prior legislative experience.
