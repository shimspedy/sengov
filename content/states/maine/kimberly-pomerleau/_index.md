Representative Kimberly Pomerleau is in her inaugural term representing House District 85, which includes part of Standish. Within the 131st Legislature, she actively contributes to the Joint Standing Committee on State and Local Government.

Beyond her legislative role, Rep. Pomerleau is a small businesswoman, formerly owning Maine-ly Heating Oil, and presently overseeing Roadway Utility Transfers MFG in Standish.

Kimberly and her husband, Phil, share a family with two children, seven grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren. In their leisure time, they find joy in hiking and engaging in volunteer work in their hometown. An avid cyclist, Kimberly has even participated in the Trek Across Maine.
