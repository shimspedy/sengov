Rep. Julie McCabe is serving her first term in the Maine House of Representatives, representing District 93. She assumed office on December 3, 2024. Her current term ends on December 1, 2026. She is a member of the Health and Human Services Committee.

McCabe holds a bachelor’s degree from Bates College and a master’s degree in government from Johns Hopkins University.

She is a high school teacher in Lewiston and has taught in Maine’s public schools for the past 12 years. McCabe has also served on the Recycling and Waste Management Committee for the City of Lewiston and volunteers as a youth sports coach.

She lives in Lewiston with her husband, Matt, and their two children.
