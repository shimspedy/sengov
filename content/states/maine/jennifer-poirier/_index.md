Representative Jennifer Poirier of Skowhegan is currently in her second term, representing House District 70, which comprises the town of Skowhegan. Within the 131st Legislature, she holds a crucial role as the Ranking Member on the Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary.

Jennifer pursued Business Management at Thomas College and currently serves as a Community Living Coordinator, dedicating her efforts to assisting adults with intellectual disabilities. With a rich history of community engagement, she served on the MSAD 54 school board for an impressive 12 years. Over the span of 25 years, Jen has actively participated in various community initiatives, notably organizing numerous fundraisers for the local animal shelter and food cupboard.

In her personal life, Jen shares her world with her husband, Sean, and their seven sons. During her downtime, she finds joy in outdoor activities such as fly fishing, hunting, hiking, kayaking, and spending time at camp. Jennifer's vibrant life is enriched by her connections with friends, family, and pets, showcasing a deep commitment to both legislative responsibilities and the well-being of her community.
