Walter Riseman is an independent member of the Maine House of Representatives, representing District 83. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term ends on December 4, 2024. Riseman ran for re-election to the Maine House of Representatives to represent District 83 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Born in Norwood, Massachusetts, Riseman now resides in Harrison, Maine. He earned a B.B.A. in accounting and finance from Babson College in Wellesley, Massachusetts, in 1970. His career experience includes working as a staff accountant for Hannaford Brothers, owning his own business, and working for a nonprofit organization as a small business development specialist and later as an accounting manager, controller, director of finance, and chief financial officer.

Riseman has been assigned to the State and Local Government Committee and Veterans and Legal Affairs Committee during his tenure. He has also sponsored several pieces of legislation, although the details of these bills are not available.

In the 2022 general election for Maine House of Representatives District 83, Riseman defeated Donna Dodge, securing 52.0% of the votes.
