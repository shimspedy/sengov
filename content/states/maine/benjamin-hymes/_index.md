Benjamin Hymes is a Republican politician who serves as a member of the Maine House of Representatives for District 38. He was elected to his first term in November 2022, defeating two other candidates in the general election. He is a member of the Veterans and Legal Affairs Committee.

Hymes lives in Waldo, Maine, with his wife. He is a veteran of the U.S. Navy, where he served for 20 years on active duty. He is also a small business owner. He has not completed the Ballotpedia Candidate Connection survey, which provides more information about his personal and political views.
