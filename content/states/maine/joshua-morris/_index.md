Representative Josh Morris from Turner is currently in his third term, representing House District 91, encompassing the towns of Leeds, Turner, and Wayne. Within the 131st Legislature, he holds the position of Ranking Member on the Joint Standing Committee on Health Coverage, Insurance, and Financial Services, serving his third term on this committee. Additionally, he is a member of the Joint Standing Committee on Housing.

Having graduated from Leavitt Area High School and the University of Southern Maine with a degree in Political Science, Rep. Morris is a realtor in his hometown of Turner. He is affiliated with the Maine Association of Realtors and the Nezinscot Lodge in Turner.

In his leisure time, Josh finds enjoyment in playing basketball, reading, and taking leisurely walks with his dog.
