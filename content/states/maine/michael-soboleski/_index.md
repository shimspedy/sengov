Representative Michael Soboleski, hailing from Phillips, is presently in his inaugural term representing House District 73, encompassing sections of Franklin and Somerset Counties. He contributes his expertise to the 131st Legislature's Joint Standing Committees on Environmental and Natural Resources, as well as Labor and Housing.

Having served as a corporal in the United States Marine Corps from 1974 to 1976 in legal administration, Mike brings a wealth of experience to his legislative role. Actively engaged in his community, he is a member of the American Legion, Maine Sportsman Alliance, Franklin County Republicans Committee, National Rifle Association, and Americans for Tax Reform. Representative Soboleski, a 9/11 Ground Zero responder, passionately advocates for the World Trade Center Health Program.

In his leisure time, Representative Soboleski relishes outdoor activities such as golf, hunting, and camping.
