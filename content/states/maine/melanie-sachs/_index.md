Melanie Sachs, a member of the Democratic Party, currently holds a position in the Maine House of Representatives, representing District 102. She commenced her term on December 7, 2022, with the current tenure scheduled to conclude on December 4, 2024.

Having sought re-election, Sachs emerged victorious in the general election held on November 8, 2022.

**Biography:**
Melanie Sachs was born in Morristown, New Jersey, and currently resides in Freeport, Maine. She is an alumna of Mt. Blue High School and holds a bachelor's degree in political science from Bates College (1991) as well as a master's degree in science and social administration from Case Western Reserve University (1994).

Sachs' professional journey includes serving as the executive director of Freeport Community Services. Her affiliations extend to various organizations such as the National Association of Perinatal Social Workers, the Mabel Wadsworth Women's Health Center, the Girl Scouts, the Boy Scouts, Odyssey of the Mind, Arts and Cultural Alliance of Freeport, Freeport Rotary, and Freeport Community Players.
