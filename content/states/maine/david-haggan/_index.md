David G. Haggan is a dedicated public servant and a member of the Maine State Senate, representing District 10. He assumed office on December 3, 2024, after winning the general election on November 5, 2024. His current term is set to end on December 1, 2026. Before his tenure in the State Senate, Haggan served in the Maine House of Representatives, representing District 101 and later District 36, following redistricting.

Born and raised in Hampden, Maine, Haggan has deep roots in his community. He earned a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Maine in 1989. His professional background includes a long career as a middle school social studies teacher, where he has influenced and shaped the minds of many young students.

Haggan's commitment to public service extends beyond his legislative duties. He has been actively involved in various community organizations, including the Anah Shrine Bangor, the NRA, and the Hampden Rifle and Pistol Club. His dedication to these organizations reflects his passion for community engagement and support.

Throughout his political career, Haggan has been assigned to several important committees. During the 2023-2024 legislative session, he served on the Leaves of Absence Committee and the Judiciary Committee. In previous sessions, he was also a member of the Marine Resources Committee and the Health Coverage, Insurance, and Financial Services Committee.

David Haggan's journey in politics began with his election to the Maine House of Representatives in 2016. He was re-elected in 2018 and 2020, demonstrating the trust and confidence his constituents have in his leadership. His transition to the Maine State Senate marks a new chapter in his political career, where he continues to advocate for the needs and interests of his district.

Haggan's personal life is equally fulfilling. He is married to Kimberly Haggan, and together they have two children. The family resides in Hampden, Maine, where they enjoy the close-knit community and the natural beauty of the region.

David G. Haggan's dedication to public service, his commitment to education, and his active involvement in community organizations make him a respected and influential figure in Maine politics. His ongoing efforts to represent and serve his constituents demonstrate his unwavering commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of the people in his district.
