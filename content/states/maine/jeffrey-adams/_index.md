Jeffrey Adams is a member of the Republican Party serving in the Maine House of Representatives for District 144. He assumed office on December 7, 2022, and his current term is set to end on December 4, 2024. Adams secured his position by winning the general election held on November 8, 2022. He resides in Lebanon, Maine. In his role, Adams represents the interests of his district, contributing to the legislative process and working on various issues pertinent to his constituents. His election to the Maine House of Representatives reflects the trust and support of the voters in District 144.
