Representative Rachel Henderson, based in Rumford, is currently in her inaugural term in the Maine House, representing District 78, which encompasses the towns of Bethel, Byron, Hanover, Milton Township, Roxbury, and Rumford. Rachel actively contributes to the 131st Legislature’s Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary.

Having earned her high school equivalent degree through the Jobs for American Graduates program, Rep. Henderson has navigated a diverse career path, working as a nanny, rip-saw operator, and sales manager before reaching her current role as a Support Manager for a telecommunications company. She is a proud member of the Gun Owners of Maine and the Webb River Valley Sportsman’s club.

During her leisure moments, Representative Henderson finds joy in outdoor activities with her family.
