Tiffany Roberts is a member of the Democratic Party serving her third term in the Maine House of Representatives, representing District 149. She is the House chair of the Legislature’s Innovation, Development, Economic Advancement and Business Committee, focusing on policies related to economic development, student debt relief, and professional licensing.

Roberts is a small business owner and public school parent, working in graphic design and digital marketing, primarily for local businesses. She has served on the board of directors of a child care center and has been a vestry leadership member at St. George’s Episcopal Church in York. Her community involvement extends to the South Berwick-Tuskegee Sister City Project.

During the 129th Legislature, she was an advocate for preserving Maine’s public lands as a member of the Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry Committee. She has also been active in supporting individuals impacted by the opioid epidemic and serves as the legislative member of the MaineMOM Advisory Panel.

Roberts resides with her three sons, her partner Jeff, a cat, and a dog in the Agamenticus neighborhood of South Berwick. She is pursuing a degree in business administration and digital marketing.
