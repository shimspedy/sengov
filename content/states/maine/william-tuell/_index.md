Will Tuell of East Machias is serving his fifth non-consecutive term as the Representative for District 10.

Rep. Tuell brings extensive legislative experience, having previously served as Lead on the State and Local Government Committee, as well as a member of the Marine Resources Committee and the Environment and Natural Resources Committee.

A journalist with a background in public relations, Will is committed to community service and serves on the Arise Addiction Recovery Board of Directors. Will has also worked as a substitute teacher in Machias area schools.

He is a graduate of Washington Academy and holds a B.A. in History from the University of Maine at Machias where he was named the 2017 young alumni award winner.
