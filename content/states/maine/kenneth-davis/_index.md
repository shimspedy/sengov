Kenneth Davis, also known as "Bucket," is a member of the Maine House of Representatives, representing District 10. He is a Republican who assumed office on December 7, 2022. His current term ends on December 4, 2024.

Prior to his election to the State House, Davis served on the East Machias Select Board for 10 years. He is also a retired lobsterman and has been involved in the fishing industry for over 40 years.

Davis is a member of the Joint Standing Committee on Marine Resources. He is a strong advocate for the fishing industry and has worked to protect Maine's natural resources. He is also a proponent of responsible government spending and believes that government should be limited in its role in people's lives.

Davis is a lifelong resident of East Machias, Maine. He is married and has three children.
