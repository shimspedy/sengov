Tiffany Strout is a Republican politician who serves as a member of the Maine House of Representatives, representing District 11. She was elected to the office in November 2022, defeating her Democratic opponent Roland Rogers by a wide margin. She is a member of the Marine Resources Committee in the legislature.

Strout lives in Harrington, Maine, where she works as a business development manager with GenoTyping Center of America, a customer service representative with Wreaths Across America, and a senior project manager with The Jackson Laboratory. She has an associate degree from the University of Maine at Augusta, which she obtained in 2000.

Strout is passionate about supporting the fishing industry, promoting rural economic development, and improving access to health care and education in her district. She is also involved in various community organizations, such as the Harrington Historical Society, the Harrington Family Health Center, and the Downeast Salmon Federation.
