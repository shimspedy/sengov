Representative Kevin Campbell is a member of the Republican Party serving in the Wyoming House of Representatives. He represents District 62 and assumed office on January 6, 2025. His current term is set to end on January 4, 2027.

Kevin Campbell has a professional background in oilfield services, which has provided him with a deep understanding of the energy sector and its significance to Wyoming's economy. Beyond his professional career, he has been actively involved in various civic organizations. He has served with the Jesse Martin American Legion Post #009, the Wyoming Wool Growers, the Navajo-Churro Sheep Association, the Benevolent and Protective Order of Elks #1353, and the Wyoming Historical Society. These affiliations highlight his commitment to community service and his dedication to preserving Wyoming's rich history and traditions.

As a legislator, Campbell is focused on addressing the needs of his constituents in Converse and Natrona Counties. His priorities include supporting the energy industry, promoting economic development, and ensuring that Wyoming's rural communities have access to essential services. His leadership and dedication to public service reflect his deep-rooted commitment to the people of District 62 and the state of Wyoming.
