Julie Jarvis is a Republican member of the Wyoming House of Representatives, representing District 57. She assumed office on January 6, 2025, and her current term is set to end on January 4, 2027.

Julie Jarvis has a strong academic background, holding a bachelor's degree from the University of Bridgeport, earned in 2000, followed by a graduate degree from the same institution in 2001. She later pursued a Ph.D. at the University of Connecticut, which she completed in 2018. Her professional career includes serving as a director, showcasing her leadership and organizational skills.

Beyond her professional achievements, Julie has been actively involved in civic organizations, including The Reading League Wyoming and the American Legion Auxiliary. Her dedication to community service reflects her commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of her constituents and beyond.

Julie Jarvis's journey into politics began with her successful campaign for the Wyoming House of Representatives in 2024. She defeated the incumbent in the Republican primary and went on to win the general election with a significant majority. Her focus as a legislator includes education, community development, and fostering economic growth in her district.

With her diverse experiences and dedication to public service, Julie Jarvis continues to work diligently to represent the interests of her constituents in District 57. Her leadership and vision are shaping the future of her community and the state of Wyoming.
