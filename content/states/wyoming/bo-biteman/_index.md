Dennis Dean "Bo" Biteman (born October 23, 1978) is an American politician and a staunch conservative. He has served in both the Wyoming House of Representatives and the Wyoming Senate.

- **Wyoming House of Representatives (2017-2019):** Biteman represented District 51 in the Wyoming House of Representatives from 2017 to 2019.
- **Wyoming Senate (2019-present):** Since January 7, 2019, he has been representing District 21 in the Wyoming Senate.

**Political Positions:**
- Biteman supports restrictions on abortion and introduced a fetal heart bill to regulate abortion.
- He opposes instituting a state income tax in Wyoming and advocates for lowering taxes on mineral extraction, which contributes significantly to Wyoming's tax revenue.
- Biteman has aligned with the debunked claims of election fraud alleged by Donald Trump after the 2020 United States presidential election and is associated with the Stop the Steal movement.

Senator Biteman is a defender of free markets, the Constitution, federalism, and individual rights.
