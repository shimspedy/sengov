J.T. Larson (Republican Party) is a member of the Wyoming House of Representatives, representing District 17. He assumed office on January 2, 2023, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Larson was born in Rock Springs, Wyoming, and earned an associate degree from Western Wyoming Community College in 2021. His career experience includes working as a business manager.

Committee Assignments (2023-2024):
- Joint Travel, Recreation, Wildlife, and Cultural Resources Committee
- Joint Minerals, Business, and Economic Development Committee

Elections:
- In the 2022 general election, J.T. Larson defeated incumbent Chad Banks to secure his seat in the Wyoming House of Representatives[^1^][1].
- He is running for re-election in the Republican primary on August 20, 2024
