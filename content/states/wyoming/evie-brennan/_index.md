Evie Brennan is a thoughtful conservative who wants to preserve Wyoming for her children and their generation what her grandparents preserved for her. Evie is a third generation Wyoming native whose great-grandfather homesteaded in Wyoming. Married with four children, she and her husband are raising a new Wyoming generation.

Evie Brennan graduated from Cheyenne East High School in 2000. She attended the University of Wyoming where she graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Nursing degree in 2004.

Evie has worked as a nurse in Cheyenne since graduating college. For the past 8 years she has worked in the ICU. This experience has prepared Evie to work hard and defend the freedoms of Wyoming's next generation
