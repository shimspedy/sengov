Robert Davis was born in Omaha, Nebraska, and has lived in Wyoming for 50 years. He is a member of the Republican Party and currently serves as a representative in the Wyoming House of Representatives, representing District 47. His career experience includes working in the commercial construction business for 7 years, serving as a public works director, owning and operating Davis Roustabout and Contract Pumping for 35 years, and being a rancher. Robert Davis is married to Lexine (Herold) and has been fortunate to be surrounded by their children and grandchildren on their ranch.

Committee Assignments (2023-2024):
- Joint Agriculture, State and Public Lands and Water Resources Committee
- Regulatory Reduction Task Force
- Sage Grouse Implementation Team

Elections 2024:
Robert Davis is running for re-election to the Wyoming House of Representatives in the Republican primary on August 20, 2024.
