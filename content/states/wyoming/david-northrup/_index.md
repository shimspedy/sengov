David Northrup, born on March 6, 1961, in Laramie, Wyoming, is an accomplished legislator. He currently represents the 25th district in the Wyoming House of Representatives, having assumed office on January 10, 2023. Prior to this, Northrup served as the representative for the 50th district from 2013 to 2021.

His commitment to public service extends beyond legislative duties. Northrup has actively volunteered with various civic organizations, including the Willwood Irrigation District, Willwood Light and Power, Park County School District #1, Northwest College Ag advisory board, and as the Park County Republican chairman.

Northrup's dedication to his constituents and community underscores his role as a responsible and engaged representative. His contributions have left a lasting impact on Wyoming's political landscape.
