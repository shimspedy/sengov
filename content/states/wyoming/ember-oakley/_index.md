Ember Oakley, a Wyoming native, grew up surrounded by the rugged beauty of the state. Her father, a Wyoming Game Warden, instilled in her a love for the outdoors and a strong work ethic. As a three-sport athlete in high school, Ember learned the value of discipline and perseverance.

Education:
- Bachelor of Science in Agribusiness from Texas A&M University (1998)
- Juris Doctor (J.D.) from the University of Wyoming (2009)

Career:
- Since 2011: Prosecutor at the Fremont County, Wyoming Attorney's Office
- Elected to the Wyoming House of Representatives (55th district) in November 2020

Ember Oakley's commitment to public service and her deep roots in Wyoming make her a dedicated advocate for her community. She continues to work tirelessly to address the needs of her constituents and contribute to the betterment of the state.
