Bill Allemand is a member of the Wyoming House of Representatives, representing District 58. He assumed office on January 2, 2023, and his current term ends on January 6, 2025. Born and raised in Wyoming, Bill has deep roots in the state. His career experience includes working as a rancher and business owner.

**Committee Assignments (2023-2024)**
- Joint Agriculture, State and Public Lands, and Water Resources Committee
- NCSL - Natural Resources and Infrastructure

**Elections**
- Bill Allemand won election in the general election for Wyoming House of Representatives District 58 on November 8, 2022.
- In the upcoming 2024 election, he is running for re-election in the Republican primary on August 20, 2024.

**About Bill**
Bill Allemand was born in Wyoming and raised on his family ranch in Natrona County. After graduating high school from Midwest in 1977, he moved to Kansas, raised four wonderful children, and became a grandfather. However, his Wyoming roots kept calling him home.
