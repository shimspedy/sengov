Ken Chestek is an American politician and a Democratic member of the Wyoming House of Representatives. He has been representing the 13th district since January 10, 2023. In addition to his political role, Chestek is also a professor of law emeritus at the University of Wyoming College of Law.

Born in Cincinnati, Ohio, Chestek earned a bachelor's degree from Penn State University in 1975 and a law degree from the University of Pittsburgh in 1979. His career experience includes working as a professor.
