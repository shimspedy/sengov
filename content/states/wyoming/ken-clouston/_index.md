Ken Clouston is an American politician and businessman serving as a Republican member of the Wyoming House of Representatives from the 32nd district. He was elected unopposed in the 2022 Kansas House of Representatives election on November 8, 2022.

Ken Clouston was born in Bismarck, North Dakota, and lives in Wyoming. Clouston earned a B.S. in physical therapy in 1992 and a doctorate of physical therapy in 2005, both from the University of North Dakota. His career experience includes working as a physical therapist.

Clouston is a former Campbell County School District board member who moved to Wyoming from North Dakota in 1993. He was first elected to the Legislature in 2022, and has made education and health issues his main focus, which aligns with his previous experience.
