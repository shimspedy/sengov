Wendy Davis Schuler (Republican Party) is a member of the Wyoming State Senate, representing District 15. She assumed office on January 7, 2019. Schuler earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Education from the University of Wyoming. Prior to entering politics, she was a teacher in the Uinta County School District Number 1. Schuler defeated incumbent Paul Barnard in the 2018 Republican primary and has been serving as a senator since then. She is known for her mild-mannered approach and commitment to compromise and reason within the legislative chamber.
