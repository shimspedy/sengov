Cody Wylie, a Republican Party member, currently serves as a representative in the Wyoming House of Representatives, representing District 39. He assumed office on January 2, 2023, and his current term extends until January 6, 2025.

## Early Life and Education

Cody Wylie was born in Rock Springs, Wyoming. He pursued higher education at the University of Wyoming, where he earned a bachelor's degree in Political Science in 2011. Prior to that, he completed an associate degree at Western Wyoming Community College.

## Career and Contributions

Wylie's professional background includes working as a construction manager. His commitment to public service led him to serve on the Rock Springs planning and zoning board as of 2022. His dedication to his community and constituents has been evident throughout his tenure.

## Legislative Involvement

During his time in office, Cody Wylie has actively participated in legislative matters. He has been assigned to several committees, including:

- **Joint Corporations, Elections, and Political Subdivisions Committee**
- **Joint Transportation, Highways, and Military Affairs Committee**
- **Energy Council**

## 2024 Elections

In the upcoming 2024 elections, Wylie is running for re-election in the Republican primary for Wyoming House of Representatives District 39. The primary will take place on August 20, 2024. His opponents include Marshall Burt and Laura Mckee.

Cody Wylie's commitment to public service, combined with his educational background and experience, makes him a dedicated representative for District 39. As he continues to serve, his constituents can expect him to advocate for their interests and contribute to the betterment of Wyoming.
