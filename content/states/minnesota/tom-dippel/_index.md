Tom Dippel, born on May 19, 1982, is an American politician who represents District 41B in the Minnesota House of Representatives. He attended Park High School and later became a business owner. In November 2022, Dippel defeated Jen Fox in the general election for the 41B district of the Minnesota House of Representatives, winning 51 percent of the votes. He succeeded Shane Hudella and assumed office on January 14, 2025.

Dippel's political career has been marked by his commitment to his constituents and his dedication to public service. As a member of the Minnesota House of Representatives, he has been actively involved in various committees, including Education Finance, Education Policy, Health Finance and Policy, and Legacy Finance. His work in these committees has focused on improving the quality of education, healthcare, and preserving the state's cultural heritage.

In addition to his political career, Dippel is a devoted family man. He is married to his spouse, Carrie, and together they have six children. The Dippel family resides in Cottage Grove, Minnesota, where they are actively involved in their local community.
