Karen McCormick is an American politician and veterinarian who is currently serving as a member of the Colorado House of Representatives from the 11th district. She was elected in November 2020 and assumed office on January 13, 2021.

McCormick earned a Bachelor of Science degree in dairy science and a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine from the University of Florida. She owned and operated a veterinary clinic for 16 years and has also worked as a communications coach at the Colorado State University Veterinary Teaching Hospital.

In 2018, McCormick was the Democratic nominee for Colorado's 4th congressional district, but she lost to incumbent Republican Ken Buck. McCormick was elected to the Colorado House of Representatives in November 2020 and assumed office on January 13, 2021.

McCormick and her husband have three children and live in Longmont, Colorado. Her current term ends on January 8, 2025.
