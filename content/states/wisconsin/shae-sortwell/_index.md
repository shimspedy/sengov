Shae A. Sortwell, born on August 3, 1985, hails from Saratoga Springs, New York. He is a dedicated Republican politician and currently serves as a member of the Wisconsin State Assembly, representing the 2nd Assembly district since 2019. Before his political career, Shae wore multiple hats: he served in the U.S. Army Reserve Chemical Corps, achieving the rank of Sergeant, and also worked as a truck driver. His educational background includes a Bachelor of Arts degree in Public Administration from the University of Wisconsin-Green Bay (class of 2006), and he holds Hazmat Tech certification from the U.S. Army Chemical School (2012). Shae resides in the community of Gibson, Wisconsin, with his wife Krista and their six children.
