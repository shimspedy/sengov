Jason Thompson is a Republican member of the Utah House of Representatives, representing District 3. He assumed office on January 1, 2025, and his current term is set to end on January 1, 2027.

Jason Thompson has a deep-rooted passion for public service and community development. A long-time resident of Cache Valley, Utah, Jason has dedicated his career to improving the lives of those around him. He holds a bachelor's degree in History and Political Science, which he earned in 2006, and a Master of Public Administration degree, both from Brigham Young University. His academic background laid the foundation for his commitment to leadership and governance.

Before joining the Utah House of Representatives, Jason served as the Mayor of River Heights, Utah. His tenure as mayor provided him with valuable insights into the challenges and opportunities faced by local communities. Jason's leadership style is characterized by collaboration, innovation, and a steadfast commitment to addressing the needs of his constituents.

In addition to his political career, Jason is a successful entrepreneur. He co-founded Fox Pest Control, which has grown to become one of the largest pest control companies in the nation. Through his entrepreneurial ventures, Jason has created thousands of jobs, many of which are based in Cache Valley. His business acumen extends to other industries as well, including real estate and home services.

Jason is also a devoted family man. He and his wife, Dana, are proud parents of six children, including two sets of twins. In his free time, Jason enjoys engaging in outdoor activities with his family, such as gardening and wildlife photography. He is also an avid reader and collector of rare books, a hobby that reflects his lifelong love of learning.

As a legislator, Jason Thompson is committed to fostering economic growth, enhancing educational opportunities, and preserving the values that make Utah a great place to live. His dedication to public service and his diverse experiences make him a respected and effective representative for District 3.
