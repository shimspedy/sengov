David Shallenberger, a member of the Republican Party, serves in the Utah House of Representatives, representing District 58. He assumed office on January 1, 2025, and his current term is set to conclude on January 1, 2027.

David Shallenberger's journey into public service is rooted in his deep connection to Utah and his commitment to its communities. A proud graduate of Brigham Young University, where he earned a degree in mechanical engineering, David furthered his education by obtaining a Juris Doctorate from Willamette University. His professional career spans multiple disciplines, including engineering, law, and entrepreneurship. As a small business owner, he has demonstrated a keen ability to navigate complex challenges and implement innovative solutions, particularly in the fields of energy and water conservation.

David's dedication to his constituents is evident in his proactive approach to addressing the needs of District 58. He is passionate about fostering sustainable economic growth, supporting local businesses, and ensuring that the voices of his community are heard on Capitol Hill. His leadership style is characterized by a blend of analytical problem-solving and a genuine desire to make a positive impact.

Beyond his professional and political endeavors, David is a devoted family man. He and his wife, Lara, are raising six children in Orem, Utah. The Shallenberger family is actively involved in their community, participating in sports, music, and outdoor activities. David's love for Utah's natural beauty is reflected in his hobbies, which include paragliding, hiking, mountain biking, and skiing.

David Shallenberger's vision for Utah is one of progress and unity. He believes in the power of collaboration and is committed to working tirelessly to uphold the values and aspirations of the people he represents. His multifaceted background and unwavering dedication make him a dynamic and effective leader in the Utah House of Representatives.
