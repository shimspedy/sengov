Neil Walter is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 74 in the Utah House of Representatives. He assumed office on January 1, 2023, and his current term ends on January 1, 2025.

Neil Walter was born in and lives in Utah. He graduated from Dixie High School and went on to pursue higher education. He earned a B.S. in finance from Brigham Young University in 1999 and an M.B.A. from Carnegie Mellon University in 2003.

In terms of his professional career, Neil Walter has had a diverse range of experiences. He worked as a quantitative analyst with ConocoPhillips and later transitioned into academia as a finance and economics teacher at Dixie State University. He also held the position of CEO at ERA Brokers and NAI Excel.

Neil Walter ran for election to the Utah House of Representatives to represent District 74 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. His victory was decisive, with him securing 100% of the 15,666 votes cast.

In the Republican primary for Utah House of Representatives District 74, Neil Walter defeated Kristy Pike on June 28, 2022. He secured 63.4% of the 9,387 votes cast.
