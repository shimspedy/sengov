Jason B. Kyle is an American engineer and politician serving as a member of the Utah House of Representatives for the 8th district. He was elected in November 2022 and assumed office on January 1, 2023.

Kyle was born and raised in Utah. He earned a Bachelor of Science degree in chemical engineering from the University of Utah. From 2010 to 2017, Kyle worked for Varian Medical Systems as a manufacturing engineer, environmental manager, and safety manager. He joined the Varex Imaging Corporation as an environmental safety manager in 2017 and became a production manager in 2019.

He is a member of the Republican Party and his current term ends on January 1, 2025. He ran for election to the Utah House of Representatives to represent District 8 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022.

Jason Kyle is a husband, father, and business manager passionate about fighting for the future of Utah. He built a career in management within the medical and industrial manufacturing industry. His career experience includes working as a chemical engineer and environmental manager at Varian Medical Systems and a corporate environmental, health and safety manager and production manager at Varex Imaging Corporation.
