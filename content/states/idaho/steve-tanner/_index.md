Steve Tanner is a dedicated public servant and a member of the Idaho House of Representatives, representing District 13B. Born in Honolulu, Hawaii, Steve was raised in a military family, which instilled in him a strong sense of duty and service. His father, a World War II Navy corpsman, played a significant role in shaping his values and commitment to public service.

Steve's educational journey began at California State University, Long Beach, where he earned a bachelor's degree in philosophy. He furthered his education by obtaining a Master of Divinity from Fuller Seminary in Pasadena. Currently, he is completing a Master of Arts in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Liberty University, reflecting his passion for mental health and helping others.

Throughout his career, Steve has worked in various IT roles, primarily as a database programmer and analyst. His professional experience includes positions at AT&T, Countrywide Bank, Bank of America, and Latham & Watkins Law Firm. This diverse background has equipped him with a unique skill set and a deep understanding of the challenges faced by individuals and businesses alike.

Steve's love for the outdoors and adventure is evident in his personal life. He enjoys camping, backpacking, and exploring the American West. Some of his notable achievements include reaching the peak of Mt. Whitney three times and canoeing the Green River in Utah. His appreciation for nature and the environment is a testament to his commitment to preserving Idaho's natural beauty.

In addition to his professional and personal pursuits, Steve is actively involved in his community and church. He and his wife, Kathy, have been dedicated to serving others and making a positive impact on those around them. Steve's commitment to his faith and community is a driving force behind his work as a legislator.

As a member of the Idaho House of Representatives, Steve Tanner is focused on addressing the needs and concerns of his constituents. He serves on several committees, including Commerce & Human Resources, Education, and Environment, Energy & Technology. His legislative priorities include promoting economic growth, improving education, and protecting Idaho's natural resources.

Steve's journey to Idaho began over 30 years ago when he first visited Boise and fell in love with the state. He and his family eventually relocated to Idaho, seeking more freedom and a better quality of life. Since moving to Idaho, Steve has been an active participant in the political process, serving as a Republican precinct committeeman and working to raise awareness about important issues.

Steve Tanner's dedication to public service, combined with his diverse background and passion for helping others, makes him a valuable asset to the Idaho House of Representatives. His commitment to his constituents and his unwavering determination to make a positive impact on his community are the hallmarks of his career.
