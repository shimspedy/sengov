Kathy Frelich is an American politician and a member of the Republican Party. She is currently serving as a representative in the North Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 15. She assumed office on December 1, 2022.

Frelich's career experience includes working as a hearing outreach specialist with the North Dakota School for the Deaf. She has a Bachelor of Arts degree in Human Resources and Social Services. 

In addition to her professional career, Frelich is also a family woman. She is married to Mike and they have three children together.

Frelich's political journey began when she ran for election to the North Dakota House of Representatives to represent District 15. She won in the general election on November 8, 2022. Her current term ends on December 1, 2026.

As a representative, Frelich has been active in sponsoring bills and participating in various committees. She is a member of the Interim Health Services Committee, the Human Services Committee, and the Transportation Committee.

Frelich represents District 15, which is located in Devils Lake, North Dakota. As of the 2020 Census, North Dakota state representatives represented an average of 8,295 residents. After the 2010 Census, each member represented 7,190 residents.

Frelich's dedication to her work and her constituents is evident in her active participation in the legislative process. Her work in the North Dakota House of Representatives continues to impact the lives of the residents of District 15 and the state of North Dakota.
