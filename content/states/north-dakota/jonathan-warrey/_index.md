Jonathan Warrey is an American politician and businessman who has been serving as a member of the North Dakota House of Representatives from the 22nd district since 2022. He is a member of the Republican Party.

Warrey lives in Casselton, North Dakota. He earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing from the University of North Dakota in 1996. His career experience includes working as a director of sales development and Chief Operating Officer for Marco Technologies. He previously worked as vice president of sales and regional sales director for Marco.

Before his political career, Warrey started as a sales representative after graduating from the University of North Dakota. He quickly earned leadership roles in sales management. He became Vice President of Sales in 2012 and was COO from 2014-2021. An idealist at heart, he sees what can be and then enjoys bringing people together to achieve big outcomes.

In his political career, Warrey assumed office on November 14, 2022. His current term ends on December 1, 2024. Warrey is running for re-election to the North Dakota House of Representatives to represent District 22. He declared candidacy for the Republican primary scheduled on June 11, 2024. Warrey was appointed to the North Dakota House of Representatives on November 14, 2022, to replace Michael Howe (R).

In the 2023-2024 legislative session, Warrey was assigned to the following committees: House Industry, Business and Labor Committee and House Political Subdivisions Committee.

Warrey is also involved in his local community. He served as the Casselton Park Board President, was a member of the UND College of Business Advisory Panel, and served as the Emerging Prairie Board Treasurer. He also contributed to the Casselton Economic Development Committee.

Jonathan Warrey is married to Amy and they have two children.
