​Doug Osowski is a Republican member of the North Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 42. He assumed office on December 1, 2024, and his current term will end on December 1, 2028.​

Born in December 1961, Doug has spent his life deeply rooted in Grand Forks, North Dakota. He pursued higher education in the field of industrial technology, earning both a Bachelor of Arts and a Master of Arts degree. These academic achievements laid the groundwork for his extensive career in information technology within the North Dakota University System. Over the years, Doug has demonstrated a steadfast commitment to the state's educational infrastructure, culminating in a Certificate of Appreciation honoring his 25 years of dedicated service.​

In his professional capacity, Doug serves as a Senior Systems Administrator for the North Dakota University System. In this role, he is responsible for overseeing critical IT operations, ensuring the seamless functioning of technological systems that support both students and faculty across the state's higher education institutions. His expertise in this area has been instrumental in advancing the technological capabilities of the university system.​

Within the North Dakota House of Representatives, Doug is an active participant on the Education and Transportation Committees. His involvement in these committees allows him to leverage his professional experience to influence policies that enhance the state's educational framework and transportation infrastructure. Doug's legislative priorities are deeply aligned with fostering advancements in education and improving the state's transportation systems, reflecting his commitment to the well-being and progress of his constituents in District 42.​

Residing at 2510 Ninth Avenue North in Grand Forks, Doug maintains a close connection to the community he represents. He shares his home with his wife, Connie, and together they have raised three children. This familial foundation provides Doug with a profound understanding of the challenges and aspirations faced by families in his district, further informing his approach to policymaking and constituent advocacy.​

Through his extensive experience in information technology, dedication to public service, and active engagement in legislative committees, Doug Osowski brings a comprehensive and informed perspective to the North Dakota House of Representatives. His unwavering commitment to enhancing education and transportation within the state underscores his role as a valuable representative for the people of District 42.​
