Judy Estenson is an American politician who is currently serving as a member of the North Dakota Senate from the 15th district. She is a member of the Republican Party.

Estenson assumed office on December 1, 2022. Her current term ends on December 1, 2024. Prior to her political career, she was a retired registered nurse. She holds a Bachelor of Arts degree.

She is married to Hal and they have five children. She resides in Warwick, North Dakota, U.S. In addition to her role as a senator, she is also the Senate Portfolio Leader for Lake Region State College and Spirit Lake Reservation.

Throughout her political career, she has shown dedication and commitment to her role. Her work in the Senate has been marked by her advocacy for healthcare and education, reflecting her background in nursing and her commitment to improving the lives of her constituents.

In her spare time, Estenson enjoys spending time with her family and participating in community events. She is known for her approachability and her willingness to listen to the concerns of her constituents. Her dedication to public service continues to make a significant impact on the people of North Dakota.
