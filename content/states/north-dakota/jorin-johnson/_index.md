Jorin Johnson is a member of the North Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 41. He is affiliated with the Republican Party. Johnson assumed office on December 1, 2022, and his current term ends on December 1, 2026.

Johnson ran for election to the North Dakota House of Representatives to represent District 41 and won in the general election held on November 8, 2022. He has been serving in the House since 2023.

Johnson earned a B.S. in economics from North Dakota State University in 1992. His career experience includes working as a financial representative with Superior Insurance Agency, Inc., a facilitator with Dale Carnegie Training, and a relationship manager with Community First Bank.

In the 2023-2024 legislative session, Johnson was assigned to the House Industry, Business and Labor Committee and the House Government and Veterans Affairs Committee. He has sponsored several bills during his tenure, contributing to the legislative process.

Johnson's office is located at 1002 28th Street South, Suite C, Fargo, ND 58103-8736. He can be contacted at his work number, 701-356-3233, or via email at jjohnson@ndlegis.gov.

In the general election for North Dakota House of Representatives District 41, Johnson and incumbent Michelle Strinden defeated Megan Edwardson and Lillian Jones. Johnson received 27.0% of the votes, securing his position in the House of Representatives.
