JoAnne Rademacher is an American politician who has been serving as a member of the North Dakota House of Representatives since 2024. She represents the 38th district of North Dakota. She is a member of the Republican Party.

Born around 1954, Rademacher has made a significant impact in her political career. She assumed office on January 19, 2024, serving alongside Dan Ruby. Prior to her, the position was held by Larry Bellew. Rademacher's political journey has been marked by dedication and commitment to her role and responsibilities.
