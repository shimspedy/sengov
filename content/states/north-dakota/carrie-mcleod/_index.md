Carrie McLeod is an American politician and a member of the Republican Party. She represents District 45 in the North Dakota House of Representatives. McLeod assumed office on December 1, 2022, and her current term ends on December 1, 2026.

McLeod ran for election to the North Dakota House of Representatives to represent District 45 and won in the general election held on November 8, 2022. She serves alongside Scott Wagner, who is also a member of the Republican Party.

In terms of education, McLeod earned a Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Nutrition and Dietetics from North Dakota State University. She also holds a Master of Business Administration (M.B.A.) and a Master of Arts (M.A.) in Business Management from the University of Mary.

Professionally, McLeod is the owner of Disease Management and Prevention Dietitians, PLLC. Her career experience includes working as a corporate community benefit/community health improvement lead with Sanford Health, a member of the clinical faculty with the University of North Dakota School of Medicine, and a regional clinic manager with the MeritCare Health System.

As a legislator, McLeod has sponsored several bills and has been assigned to various committees. She has been a part of the House Human Services Committee and the House Government and Veterans Affairs Committee.

McLeod resides in Fargo, North Dakota, which is part of the district she represents.
