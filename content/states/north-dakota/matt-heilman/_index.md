# Biography of Representative Matt Heilman

Matthew Heilman, known as Matt Heilman, is an American politician and a member of the Republican Party. He was born on July 19, 2001, in Bismarck, North Dakota, U.S. Heilman is a member of the North Dakota House of Representatives, representing the 7th district. His district comprises part of Burleigh County.

## Education

Heilman is an alumnus of Bismarck State College, where he earned an Associate degree. 

## Political Career

Before his election, Heilman served as a precinct committeeman for the District 7 Republicans. He ran for election to the North Dakota House of Representatives to represent District 7 and won in the general election on November 8, 2022. He assumed office on December 1, 2022. His current term ends on December 1, 2026.

## Political Positions

Heilman has been active in various political positions. He supports House Bill 1494, which is aimed at making school meals more accessible to students. He also introduced a bill to allow university students to carry weapons on campus, stating that if students were carrying, it would be a great deterrent for people who want to commit violent acts against them. Heilman opposes sanctuary cities and supports a bill that would ban them in North Dakota. He stated that this was a proactive measure to prevent the "utter disarray" seen at the southern border from coming to North Dakota.

## Personal Life

In his personal life, Heilman is an Eagle Scout. He currently resides in Bismarck, North Dakota, U.S.
