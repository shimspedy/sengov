Jeff Barta is a distinguished member of the North Dakota Senate, representing the 43rd district. He assumed office on December 1, 2022, and is affiliated with the Republican Party. His political journey is marked by dedication and a commitment to serve his constituents.

Born and raised in Michigan, North Dakota, Barta's roots are deeply embedded in the state. He pursued his undergraduate degree from the University of North Dakota, furthering his education with an MBA from Troy University in Troy, Alabama.

Barta's professional life is as diverse as it is impressive. He is an Athletic Trainer and a Small Business Owner, demonstrating his versatility and entrepreneurial spirit. His business endeavors are based in Grand Forks, where he has resided with his wife since 1984. They are proud parents of one child.

In his role as a senator, Barta has been proactive and involved in various committees. He has served as the Senate Portfolio Leader for The North Dakota State Mill and Elevator. His contributions to the Government Finance Committee and Health Services Committee have been significant. He has also been a part of the Industry and Business Procedural Employment North Dakota Statutory Committee and the North Dakota Lottery Advisory Commission.

Barta's work extends beyond the Senate. He is actively involved in community service, contributing to The Chamber in Grand Forks/East Grand Forks, St. Joseph's Social Care, and the Holy Family Catholic Church.

Senator Jeff Barta's journey is a testament to his dedication to public service and his commitment to the people of North Dakota. His work continues to impact the lives of his constituents positively, making him a respected figure in North Dakota's political landscape.
