Landon Bahl is an American politician and a member of the Republican Party. He is currently serving as a representative in the North Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 17. His tenure began on December 1, 2022, and his current term is set to end on December 1, 2026.

Born and raised in Minot, North Dakota, Bahl pursued his higher education at the University of North Dakota. He graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Entrepreneurship and Marketing in 2016. His academic achievements also include a minor in Sport Business.

In addition to his political career, Bahl has made significant contributions to the business sector. He is the Director of Business Development at 322 Hospitality Group. His entrepreneurial spirit is further demonstrated by his ownership of Knowles Jewelry. 

Bahl's dedication to his community extends beyond business and politics. He has served as the Festival Director of LandoLIVE Music Festival and the Director of Development of the University of North Dakota Alumni Association & Foundation Center for Innovation.

In the 2022 elections, Bahl ran for a seat in the North Dakota House of Representatives to represent District 17. He won the general election held on November 8, 2022, alongside incumbent Mark Sanford. The duo now represents District 17 in the North Dakota House of Representatives.

During his tenure, Bahl has been assigned to several committees. In the 68th Legislative Assembly - Regular Session (2023), he served on the Interim Higher Education Committee, Workforce Committee, Standing Government and Veterans Affairs, and Judiciary.

Landon Bahl's journey from a business owner to a representative in the North Dakota House of Representatives is a testament to his commitment to serving his community. His work in both the private sector and public service demonstrates his dedication to improving the lives of the people in his district.
