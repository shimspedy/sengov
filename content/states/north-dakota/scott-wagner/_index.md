Scott Wagner is an American politician and a member of the Republican Party. He is currently serving as a representative in the North Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 45.

Wagner is the owner of Wagner Window Cleaning LLC. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Political Science from North Dakota State University (NDSU). Prior to his tenure in the House, he served as a Cass County Commissioner from 2000 to 2012. During his time as a commissioner, he was recognized with the NDACo Excellence in County Government Award in 2021.

He began his term in the North Dakota House of Representatives in 2023. His contact information is as follows: 2906 Edgemont Street North, Fargo, ND 58102-1552. He can be reached via cell at 701-799-2880 or via email at swagner@ndlegis.gov.

In addition to his legislative duties, Wagner is a member of several committees. He serves on the Interim Legislative Audit and Fiscal Review Committee and the Workforce Committee. He also holds positions on the Standing Industry, Business and Labor Transportation committee.

Wagner ran for election to the North Dakota House of Representatives to represent District 45 and won in the general election held on November 8, 2022.

His career experience includes owning Wagner Window Cleaning LLC. He resides in Fargo, North Dakota with his wife, Greta.
