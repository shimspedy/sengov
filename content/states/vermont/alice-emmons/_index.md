## Early Life and Education
Alice M. Emmons was born and raised in Springfield, Vermont. She attended local elementary schools and graduated from Springfield High School. Alice pursued higher education at the University of New Hampshire in Durham, where she earned a Bachelor of Science degree.

## Professional Career
Alice Emmons is the owner and proprietor of Lamps & Shades by Alice, a business she has successfully managed for several years. Her entrepreneurial spirit and dedication to her community are evident in her professional endeavors.

## Political Career
Alice Emmons has been a dedicated public servant for many years. She has represented the Windsor-3-2 district as a Democrat in the Vermont House of Representatives since 1983. Throughout her tenure, she has been an active member of various committees, including:

- House Committee on Corrections and Institutions (Chair)
- Joint Legislative Justice Oversight Committee (Chair)
- Capitol Complex Security Advisory Committee (Vice Chair)
- Legislative Advisory Committee on the State House (Chair)
- Access Board (Architectural Barrier Compliance Board)
- Art in State Buildings Advisory Committee
- Vermont State Council on Interstate Adult Offender Supervision
