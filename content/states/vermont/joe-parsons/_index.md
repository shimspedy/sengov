## Early Life and Education
Joseph Parsons was born and raised in Bradford, Vermont. He attended Oxbow High School, where he developed a strong work ethic and a passion for community service. After graduating from high school, Joe began working in the flooring trade at Valley Floors in Bradford, a position he has held since 2005.

## Personal Life
In 2004, Joe met his future wife, Jenna, while she was attending nursing school at the University of Vermont. The couple married and now have two young children. They enjoy living a homesteading lifestyle, which includes gardening, raising animals, and being self-sufficient.

## Political Career
Joe Parsons' political career began at the local level when he was elected to the Newbury Select Board in 2019. His dedication to his community and his ability to address local issues effectively led to his appointment as a member of the Budget Committee and the Board of Civil Authority. In 2020, he was appointed as the Road Commissioner, where he oversaw the maintenance and improvement of local infrastructure.

In January 2021, Joe was elected to the Vermont House of Representatives, representing the Orange-Caledonia District. As a member of the Republican Party, he has been actively involved in legislative matters, focusing on issues such as general housing, military affairs, and community development.

## Legislative Work
During his tenure in the Vermont House of Representatives, Joe has been a member of the House Committee on General, Housing, and Military Affairs. He has sponsored several bills and resolutions aimed at improving the quality of life for his constituents. His voting record reflects his commitment to fiscal responsibility, community development, and support for veterans and military families.

## Community Involvement
Joe Parsons is deeply committed to his community. He has been involved in various local initiatives and projects aimed at enhancing the well-being of residents in his district. His hands-on approach and willingness to listen to his constituents have earned him a reputation as a dedicated and approachable representative.

## Future Goals
Looking ahead, Joe aims to continue his work in the Vermont House of Representatives, focusing on policies that promote economic growth, improve infrastructure, and support education and healthcare. He is dedicated to ensuring that the voices of his constituents are heard and that their needs are addressed effectively.
