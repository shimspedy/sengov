## Early Life and Education
Matthew E. Walker, commonly known as Matt Walker, was born in Rutland, Vermont. He spent his formative years in Brandon and Northfield, Vermont, where he attended Otter Valley Union High School and Northfield Middle and High School. His academic journey continued at the University of Vermont, where he earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in History and Political Science.

## Professional Career
Before entering politics, Matt Walker had a diverse career in international logistics. He spent twenty years in this field and became a Licensed U.S. Customs Broker. His expertise in logistics and customs brokerage provided him with a solid foundation in understanding complex regulatory environments and international trade.

In addition to his career in logistics, Matt is an entrepreneur. He owns and operates the Vermont Clothing Company in St. Albans, Vermont, alongside his wife. The business is a family affair, with their three children also involved in its operations.

## Political Career
Matt Walker's political career began with his appointment to the Vermont House of Representatives in February 2022. He was appointed to fill the seat vacated by Representative Brian Savage in the Franklin-4 District. His appointment marked the beginning of his journey as a public servant, representing the interests of his constituents in Franklin County.

In the 2022 general election, Matt Walker successfully retained his seat, demonstrating the trust and confidence that the voters of his district placed in him. As a member of the Vermont House of Representatives, he serves on the House Committee on Transportation, where he contributes to shaping policies and legislation related to transportation infrastructure and services in Vermont.

## Personal Life
Matt Walker resides in Swanton, Vermont, with his family. His commitment to his community is evident not only in his political work but also in his business endeavors. He is dedicated to improving the lives of his constituents and ensuring that Vermont remains a great place to live and work.
