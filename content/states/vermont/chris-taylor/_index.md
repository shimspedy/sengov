## Early Life and Education
Christopher Taylor was born in Rutland, Vermont. He attended high school at Mount Saint Joseph Academy, where he developed a keen interest in public service and community involvement. Following his high school education, Chris pursued a Bachelor of Science degree in public relations and media communications from Champlain College.

## Professional Career
Chris Taylor has accumulated over 20 years of experience in business management, showcasing his leadership and organizational skills. His career in education spans seven years, during which he dedicated six years as a behavior interventionist at Milton Elementary School. Currently, he serves as the Behavior Services Coordinator at the same institution, where he continues to make a positive impact on students' lives.

## Political Career
Chris Taylor embarked on his political journey as a member of the Vermont House of Representatives, representing the Chittenden-Franklin District. He assumed office on January 4, 2023, and has been actively involved in legislative activities since then. As a Republican, Chris is committed to advocating for policies that benefit his constituents and the broader Vermont community.

## Personal Life
Chris resides in Milton, Vermont, with his wife and two children. His dedication to his family and community is evident in his professional and political endeavors. Chris is known for his approachable demeanor and his unwavering commitment to serving the people of Vermont.

## Legislative Work
In his role as a state representative, Chris Taylor serves on the House Committee on Education. He is also a member of the State Aid for School Construction Working Group. Through these positions, he works diligently to improve the educational infrastructure and resources available to Vermont's students.
