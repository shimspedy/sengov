David K. Durfee was born and raised in rural upstate New York. He developed a strong connection to his community and a passion for public service from an early age. David pursued higher education at Williams College, where he earned his undergraduate degree. He furthered his education by obtaining an MBA from Cornell University, equipping him with the skills and knowledge to excel in various professional roles.

David's professional journey has been diverse and impactful. He has worked as a consultant to cooperatives, non-profits, and small businesses, providing valuable insights and guidance to help these organizations thrive. One of his notable roles was serving as the General Manager of Wild Oats Market, a community-owned food cooperative with 1600 members. His leadership and dedication to sustainable practices helped the cooperative grow and succeed.

David's commitment to public service is evident in his extensive involvement in local governance and community organizations. He was elected to the Vermont House of Representatives in 2018, representing the Bennington-3 district. In this role, he has been an advocate for various issues, including healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability. David serves on the House Committee on Health Care and the Joint Carbon Emissions Reduction Committee, where he works tirelessly to address the needs and concerns of his constituents.

## Community Involvement
Beyond his legislative duties, David is actively engaged in his community. He serves on the boards of BROC Community Action and the Mount Anthony Union High School District. He was also a member of the former Shaftsbury School District board and the secretary of the Neighboring Food Cooperative Association board. His dedication to education and community development is evident through his ongoing involvement in these organizations.

David resides in Shaftsbury, Bennington County, with his wife Melanie. Together, they have three sons who are a source of pride and joy. David's family is an integral part of his life, and he values the support and encouragement they provide as he continues to serve his community and state.

Representative David Durfee's journey from rural upstate New York to the Vermont House of Representatives is a testament to his dedication, hard work, and commitment to public service. His diverse professional background, combined with his passion for community involvement, makes him a respected and effective leader. David continues to work towards a better future for his constituents, focusing on key issues that impact their daily lives and well-being.
