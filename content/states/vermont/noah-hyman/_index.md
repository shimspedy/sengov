NOAH HYMAN is a Democratic who serves as a State Representative in the Vermont House of Representatives, representing the new Chittenden 8 district, which covers parts of South Burlington and Williston Vermont.
Noah was born in Philadelphia and raised in Lafayette Hill, PA. Born into a family of jewelers he is proud to be a fourth-generation jeweler and goldsmith. After many years apprenticing with his grandfather in the family business, he eventually became the owner. Owning and operating a small business taught Noah about the importance of hard work and the positive impact small business owners can have on a community.
