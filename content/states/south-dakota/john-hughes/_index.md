John Hughes is a Republican member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 13. He assumed office on January 14, 2025, and his current term is set to end on January 12, 2027.

Born in Huron, South Dakota, John Hughes has deep roots in the state he now serves. He pursued higher education at South Dakota State University, where he earned his bachelor's degree, and later attended the University of Nebraska College of Law to obtain his law degree. His professional journey has been marked by his work as an attorney and business owner, showcasing his dedication to both his community and his career.

Hughes entered the political arena with a commitment to representing the interests of his constituents in District 13, which includes parts of Lincoln and Minnehaha counties. His election to the South Dakota House of Representatives in November 2024 marked the beginning of his legislative career. As a new member, he brings fresh perspectives and a strong desire to address the needs and concerns of his district.

In addition to his professional and political endeavors, Hughes is known for his active engagement with his community. Whether through his legal practice or his legislative work, he has consistently demonstrated a passion for service and a drive to make a positive impact. His leadership and dedication are qualities that resonate with the people he represents, making him a respected figure in South Dakota's political landscape.
