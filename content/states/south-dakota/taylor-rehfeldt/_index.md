Taylor Rae Rehfeldt is an American politician and registered nurse anesthetist. She was elected to the South Dakota House of Representatives in November 2020 and assumed office on January 11, 2021. Prior to entering politics, Rehfeldt worked as a nurse anesthetist.

Education:
- Bachelor of Science in Nursing from South Dakota State University
- Bachelor of Science in interdisciplinary science from the South Dakota School of Mines and Technology
- Master of Science and Doctor of Nursing Practice in nurse anesthesia from Mount Marty University

Rehfeldt is an experienced leader, compassionate nurse, and community advocate. She appreciates the beauty of South Dakota, from the nature of the Black Hills to vibrant downtown Sioux Falls and the plains.
