In 2013, he returned to Watertown and transitioned to the private sector, working for an insurance firm and establishing himself as a private practice attorney.

Currently, he serves as Vice President for an institutional investment advisory firm, showcasing his expertise in compliance and research analysis.

Roby is also committed to public service and community engagement. He serves on non-profit boards, contributes to professional associations, and advocates for conservative solutions to pressing issues. His principles emphasize limited government, local control, strong families, and healthy communities. As a fourth-generation Watertown resident, Roby is passionate about addressing challenges such as education, public safety, mental health, and economic development, striving to create a brighter future for his constituents.
