Rocky Blare is an American politician and insurance agent. He currently represents the 21st district in the South Dakota House of Representatives. Prior to this, he served in the same district in the South Dakota Senate from 2019 to 2021.

Blare resides outside Ideal, South Dakota, with his wife, who is a public school teacher. His political career has been marked by successful elections:

- In 2018, Blare was elected to the South Dakota Senate, defeating Julie Bartling.
- In 2020, he transitioned to the South Dakota House of Representatives, securing re-election alongside Caleb Finck.

Blare's commitment to public service and his background in insurance contribute to his effective representation of the 21st district. His dedication to his constituents and community remains unwavering.
