Phil Jensen, born on July 24, 1952, in Wichita, Kansas, is an American politician who has dedicated his career to public service. He currently serves as a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 33. Additionally, he held the position of a Republican member of the South Dakota Senate from 2013 to 2021.

Early Life and Career:
Phil Jensen's journey into politics began with a strong commitment to conservative principles. He firmly believes in limited government, individual freedom, and free-market solutions. His dedication to these values has shaped his legislative career.

Jensen's Background:
Phil Jensen moved to Pennington County, South Dakota, where he established himself as a respected community member. His passion for public service led him to seek elected office, and he has consistently championed conservative causes.

Legislative Accomplishments:
During his tenure in the South Dakota Legislature, Jensen worked tirelessly to promote policies aligned with his core beliefs. He advocated for lower taxes, responsible spending, and a business-friendly environment. His commitment to economic growth and individual liberty earned him the reputation of a true Reagan conservative.

Controversies and Disputes:
Jensen's outspoken views occasionally sparked controversy. Notably, he asserted that the free market, rather than government intervention, should determine whether racial discrimination is acceptable. His SB 128 bill, introduced in 2013, aimed to allow business owners to discriminate based on their constitutional rights to free association, free speech, and private property. Although the bill faced opposition, Jensen remained steadfast in his convictions.

Governor Dennis Daugaard publicly disagreed with Jensen's stance, emphasizing that it did not align with South Dakota values. Despite criticism, Jensen remained committed to his principles.

Oath Keepers Membership:
In 2022, it came to light that Jensen had been a member of the far-right militia group, the Oath Keepers. This revelation added another layer of complexity to his political career.

Conclusion:
Senator Phil Jensen's legacy is one of unwavering conservatism, principled leadership, and a commitment to the people of South Dakota. His impact on state politics remains significant, and his dedication to conservative ideals continues to shape the legislative landscape.
