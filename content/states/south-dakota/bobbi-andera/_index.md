Bobbi Andera is a member of the Republican Party serving in the South Dakota House of Representatives. She represents District 10 and assumed office on January 14, 2025. Her current term is set to conclude on January 12, 2027.

Bobbi Andera brings a wealth of experience and dedication to her role as a legislator. Before entering politics, she served in the U.S. Air Force, where she developed a strong sense of discipline and commitment to public service. Her professional background as a scientist further highlights her analytical skills and problem-solving abilities, which she applies to her work in the House of Representatives.

Throughout her career, Andera has been an advocate for fiscally conservative policies, emphasizing the importance of small government and the responsible use of taxpayer money. She is committed to ensuring fair representation for all constituents in District 10 and fostering bipartisan cooperation to achieve the best solutions for her community. Her approach to governance is rooted in upholding the state and U.S. constitutions, particularly the Bill of Rights, to mitigate government overreach.

In addition to her legislative duties, Andera is an entrepreneur and laboratory consultant. She has also founded a non-profit organization, showcasing her dedication to serving her community in various capacities. Her diverse experiences and unwavering commitment to public service make her a respected and effective representative for District 10.
