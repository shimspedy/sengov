Oren Lesmeister (Democratic Party) has been serving in the South Dakota House of Representatives since January 10, 2017. Born on July 7, 1966, in Eagle Butte, South Dakota, Lesmeister brings a wealth of experience to his role.

Background and Career:
Oren Lesmeister's professional journey spans various domains. He is a farmer, rancher, and ag business owner. His commitment to agriculture and rural communities has been a driving force throughout his career.

Political Involvement:
- Party Affiliation: Democratic
- District: 28A (which includes Corson, Dewey, Perkins, and Ziebach counties)
- Term: Incumbent (ends on January 14, 2025)
- Leadership Role: Lesmeister has served as the state House minority leader since January 10, 2023.

Elections:
- 2022 General Election: In the 2022 general election, Oren Lesmeister secured victory, defeating Ralph Lyon. He received 50.6% of the votes (1,890 votes), while Lyon received 49.4% (1,847 votes).
- 2020 General Election: In the 2020 general election, Lesmeister won with 100% of the votes (2,585 votes).

Education:
Oren Lesmeister attended Western Dakota Technical Institute.

Senator Lesmeister's dedication to public service and his deep roots in South Dakota make him a valuable representative for District 28A. His work continues to impact the lives of constituents and contribute to the betterment of the state.
