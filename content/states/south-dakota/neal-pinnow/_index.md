Neal Pinnow (Republican Party) is a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 28B. He assumed office on January 10, 2023, and his current term ends on January 14, 2025.

Biography:
Neal Pinnow earned a Bachelor's degree in secondary education and teaching from Dickinson State University in 1994. His career experience includes owning and operating Hausauer Seamless Products LLC. Additionally, he worked as a controller with Northwest Farm & Home Supply and served as a secondary teacher at McIntosh High School. Notably, Pinnow has also held the position of mayor in Lemmon, South Dakota.

Committee Assignments (2023-2024):
- House Commerce and Energy Committee
- House Retirement Laws Committee
- House Taxation Committee

Elections:
- In the 2022 general election, Neal Pinnow defeated Calvin Reilly to secure his seat in the South Dakota House of Representatives District 28B. He received 80.7% of the votes.
- During the Republican primary for the same district, Pinnow emerged victorious, defeating Thomas Brunner with 56.5% of the votes.

District 28B encompasses all of Butte and Harding Counties, as well as the northern one-third of Perkins County.

Occupation:
Owner/Operator of Hausauer Seamless Products LLC.
