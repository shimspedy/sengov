Bethany Soye is an American attorney and politician. Born in Chadron, Nebraska, she has a diverse educational background. She earned a Bachelor of Science degree in business administration from North Central University. Later, she pursued a joint Master of Public Policy and Juris Doctor from the College of William & Mary.

Soye's career path reflects her commitment to public service. She currently works as a financial compliance attorney at MetaBank. Prior to that, she gained valuable experience as a legal intern in the United States Department of Justice and served as a law clerk in the office of Senator Ted Cruz. Additionally, she worked as a judicial extern for the Supreme Court of Virginia and spent two years as a research assistant at the National Center for State Courts.

In November 2020, Bethany Soye was elected to the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing the 9th district. She officially assumed office on January 12, 2021. As a uniquely qualified Representative with both a J.D. and a Master of Public Policy, she brings a wealth of knowledge and dedication to her role. Her expertise in banking compliance further enhances her ability to serve her constituents effectively.

Bethany Soye is a Republican and a strong advocate for her community. Her commitment to public service and her legal background make her a valuable asset in the South Dakota State Legislature.
