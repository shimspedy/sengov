Dylan Jordan, a member of the Republican Party, serves in the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 4. He assumed office on January 14, 2025, and his current term is set to conclude on January 12, 2027.

Born and raised in Eastern South Dakota, Dylan Jordan grew up as the eldest of four siblings. His upbringing instilled in him the values that define South Dakota's spirit. He spent his formative years in the Watertown and Sioux Falls areas, eventually graduating from Parker and settling in Clear Lake, South Dakota. Dylan's professional journey has been diverse, including roles in manufacturing, delivery services, and even running a side business as a DJ entertainer. His multifaceted career reflects his adaptability and commitment to hard work.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Dylan is deeply involved in his community. He volunteers at his church, actively participates in local activism, and attends various community events. His dedication to public service is further exemplified by his role as Vice-Chair of the Deuel County Republicans. Dylan's passion for preserving the American spirit is evident in his advocacy for issues such as taxation reform, pro-life policies, and the protection of constitutional rights.

Dylan Jordan's political platform emphasizes the importance of low taxes, family values, and individual freedoms. He is a staunch supporter of the First and Second Amendments, advocating for freedom of speech and the right to bear arms. His commitment to property rights and state sovereignty underscores his belief in the principles of life, liberty, and property. Dylan also champions election integrity, medical freedom, and streamlined adoption processes, reflecting his dedication to addressing the concerns of his constituents.

As a legislator, Dylan Jordan strives to uphold the values that resonate with the people of District 4. His leadership and vision aim to ensure that South Dakota remains a state where the American spirit thrives. Through his work in the South Dakota House of Representatives, Dylan continues to advocate for policies that benefit his community and uphold the principles of freedom and democracy.
