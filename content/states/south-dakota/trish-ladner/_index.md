Trish Ladner is an American politician serving as a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives from the 30th district. Elected in November 2020, she assumed office on January 12, 2021.

Ladner, a mother, business owner, and community leader, embodies common sense values. As a Christian conservative Republican, she believes in limited government, low taxes, and less regulation. Her commitment to these principles has shaped her legislative work.

During her tenure, Ladner has actively participated in the legislative sessions. Notably, she was awarded for her work on HB1039, which was integrated into HB1325. Currently, she serves on the Ag & Natural Resources and the Commerce & Energy committees.

In June 2021, Ladner authored an op-ed in the Rapid City Journal, where she criticized critical race theory and pledged to support legislation banning its teaching in South Dakota.

Trish Ladner continues to represent her constituents with dedication and integrity, advocating for policies that align with her conservative values.
