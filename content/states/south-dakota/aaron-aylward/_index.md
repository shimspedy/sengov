Aaron Aylward is an American politician who has been representing the 6th district in the South Dakota House of Representatives since January 12, 2021. He was elected in November 2020.

Education:
- Aylward earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in exercise science from Dakota State University.
- He also holds a Master of Arts in kinesiology from the University of South Dakota.

Career:
- In 2012 and 2013, Aylward worked as a personal trainer.
- From 2013 to 2019, he served as a search consultant for Growing People and Companies (GPAC).
- In 2019, he transitioned to become a sales representative for Cintas, a business services firm.
- In 2018, Aylward ran as an unsuccessful candidate for the South Dakota House as a Libertarian.

Aaron Aylward is a dedicated public servant, committed to representing his constituents and contributing to the legislative process in South Dakota.
