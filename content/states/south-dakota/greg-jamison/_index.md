Greg Jamison is an American politician who has been serving as a Republican member for the 12th district in the South Dakota House of Representatives since January 12, 2021. His current term is set to end on January 14, 2025.

Career Experience:
Before entering politics, Greg Jamison worked as a real estate broker associate. His expertise in the real estate industry has informed his legislative work, particularly in areas related to local government and taxation.

Committee Assignments:
During the 2023-2024 legislative session, Jamison was assigned to the following committees:
- House Local Government Committee
- House Taxation Committee

In the previous 2021-2022 session, he held the following committee assignments:
- House Local Government Committee (Vice Chair)
- House Taxation Committee

Elections:
In the 2020 election, Greg Jamison secured his seat with 6,799 votes. His fellow Republican, Rep. Arch Beal, was also re-elected with 5,621 votes, and Erin Royer received 5,555 votes.

Upcoming Election:
Jamison is currently running for re-election in the South Dakota House of Representatives to represent District 12. He is on the ballot in the Republican primary scheduled for June 4, 2024.

General Election Candidates (District 12, 2 seats):
- JR Anderson (D)
- Erin Royer (D)

There are no incumbents in this race, and the general election will take place on November 5, 2024.
