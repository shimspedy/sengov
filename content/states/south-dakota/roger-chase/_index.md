Roger D. Chase is a dedicated public servant with a passion for improving the lives of South Dakotans. Born and raised in the heart of the state, he has deep roots in the community and a strong commitment to its well-being.

Early Life and Education:
Roger was born in South Dakota, where he attended local schools. His interest in public service blossomed during high school, where he actively participated in student government and community projects. After graduating, he majored in Political Science. His academic journey fueled his desire to make a positive impact on the state's policies and governance.

Career and Advocacy:
Upon completing his degree, Roger worked as an insurance broker, gaining insights into the challenges faced by small businesses and individuals seeking affordable coverage. His experiences led him to advocate for better healthcare access and insurance reforms. He also became involved in local agricultural initiatives, recognizing the vital role of farmers in South Dakota's economy.

Roger's commitment to community development and economic growth prompted him to run for public office. In 2016, he was elected to represent District 22, which includes Beadle, Clark, and Spink counties. As a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, Roger has championed various causes:

1. Affordable Housing: Roger co-chaired a summer study in 2021 that highlighted the urgent need for additional housing across the state. His dedication to solving this issue has been unwavering, as he believes that stable housing is essential for thriving communities.

2. Agriculture and Natural Resources: Given his background, Roger actively participates in committees related to agriculture and natural resources. He understands the importance of sustainable practices and rural development.

3. Transportation Infrastructure: Roger recognizes that well-maintained roads and bridges are crucial for economic growth. He advocates for strategic investments in infrastructure to connect communities and facilitate commerce.

Personal Life:
Outside of politics, Roger enjoys spending time with his family, exploring South Dakota's natural beauty, and volunteering at local events. His love for music and the arts often leads him to attend community concerts and support local artists.

Roger Chase remains committed to serving the people of South Dakota with integrity, empathy, and a forward-thinking approach. His tireless efforts continue to shape policies that enhance the quality of life for all residents.
