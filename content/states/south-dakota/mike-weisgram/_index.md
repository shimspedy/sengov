Mike Weisgram is a business owner and has been serving as a Republican member of the South Dakota House of Representatives since January 12, 2021. Representing District 24, he brings a wealth of experience to his role.

Career and Business Background:
Mike's career experience includes owning a business, where he has honed his entrepreneurial skills and contributed to the local economy. His commitment to business development and community growth has been a cornerstone of his legislative work.

Committee Assignments:
During his tenure, Mike has actively participated in legislative committees. In the 2023-2024 session, he served on the following committees:
- House Commerce and Energy Committee (Chair)
- House Health and Human Services Committee
- House Retirement Laws Committee (Vice Chair)

In the previous session (2021-2022), he was assigned to the following committees:
- House Commerce and Energy Committee
- House Local Government Committee

Elections and Re-Election:
Mike was elected in 2020 with 7,786 votes, alongside fellow Republican Will Mortenson (8,410 votes). Amanda Bachmann received 3,079 votes. His current term runs until January 14, 2025. In the 2024 election, he is running for re-election.

Endorsements and Future Outlook:
As an incumbent, Mike continues to work diligently for his constituents. His commitment to public service and community welfare remains unwavering. We look forward to witnessing his continued contributions to South Dakota's legislative landscape.
