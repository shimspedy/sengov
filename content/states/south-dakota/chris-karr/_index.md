Chris Karr is an American businessman and politician. He currently serves as a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing the 11th district. Karr assumed office on January 10, 2017.

Early Life and Education:
Karr hails from Tabor, South Dakota. He earned a Bachelor of Science degree in business economics from South Dakota State University and later completed his Master of Business Administration at the University of South Dakota.

Career:
Karr has a diverse professional background. He has operated a small business in Sioux Falls, South Dakota, demonstrating his entrepreneurial spirit. Additionally, Karr worked as a Certified Valuation Analyst, showcasing his expertise in financial matters.

As a member of the Republican Party, Karr plays a significant role in the South Dakota House of Representatives. He currently serves as one of the five Majority Whips in the state legislature.

In addition to his legislative duties, Karr is running for election to the South Dakota State Senate, aiming to represent District 11. His commitment to public service and business acumen make him a valuable asset to the community.
