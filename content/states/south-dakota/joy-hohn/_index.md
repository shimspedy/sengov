Joy Hohn, a member of the Republican Party, serves in the South Dakota State Senate representing District 9. She assumed office on January 14, 2025, and her current term is set to conclude on January 12, 2027.

Born and raised in Sioux Falls, South Dakota, Joy Hohn has deep roots in her community. Her career as a commercial pilot has spanned decades, showcasing her dedication and skill in aviation. She earned her bachelor's degree from Augustana University in 1989, which laid the foundation for her professional journey. Beyond her career, Hohn is a devoted wife, mother, and family farmer. She and her family have been actively involved in farming, raising crops and livestock, and contributing to the agricultural backbone of their region.

Hohn's commitment to public service is evident in her advocacy for property rights and local governance. Her grassroots efforts have brought her closer to the people of Minnehaha County, where she has worked tirelessly to preserve constitutional rights and promote community welfare. Her leadership skills, honed through years of experience in both aviation and farming, have made her a trusted voice in her district.

As a state senator, Joy Hohn focuses on protecting constitutional rights, supporting families and education, and promoting fiscal responsibility. Her dedication to her constituents is unwavering, and she strives to ensure that their voices are heard in the legislative process. Hohn's values of integrity, trust, and tenacity guide her work, making her a respected figure in South Dakota's political landscape.
