Mike Walsh, a member of the Republican Party, assumed office on February 15, 2024, representing District 35 in the South Dakota State Senate. His current term will end on January 14, 2025.

Career Experience:
Mike Walsh has a background in law enforcement. His dedication to public service and community well-being is evident through his work in this field.

Appointment and Election:
Walsh was appointed to the vacant District 35 Senate seat by Governor Kristi Noem earlier this year. His appointment followed the departure of former Senator Jessica Castleberry (R). Walsh's commitment to serving his constituents is further demonstrated by his decision to run for re-election in the upcoming Republican primary on June 4, 2024.

Community Involvement:
Beyond his legislative duties, Mike Walsh actively contributes to his community. As a Marine veteran, he brings a wealth of experience and leadership to his role. Additionally, he volunteers as a Key Spouse at Ellsworth Air Force Base and serves as a volunteer boxing coach on the base.

Senator Walsh's dedication to public safety, community engagement, and effective governance makes him a valuable representative for District 35.
