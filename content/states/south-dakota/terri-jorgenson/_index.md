Terri Jorgenson is a Republican member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 29. She assumed office on January 14, 2025, and her current term is set to end on January 12, 2027.

Terri Jorgenson has a rich background in public service and community involvement. Before her tenure in the South Dakota House of Representatives, she worked as a public affairs professional, leveraging her expertise to address community concerns and advocate for meaningful change. Her career also includes significant experience in military operations, where she served with dedication and honor. This military background has equipped her with a strong sense of discipline and a commitment to upholding the values of her constituents.

A proud South Dakotan, Jorgenson has been actively involved in the Republican Party at both state and local levels. She has served as a Meade County State Committeewoman and held leadership roles such as Vice Chair of the Republican Party in Meade County and Vice President of the Meade County Republican Women. Her active participation in these roles highlights her dedication to conservative values and her commitment to representing the interests of her community.

Jorgenson's campaign for the South Dakota House of Representatives was driven by her passion for protecting liberties, preserving property rights, and promoting economic development. She has consistently emphasized the importance of upholding constitutional principles and safeguarding personal freedoms. Her legislative priorities reflect her deep-rooted belief in a government that serves the people and protects their rights.

Terri Jorgenson resides in South Dakota with her husband, Dennis, on an acreage outside of Piedmont. Married for over 37 years, the couple has two grown children. Their daughter is a devoted mother, while their son continues the family tradition of service by serving in the Air Force. Jorgenson's personal life is a testament to her values of family, community, and service.

As a legislator, Terri Jorgenson is committed to earning the trust of her constituents through her actions and dedication. She strives to make a positive impact on her district and the state of South Dakota, ensuring that the voices of her community are heard and represented.
