Tim Reisch (Republican Party) is a dedicated public servant with a rich history of service to the state of South Dakota. His career spans various roles, including military service, government administration, and leadership positions. Let's delve into the details:

1. Military Service:
   - Tim Reisch served in the South Dakota National Guard for over 40 years.
   - His commitment culminated in his role as Adjutant General from 2011 to 2019, serving under Governors Dennis Daugaard and Kristi Noem.
   - Reisch's dedication to the National Guard reflects his unwavering commitment to our country.

2. Government Administration:
   - Reisch's public service extends beyond the military. He served in Governor Mike Rounds' cabinet as Secretary of Corrections from 2003 to 2011.
   - His leadership in corrections administration demonstrates his ability to manage complex systems and address critical issues.

3. Sheriff and Deputy Secretary:
   - At just 24 years old, Reisch was elected Sheriff of Miner County.
   - He went on to serve five consecutive 4-year terms in this role.
   - Then-Governor Bill Janklow recognized Reisch's capabilities and appointed him Deputy Secretary of the South Dakota Department of Corrections.
   - Reisch's experience in law enforcement and corrections administration uniquely positions him to address criminal justice matters.

4. Educational Background:
   - Reisch holds a Bachelor of Science (B.S.) degree in public administration from Upper Iowa University.
   - He furthered his education with a Master of Science (M.S.) in administrative studies from the University of South Dakota.
   - Additionally, he earned an M.S. in strategic studies from the U.S. Army War College in 2005.
   - His academic achievements underscore his commitment to continuous learning and professional growth.

5. Legislative Role:
   - Reisch assumed office as a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 8, on January 10, 2023.
   - His current term runs until January 14, 2025.
   - As a legislator, he serves on several committees, including the House Education Committee, House Government Operations and Audit Committee, House Judiciary Committee, and as Chair of the House Military and Veterans' Affairs Committee.
   - Reisch's legislative work reflects his dedication to improving the lives of South Dakotans.

6. Upcoming Election:
   - In the 2024 election cycle, Reisch is running for re-election to the South Dakota House of Representatives.
   - He is on the ballot in the Republican primary scheduled for June 4, 2024.
   - Voters have the opportunity to recognize his contributions and support his continued service.

Tim Reisch's multifaceted background, from military leadership to government administration, positions him as a valuable advocate for his constituents. His commitment to public service remains unwavering, and he continues to make a positive impact on South Dakota's legislative landscape.
