Lance R. Koth is an American politician who has dedicated his career to public service. Born and raised in South Dakota, Koth has a deep-rooted commitment to his community and its residents.

Entry into Politics:
Koth's passion for public service led him to enter local politics. In 2018, he ran for election to one of two seats representing District 20 in the South Dakota House of Representatives. His campaign focused on fiscal responsibility, economic growth, and improving education. Koth's genuine connection with voters resonated, and he secured a seat in the state legislature.

Legislative Accomplishments:
Since assuming office in 2019, Koth has been a tireless advocate for his constituents. He serves on several committees, including the House Appropriations Committee, where he plays a crucial role in shaping the state budget. Koth's commitment to responsible spending and efficient resource allocation has earned him bipartisan respect.

Community Engagement:
Beyond legislative duties, Koth actively engages with his community. He attends town hall meetings, listens to constituents' concerns, and collaborates with local leaders to address pressing issues. Whether it's advocating for infrastructure improvements, supporting small businesses, or championing education reforms, Koth remains dedicated to South Dakota's well-being.

Re-Election and Future Goals:
In 2022, Koth faced a competitive Republican primary but emerged victorious, securing his party's nomination for re-election. His campaign emphasized transparency, accessibility, and a commitment to conservative values. In the general election, Koth's track record and community involvement resonated with voters, leading to his successful re-election.

Personal Life:
Outside of politics, Koth enjoys spending time with his family, exploring South Dakota's natural beauty, and volunteering for local charities. His down-to-earth demeanor and approachability make him a beloved figure in his district.
