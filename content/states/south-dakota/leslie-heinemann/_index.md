Leslie J. Heinemann is a Republican member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 25. He assumed office on January 14, 2025, and his current term is set to end on January 12, 2027.

Born on January 5, 1955, in Flandreau, South Dakota, Leslie Heinemann has dedicated much of his life to serving his community. Heinemann earned a bachelor's degree from Augustana College and later pursued a medical degree from Loyola University. His professional background as a dentist has allowed him to connect with people on a personal level, understanding their needs and concerns.

Heinemann's political career began in 2013 when he was elected to represent District 8 in the South Dakota House of Representatives. Over the years, he has been actively involved in legislative committees, including Health and Human Services and Local Government. His focus has often been on improving healthcare access, supporting local businesses, and ensuring the well-being of his constituents.

In 2024, Heinemann successfully ran for election to represent District 25, continuing his commitment to public service. Outside of his legislative duties, he operates a dental practice in Dell Rapids, South Dakota, and is known for his dedication to his family and community. His leadership and experience make him a respected figure in South Dakota politics.
