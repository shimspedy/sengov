Curt Massie, a Republican, assumed office on January 10, 2023, representing District 33 in the South Dakota House of Representatives. His current term extends until January 14, 2025.

Background and Education:
Curt Massie earned a Bachelor of Science degree in business administration and management from Black Hills State University in 1992. Born and raised in South Dakota on a farm/ranch west of Belle Fourche, he has a direct connection and understanding of the agricultural community and economy.

Career Experience:
Massie's career spans various roles:
- Managing Member: Dakota Business Tax, LLC
- Senior Auditor: South Dakota Department of Revenue
- State and Local Tax Consultant

Committee Assignments (2023-2024):
- House Judiciary Committee
- House Taxation Committee

Elections:
In the 2022 general election, Curt Massie secured a seat in the South Dakota House of Representatives. He is currently running for re-election in the 2024 general election alongside incumbent Phil Jensen.
