Tesa Schwans is a member of the Republican Party serving in the South Dakota House of Representatives. She represents District 9 and assumed office on January 14, 2025. Her current term is set to end on January 12, 2027.

Born in McLaughlin, South Dakota, Tesa Schwans has deep roots in her community. She graduated from Spearfish High School and later pursued her education at Black Hills Beauty College, earning her degree in 1992. Her professional journey led her to become a barber, showcasing her dedication to her craft and her entrepreneurial spirit.

Schwans entered the political arena with a commitment to representing her constituents and addressing the issues that matter most to them. Her campaign emphasized grassroots engagement and a focus on practical solutions for her district. She successfully ran for election to the South Dakota House of Representatives in November 2024, securing her position alongside incumbent Bethany Soye.

As a legislator, Schwans is passionate about several key areas, including reforming the Cosmetology Commission to enhance public safety and health standards. She is also deeply invested in addressing mental health issues, advocating for early intervention strategies that can positively impact families and communities.

In addition to her legislative duties, Schwans is actively involved in her local community, contributing to initiatives that support small businesses and foster economic growth. Her dedication to public service and her ability to connect with people from all walks of life make her a respected figure in South Dakota politics.
