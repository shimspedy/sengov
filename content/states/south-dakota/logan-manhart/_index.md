Born in Minneapolis, Minnesota, Logan Manhart has established himself as a dedicated public servant with a focus on representing the interests of his constituents in District 1. His journey into politics began with a strong commitment to community engagement and addressing the needs of the people he serves. Before his tenure in the South Dakota House of Representatives, Manhart actively participated in local initiatives and demonstrated a keen interest in legislative processes.

Manhart's election to the South Dakota House of Representatives in 2024 marked a significant milestone in his career. He ran a campaign centered on transparency, accountability, and fostering economic growth within his district. His efforts resonated with voters, leading to his successful election alongside fellow Republican Christopher Reder. Together, they have worked to uphold conservative values while addressing pressing issues such as infrastructure development, education reform, and rural community support.

His professional experience has informed his approach to crafting policies that aim to improve the quality of life for South Dakota residents.

Manhart's dedication to his role as a legislator is evident in his active participation in committees and his commitment to staying connected with his constituents. He values open communication and regularly engages with community members to understand their concerns and priorities. Through his work in the South Dakota House of Representatives, Logan Manhart continues to advocate for policies that promote growth, stability, and prosperity for District 1 and the state as a whole.
