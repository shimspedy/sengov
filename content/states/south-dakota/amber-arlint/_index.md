Amber Arlint (Republican Party) is a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 12. She assumed office on January 10, 2023, and her current term ends on January 14, 2025. Arlint is running for re-election to the South Dakota House of Representatives in the Republican primary on June 4, 2024.

Biography:
Amber Arlint earned a B.S. in political science, an M.A. in adult and higher education, and an Ed.D. in adult and higher education from the University of South Dakota in 2009, 2014, and 2018, respectively. Her career experience includes owning a State Farm Insurance Agency and working as a distributor sales representative with Western Refining. Additionally, she served as the director of sorority/fraternity life and leadership at the University of South Dakota.

Committee Assignments (2023-2024):
- House Commerce and Energy Committee
- House Education Committee

Elections 2024:
In the upcoming general election for South Dakota House of Representatives District 12 (2 seats), JR Anderson and Erin Royer are running alongside Amber Arlint. There are no incumbents in this race.
