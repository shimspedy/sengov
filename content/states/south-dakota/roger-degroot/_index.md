Roger DeGroot is an American politician who has dedicated his career to public service and education. Born and raised in South Dakota, he has been a tireless advocate for his constituents and a champion for the people.

Early Career and Education:
Roger began his journey in education as a teacher in 1975. His passion for teaching and commitment to shaping young minds led him to serve as the superintendent of schools in both Lennox and Brookings. During his tenure, he not only built educational institutions but also provided avenues for aspiring educators to earn advanced degrees.

Legislative Service:
In 2022, Roger DeGroot won election to serve as the District 7 State Representative. Since assuming office on January 10, 2023, he has continued to demonstrate his dedication to the people of South Dakota. As a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, Roger serves alongside Mellissa Heermann, working tirelessly to address the needs of their district.

Fiscal Responsibility and Education Advocacy:
Roger's experience as an educator and school board member has been invaluable during his time in the Legislature. He has served on important committees related to education and taxation, drawing upon his expertise to make informed decisions. His commitment to maintaining a fiscally responsible state government ensures that South Dakota's resources are well-managed.

Community Stewardship:
Beyond his legislative duties, Roger DeGroot remains deeply connected to his community. His leadership extends beyond the political arena, and he continues to be a trusted steward of South Dakota's valuable resources. Whether it's advocating for agriculture, supporting young people, or championing fiscal responsibility, Roger's impact is felt far and wide.

Roger DeGroot's legacy is one of service, integrity, and dedication. As he continues to serve District 7, we can count on him to be a strong voice for the people of South Dakota.
