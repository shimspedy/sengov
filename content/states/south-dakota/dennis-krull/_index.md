Dennis Krull, a Republican, serves as a member of the South Dakota House of Representatives, representing District 30. He assumed office on January 10, 2023, and his current term extends until January 14, 2025.

Background:
- Krull and his wife Kim owned and operated Krull's Market in Hill City since 1992.
- They also managed stores in Armour and Wall, SD.
- Krull believes in community support and has been an active member of the Hill City Volunteer Fire Department for over 25 years.

Political Career:
- Elected to the South Dakota House of Representatives in the 2022 general election.
- Serves on the House Appropriations Committee.
- Committed to representing the interests of Custer, Fall River, and Pennington counties.

Krull's dedication to public service and community involvement underscores his commitment to South Dakota. As a legislator, he aims to address the needs of his constituents and contribute to the betterment of the state.
