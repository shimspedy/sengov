Sydney Davis, a fourth-generation cattle producer, has dedicated her life to serving her rural community. Her commitment to helping others led her to pursue a career in healthcare. As a Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetist (CRNA), she has served communities in the southeastern corner of South Dakota and the tri-state area.

Education:
- Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) from South Dakota State University
- Master of Science (MSN) from Mount Marty College

Career:
- Nurse: Sydney has worked as a nurse, providing compassionate care to patients.
- Nurse Anesthetist: Her expertise as a CRNA has been invaluable in ensuring safe and effective anesthesia administration during medical procedures.
- Cattle Producer: Sydney's roots in agriculture run deep. She actively manages her family's cattle operation.

Political Journey:
- Elected to the South Dakota House of Representatives in November 2020, Sydney assumed office on January 12, 2021, succeeding Ray Ring.
- On January 4, 2022, she announced her candidacy for the District 17 State Senate seat, demonstrating her commitment to public service and community welfare.

Legislative Priorities:
During the last legislative session, Sydney supported grant programs aimed at providing affordable housing in South Dakota communities. Her intentions are solely to be a thoughtful legislator, considering all sides and working to be a problem solver for the betterment of District 17 constituents and the state.

Sydney Davis exemplifies dedication, compassion, and a strong work ethic. Her journey from nursing to politics reflects her unwavering commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of South Dakotans.
