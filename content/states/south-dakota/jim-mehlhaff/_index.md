Jim Mehlhaff (Republican Party) is a dedicated public servant who assumed office on January 10, 2023, as a member of the South Dakota State Senate. Representing District 24, he has been actively involved in shaping legislation and advocating for his constituents.

Background and Career:
Jim Mehlhaff brings a wealth of experience to his role. Prior to serving in the State Senate, he owned a business and worked in state government. His commitment to public service extends beyond legislative duties, as he previously served on the St. Joseph School Board.

Committee Assignments:
During his first term in office, Senator Mehlhaff has been an active participant in various committees:
- Agriculture and Natural Resources: Contributing to discussions on matters related to agriculture, environmental conservation, and natural resources.
- Judiciary: Engaging in legal and constitutional matters, ensuring fair and just policies.
- Local Government: As Vice-Chair, he plays a crucial role in local governance issues.
- Retirement Laws: Also serving as Vice-Chair, Senator Mehlhaff focuses on retirement-related legislation.

Election and Re-election:
In the 2022 general election, Jim Mehlhaff secured victory for the South Dakota State Senate District 24 seat. His dedication to public service continues as he seeks re-election in the upcoming 2024 election.

Senator Mehlhaff's commitment to his constituents, combined with his diverse background, positions him as an influential voice in South Dakota's legislative landscape. As he continues to serve, his impact on policy-making and community development remains significant.
