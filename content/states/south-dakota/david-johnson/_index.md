David Johnson is an American politician and a dedicated public servant. He currently represents the 33rd district in the South Dakota Senate. Prior to his election to the State Senate, Johnson served two terms in the South Dakota House of Representatives, where he was one of the two representatives from the same district.

Background and Education:
David Johnson earned a Bachelor of Science degree from the South Dakota School of Mines and Technology. His commitment to education continued as he pursued a Master of Business Administration from the University of South Dakota.

Professional Career:
Before entering politics, Johnson established himself as a successful entrepreneur. He is the owner of Johnson Tree Company, a business he has managed since 1980. His expertise in arboriculture and his dedication to environmental conservation have made him a respected figure in his community.

Military Service:
In addition to his civilian pursuits, Johnson served as an officer in the United States Air Force (USAF). His military background instilled in him a strong sense of duty and discipline, qualities that continue to guide his public service.

Community Involvement:
Beyond his legislative responsibilities, Senator Johnson actively engages with his constituents. He is a member of the National Rifle Association (NRA) and serves as the President of the Rapid City Right to Life (RCRTL) organization. His commitment to protecting individual rights and advocating for pro-life causes reflects his unwavering principles.

Election History:
- In November 2020, Johnson secured victory in the South Dakota Senate race, receiving 11,369 votes and defeating his opponent, Ryan A. Ryder.
- During the June 2020 primary, Johnson emerged as the clear choice, garnering 2,264 votes and surpassing Janet Jensen, wife of legislator Phil Jensen.
- In previous years, Johnson's re-election to the South Dakota House of Representatives demonstrated consistent support from voters. Notably, he received 6,094 votes in November 2018 and 8,245 votes in November 2016.

Senator David Johnson's dedication to public service, combined with his diverse background, makes him a valuable asset to South Dakota's legislative body. His commitment to representing the interests of his constituents remains unwavering, and he continues to work tirelessly to improve the lives of those he serves.
