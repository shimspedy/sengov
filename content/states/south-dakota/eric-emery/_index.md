Emery's commitment to public service extends beyond legislative duties. He also serves as the emergency medical services director for the Rosebud Indian Reservation, where his expertise in healthcare and emergency response has a direct impact on the community's well-being.

Throughout his career, Emery has demonstrated unwavering dedication to improving healthcare access, advocating for marginalized communities, and ensuring that essential services reach those in need.
