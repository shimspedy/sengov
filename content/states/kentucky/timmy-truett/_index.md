Born: December 1, 1977
Political Party: Republican

Biography

Timmy Truett is an American politician and educator serving as a member of the Kentucky House of Representatives from the 89th district. He assumed office on November 23, 2021.

Before his political career, Truett worked as a pastor, coach, and educator. He also owns a pumpkin patch. He was the principal of McKee Elementary School in McKee, Kentucky.

Truett was elected to the Kentucky House of Representatives in a November 2021 special election. His current term ends on January 1, 2025. He is running for re-election to the Kentucky House of Representatives to represent District 89.

Personal Life

Timmy Truett lives in McKee, Kentucky. He earned a master's degree in education. His career experience includes working as the principal of McKee Elementary School.
