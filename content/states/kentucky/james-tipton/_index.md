James A. Tipton is an American politician from Kentucky. He is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 53 in the Kentucky House of Representatives. He was first elected to the house in 2014 when incumbent representative Bart Rowland was redistricted to the 21st district.

James Tipton was born in Taylorsville, Kentucky. He graduated from Taylorsville High School in 1977. Tipton earned a Bachelor of Science in Agriculture in 1981 and a Master's degree in Vocational Education in 1985 from the University of Kentucky.
