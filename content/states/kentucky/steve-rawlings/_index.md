Steve Rawlings is an American attorney and politician who is currently serving as a member of the Kentucky House of Representatives from the 66th District. He was elected in November 2022 and assumed office on January 1, 2023. His current term ends on January 1, 2025. His district comprises the northern part of Boone County.

Rawlings earned a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Cincinnati in 1980, and he earned a Juris Doctor from Northern Kentucky University in 1989. Before his political career, Rawlings worked as an attorney. In 2022, he ran against incumbent representative C. Ed Massey. Rawlings defeated Massey in the primary by a significant margin. He went on to face Tim Montgomery on November 8, 2022. Rawlings defeated Montgomery by a large margin, and he assumed office on January 1, 2023.

In addition to his political career, Rawlings' experience includes owning My Father's Design and working as an attorney, author, and craftsman. He has served on the board of directors of the Merit Theatre Company. He is also a member of the Judiciary Committee.

Rawlings is running for the Kentucky Senate in 2024. He is on the ballot in the Republican primary on May 21, 2024.
