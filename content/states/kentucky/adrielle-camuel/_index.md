Adrielle Camuel is a member of the Democratic Party and represents District 93 in the Kentucky House of Representatives. She assumed office on November 29, 2023, and her current term ends on January 1, 2025. She is running for re-election to the Kentucky House of Representatives to represent District 93 and is on the ballot in the Democratic primary on May 21, 2024.

Adrielle was born in and lives in Kentucky. She earned a Bachelor's degree in Political Science and a Master's degree in Political Science from the University of Louisville. Her career experience includes working as a small business employee and as an administrative professional at Fayette County Public Schools.

In the special general election for Kentucky House of Representatives District 93 held on November 7, 2023, Adrielle Camuel defeated Kyle Whalen. She secured 57.6% of the votes, amounting to 7,924 votes.

In the upcoming Democratic primary for Kentucky House of Representatives District 93 scheduled on May 21, 2024, incumbent Adrielle Camuel and Sarah Ritter are running.

Adrielle Camuel's work extends beyond her role as a representative. She is also a Support Professional at Fayette County Public Schools. She is a lifetime member of Hadassah, a Jewish women's organization. Her educational background and work experience have equipped her with the skills and knowledge necessary to serve her constituents effectively.
