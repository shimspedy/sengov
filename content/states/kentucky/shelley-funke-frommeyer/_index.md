Shelley Funke Frommeyer, born on June 21, 1969, is an American politician from Kentucky. She is a member of the Republican Party and has represented District 24 in the Kentucky Senate since January 1, 2023.

Frommeyer is a certified financial planner and resides in Alexandria, Kentucky. She has had a diverse career, including founding FFR Wealth Team and serving as its CEO, working as a financial advisor with Waddell & Reed, and being an executive recruiter with Johnson ReSource Group/Professions, Inc.

She earned a bachelor's degree in business administration from the University of Kentucky in 1991. Her commitment to her community is evident in her involvement with various organizations. She is a USO Volunteer and has been involved with St Mary Catholic Church and School as an Extraordinary Minister, Finance Committee member, Morning Mom, and Volleyball Coach.

She is also a member of several caucuses including the Northern Kentucky Caucus, Aerospace/Aviation Caucus, Kentucky History Caucus, Kentucky Rural Electric Cooperatives Caucus, Kentucky Sportsmen's Caucus, Kentucky-Israel Caucus, and Pro-Life Caucus.
