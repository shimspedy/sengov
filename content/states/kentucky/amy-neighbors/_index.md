Amy Neighbors (born November 12, 1975) is an American politician who has served as a member of the Kentucky House of Representatives since January 2023. She represents Kentucky's 21st House district. She is a retired Nursing home administrator. She is the first woman to ever represent Metcalfe County in the house.

Amy Neighbors was born on November 12, 1975. She earned a Bachelor of Science in health care administration from Western Kentucky University in 1999. After her graduation, she worked as a Nursing Home Administrator. She is now retired from her profession.

In addition to her professional career, Neighbors has been actively involved in community service. She has served on the board of Life Skills Inc and has been affiliated with Leadership Kentucky. Her dedication to service and leadership has been recognized by her community and peers.

Neighbors began her political career when she was elected to the Kentucky House of Representatives on November 8, 2022. She assumed office on January 1, 2023. She represents Kentucky's 21st House district. Her current term ends on January 1, 2025.

Neighbors is a member of the Republican Party. She is running for re-election to the Kentucky House of Representatives to represent District 21. The Republican primary for this office on May 21, 2024, was canceled.

Neighbors' career experience includes working as an administrator with Metcalfe Health Care Center. She has served on the board of Life Skills Inc. and has been affiliated with Leadership Kentucky.

Neighbors was assigned to the following committees: House Agriculture Committee, Health and Family Services Committee, House Transportation Committee, and Transportation Committee.

In the upcoming general election for Kentucky House of Representatives District 21 on November 5, 2024, incumbent Amy Neighbors and Jeffery Humble are running.
