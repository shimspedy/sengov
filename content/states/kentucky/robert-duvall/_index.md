Robert Duvall is an American politician who has been serving as a member of the Kentucky House of Representatives since January 2023. He represents Kentucky's 17th House district. In addition to his political career, he also works part-time at Western Kentucky University.

Electoral History:
Robert Duvall was elected on November 8, 2022, in the 2022 Kentucky House of Representatives election unopposed. He assumed office in January 2023.

Political Positions:
Duvall is known for his conservative stance. He is pro-life, opposes vaccine mandates, and is against Sanctuary immigration policy.

Education and Career:
Robert Duvall earned a doctorate in optometry from the University of Alabama at Birmingham in 1995. He has been practicing optometry in Bowling Green for over 26 years. His team has been recognized as the Best Eye Doctors by the Bowling Green Daily News in 2020 and again in 2021. He owns Precision Eye Care & Optical and Drs. Duvall, Tade, & Associates and has worked as an optometrist with Doctor's ValuVision.

Personal Life:
Robert Duvall is married to Amy Duvall and they have three children named Alex, Will, and Grayson. They reside in Bowling Green, Kentucky. He is a Baptist. His father served in World War II.

Future Plans:
Robert Duvall is running for re-election to the Kentucky House of Representatives to represent District 17. The Republican primary for this office on May 21, 2024, was canceled. He will be running in the general election for Kentucky House of Representatives District 17 on November 5, 2024.
