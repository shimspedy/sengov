Candy Massaroni is an American politician who has served as a member of the Kentucky House of Representatives since January 2023. She represents Kentucky's 50th House district. Massaroni was first elected in 2022, defeating incumbent Republican Chad McCoy for renomination.

She was elected on November 8, 2022, in the 2022 Kentucky House of Representatives election. She assumed office on January 1, 2023. Massaroni is a Christian and a United States Air Force veteran.

Massaroni lives in Bardstown, Kentucky. She is a member of the Republican Party and her current term ends on January 1, 2025. Massaroni is running for re-election to the Kentucky House of Representatives to represent District 50. She is on the ballot in the Republican primary.

In terms of her legislative work, Massaroni has been assigned to several committees including the House Education Committee, Tourism and Outdoor Recreation Committee, House Veterans, Military Affairs, and Public Protection Committee, Education Committee, Small Business & Information Technology Committee, and Veterans, Military Affairs and Public Protection Committee.

Massaroni's work in the Kentucky House of Representatives has been marked by her commitment to her constituents and her dedication to public service. Her tenure in office has seen her sponsor numerous bills and take on key roles in various committees. Her work reflects her deep understanding of the issues facing her district and her determination to make a positive impact.
