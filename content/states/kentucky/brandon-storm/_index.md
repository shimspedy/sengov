Brandon Jackson Storm, born on May 14, 1976, is an American attorney and a prominent political figure serving as a member of the Kentucky Senate from the 21st district. He is affiliated with the Republican Party.

Storm is a native of Laurel County, Kentucky. He pursued his higher education at Eastern Kentucky University, where he earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Police Administration in 2000. Furthering his academic pursuits, he obtained a Juris Doctor from the Mississippi College School of Law in 2003.

Before embarking on his political journey, Storm worked in independent law firms. In 2009, he founded his own law firm, further establishing his career in the legal field. His professional experience includes owning the Brandon J. Storm law firm.

Storm's political career began when he was elected to the Kentucky Senate in November 2020. He assumed office on January 1, 2021. His current term is set to end on January 1, 2025. He is also running for re-election to the Kentucky State Senate to represent District 21.

In addition to his role as a senator, Storm holds significant positions in various committees. He serves as the vice chair of the Senate Transportation Committee and the chair of the Senate Enrollment Committee. His committee assignments for 2023-2024 include the Senate Banking and Insurance Committee, Economic Development, Tourism and Labor Committee, Senate Judiciary Committee, State and Local Government Committee, and Senate Transportation Committee.

Storm's dedication to his work and his significant contributions to his district and the state of Kentucky are commendable. His journey from being an attorney to a respected member of the Kentucky Senate is a testament to his hard work and commitment to public service.
