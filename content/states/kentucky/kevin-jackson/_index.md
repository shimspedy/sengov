Kevin Jackson is an American politician from Kentucky. He is a member of the Republican Party and represents District 20 in the state house. Jackson represents parts of Bowling Green and is a retired teacher and coach.

He assumed office on January 1, 2023, succeeding Patti Minter. His current term ends on January 1, 2025. Jackson is running for re-election to the Kentucky House of Representatives to represent District 20. The Republican primary for this office on May 21, 2024, was canceled.

In terms of education, Kevin Jackson earned a Bachelor of Science (B.S.) and a Master of Arts (M.A.) in educational leadership and administration from Western Kentucky University.

Before his political career, Jackson worked as a financial advisor with Studle Financial Services. He also had a career as an educator.

In addition to his political and professional roles, Jackson has also been a coach. His diverse background and experience in education and finance have undoubtedly contributed to his role as a representative.
